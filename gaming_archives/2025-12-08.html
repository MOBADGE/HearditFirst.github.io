December 08, 2025

The fan-made Team Fortress 2 Classic project has been renamed Team Fortress 2 Classified, with its release postponed to January 30, 2026. The developers referenced Valve's reputation for delayed releases, stating the extra time is necessary to deliver an authentic TF2 experience. This delay means fans seeking a fresh take on the classic shooter will need to wait longer, but it reflects the team’s commitment to quality and faithfulness to the original game’s spirit. The project remains a notable example of ongoing community efforts to expand and preserve older multiplayer games.

Collectibles publisher iam8bit has filed a lawsuit against Skybound Entertainment, the publisher behind the cat simulation game Stray, alleging fraud related to the production and distribution of Stray-themed gaming collectibles. The dispute centers on contractual and financial disagreements, highlighting challenges in partnerships between game publishers and third-party merchandise creators. This legal conflict draws attention to the complexities of licensing and managing physical goods tied to digital games, an area increasingly relevant as collectible markets continue to grow within gaming communities.

Northgard Updated to Definitive Edition with DLC Included

The Viking-themed real-time strategy game Northgard has been reissued as Northgard: Definitive Edition. This update bundles all major downloadable content into the base game and introduces additional free content for existing players. Alongside the relaunch, the game is discounted by 60% until December 18. The consolidation offers a convenient entry point for new players and a value boost for current fans by simplifying access to the full range of Northgard’s features without separate DLC purchases.

Descenders Next Launches Multiplayer Update After Delays

The downhill biking game Descenders Next has received a significant multiplayer update, following an extended wait. The developer apologized for the slow progress and emphasized ongoing work to recapture the original Descenders’ feel and gameplay flow. This update adds online play functionality to Descenders Next, addressing core community requests and aiming to improve longevity through player interaction. The patch marks a step forward for the title, which had faced criticism for delays since its release.

World War I-themed survival horror game Conscript has been enhanced with a major update that introduces a hardcore mode, redesigned levels, new items, and gear. The developer described this as closer to the version they originally wanted to release, signaling improvements based on extended playtesting and player feedback. These changes aim to deepen the game’s challenge and atmosphere, possibly appealing more to hardcore enthusiasts of historical horror experiences. The update may extend the game’s appeal and improve its reputation within the niche survival horror community.

The creator of the animated short The Backwater Gospel announced work on a new first-person shooter inspired by Half-Life and narrative-driven shooters from the genre’s earlier days. This project seeks to revive elements of storytelling and gameplay style characteristic of golden-age FPS titles. While details remain limited, the announcement is noteworthy for fans of cinematic and story-centric shooters, suggesting a niche but dedicated design vision emerging in the indie space.
