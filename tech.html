Pentagon Endorses Anduril Founder Palmer Luckey Amid Military Tech Push

Palmer Luckey, founder of defense technology start-up Anduril, has become a prominent figure in the U.S. military’s efforts to modernize its capabilities with autonomous systems. Anthropic and Pentagon AI Talks Collapse, OpenAI Steps In

Negotiations between AI firm Anthropic and the Pentagon broke down due to interpersonal conflicts and competition factors, ending the possibility of a contract for Anthropic’s technology. Shortly after, OpenAI reached a new agreement with the Defense Department to supply AI tools following President Trump’s directive to halt use of Anthropic’s systems across federal agencies. The episode underscores challenges in government procurement involving emerging AI companies and demonstrates how internal dynamics influence technology partnerships in the defense sector.

Instagram to Notify Parents About Teens’ Self-Harm Searches With Opt-In Feature

Instagram has introduced a new feature aimed at increasing parental awareness of teen users’ mental health by sending notifications to parents if their child makes multiple searches related to suicide or self-harm on the platform. Importantly, this feature requires users’ consent to activate notifications. The update reflects ongoing efforts by social media companies to address mental health challenges among young users while balancing privacy concerns. Such measures may influence how parents and platforms collaborate to support at-risk youth online.

Paramount-Warner Bros. Merger Faces Regulatory Review

Global regulators are set to scrutinize Paramount’s $111 billion merger with Warner Bros., an acquisition that would combine two major entertainment companies. Officials are examining the deal for potential competition issues in the media and content markets. This regulatory assessment is part of a broader trend of increased oversight on large mergers that could affect market diversity and consumer choice. The outcome will have implications for the future structure of the entertainment industry.
