Challenges in Detecting AI-Generated Images and Videos

Researchers evaluated over 1,000 tests of AI detection tools designed to identify synthetic images and videos. While these detectors showed some ability to flag fake content, they also demonstrated notable limitations, including false positives and difficulty with new or sophisticated AI-generated media. An analysis of the semiconductor supply chain warns that a potential Chinese invasion of Taiwan could disrupt global chip exports, affecting U.S. technology companies and the broader economy.
