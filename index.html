Bethlehem Christmas Celebrations Resume

For the first time since the Gaza war began, Bethlehem has lit its public Christmas tree and allowed celebrations to take place. Bethlehem, in the occupied West Bank, had canceled all public Christmas events for the past two years due to ongoing conflict. The return of these festivities may offer a brief moment of hope and normalcy amid the challenges faced by the local community.
