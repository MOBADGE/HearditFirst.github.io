Severe Flooding in Vietnam Causes Dozens of Deaths

Vietnam is grappling with the aftermath of days of intense rain that have led to widespread flooding and landslides. Authorities and residents are facing a difficult recovery ahead as they deal with the impact of this natural disaster.
