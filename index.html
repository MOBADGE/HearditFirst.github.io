The United States has proposed a draft peace plan for the Ukraine conflict, which reportedly favors Moscow's position. **Gaza: Armed Groups and Peace Prospects**

In Gaza, several armed groups opposed to Hamas are positioning themselves for roles in any future peace arrangement. Some of these groups receive backing from Israel, adding complexity to the local power dynamics. The situation underscores the challenges in establishing a lasting peace in the region, given the overlapping and fragmented alliances among armed factions.

**Natural Disasters and Accidents**

Heavy rains in Vietnam have led to severe flooding, killing at least 41 people. The worst-affected areas include the coastal cities of Hoi An and Nha Trang, as well as a major coffee-producing region. Relief and recovery efforts are underway to assist those impacted. Separately, an Indian fighter jet pilot tragically died following a crash at the Dubai airshow. Authorities have launched a court of inquiry to investigate the cause of the accident.

**Security Concerns and Legal Issues**

In Nigeria, pupils were abducted from a Catholic school in a recent attack. Security forces are actively working to assess the situation and to locate the missing children. The country's attorney general stated she may not be able to return if she attends the ceremony. Additionally, in Canada, Marcel Breton lost a 16-year legal battle to reclaim C$1.2 million seized by police from his home.

**Environment and Science**

In scientific news, researchers have identified the cause of a recent series of earthquakes near Santorini. The seismic activity was traced to magma moving through Earth's crust over a span of three months, causing thousands of tremors in the area. This finding contributes to a better understanding of volcanic and earthquake processes.
