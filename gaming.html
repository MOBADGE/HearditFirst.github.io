Valve Discontinues Steam Deck LCD Model

Valve has quietly stopped production of the 256GB Steam Deck model with an LCD screen. This version, regarded as a good entry point into handheld PC gaming due to its lower cost compared to the newer OLED models, is now sold out and will no longer be available. Valve appears to be consolidating its hardware lineup as it looks forward to future Steam-related devices. The discontinuation may affect buyers seeking a budget-friendly Steam Deck option, as the remaining models feature OLED screens and carry higher prices. This move also reflects the growing market for handheld gaming PCs, where Valve remains a significant player amidst increasing competition.
