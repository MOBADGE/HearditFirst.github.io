Steam Recovers as Epic Games Store Experiences Outage

On December 24th, Steam experienced significant downtime affecting its store, community, and web API services starting shortly after 1 pm ET. The unofficial Steam Status site reported websocket errors, and community features such as discussions, groups, and matchmaking services for Valve games were unavailable. Despite this, Steam game clients remained playable during the outage. Valve had not yet provided an official explanation at the time of reporting.

Shortly after, the situation improved for Steam, which appeared to return to normal by later in the day. However, the Epic Games Store began to face heavy disruptions, indicating possible widespread server issues affecting major digital storefronts on the day before Christmas. These outages highlight ongoing challenges in maintaining online services during peak periods, affecting user access to digital gaming communities and features.
