Microsoft Investigates Boot Failures Caused by Latest Windows Update

Microsoft is looking into reports of devices failing to boot after installing the January 2026 Windows update KB5074109 and subsequent patches. Affected machines display a stop code "UNMOUNTABLE_BOOT_VOLUME" and cannot complete startup, showing a black screen with a restart prompt. This issue appears mainly on physical devices rather than virtual machines and may affect primarily business users.

Microsoft recommends users who have not yet installed the update to delay doing so until the issue is resolved. Those experiencing problems must use recovery tools or perform clean reinstalls to regain access. The company states the number of affected devices is limited but is actively investigating the cause.

This situation illustrates ongoing challenges with maintaining stable system updates and the importance of cautious rollout, particularly in enterprise environments.
