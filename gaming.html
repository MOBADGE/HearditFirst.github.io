Hytale Developers Clarify New Patch and Magic System

Recently released updates for *Hytale* introduced a grimoire item that allows players to summon undead minions briefly. The community expressed concerns that this represented the upcoming full magic and mana system. In response, Hypixel CEO Simon Collins-Laflamme clarified on social media that the current grimoire is not the final magic system, describing it instead as a temporary, experimental item meant to add variety during early access. The developer intends to refine the magical gameplay, including mana use and spellcasting, in future updates. This conversation underscores the ongoing iterative process of game development during early access.
