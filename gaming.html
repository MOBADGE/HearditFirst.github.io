Nioh 3 Boss Guides: Yamagata Masakage and Jakotsu-baba

Two significant boss fights from Nioh 3 have detailed guides published, providing strategies to overcome their challenges.

- **Yamagata Masakage**: Considered an introductory but instructive boss, Yamagata teaches players the importance of the game's Burst Break mechanic‚Äîa timed counterattack that breaks enemy stamina. Players are advised to be aggressive following his combos, use the more mobile Ninja Mode for dodging, and learn to recognize his grab and slam attacks for timely evasion.

- **Jakotsu-baba**: This first major boss encounter within the Crucible realm brings additional difficulty because damage reduces maximum health. Preparation tips include acquiring the Supreme Parry and Enduring Destruction skills, equipping specific Soul Cores like Mezuki for summons, upgrading healing flasks, and reaching a higher character level beforehand. Jakotsu-baba has distinct attack patterns such as Burst Break windows, grabs using a chain, and elemental slams that require careful timing and avoidance.

Both fights highlight core combat elements essential for progression in Nioh 3 and help players understand how to build their characters for success.
