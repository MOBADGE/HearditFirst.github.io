Tabletop Simulator Announces Free 2.0 Update with UI Overhaul and Paid Mods Marketplace

Berserk Games, the developer behind Tabletop Simulator, has announced a significant free update scheduled for release this year. Version 2.0 will revamp the user interface to improve clarity and ease of use, with a particular focus on compatibility with Steam Deck handhelds. The update will feature two distinct modes: "Play Mode," simplified for regular gameplay, and "Edit Mode," aimed at creators and advanced users for content design.

A notable addition is a Creator Marketplace that will host paid user-generated mods, running alongside the existing free Steam Workshop content. This has drawn mixed reactions from the player community, with concerns about potential licensing issues and the impact on free modding culture.

**Why it matters:** The update modernizes Tabletop Simulator's platform and seeks to provide sustainable revenue for creators. However, the introduction of paid mods raises questions about community dynamics and access to unofficial content, which has historically been a core appeal of the game.

Dispatch Sells Over 3 Million Copies in 2025; Romance Choices Stand Out

AdHoc Studio revealed that Dispatch, an interactive superhero adventure game released in 2025, sold more than 3 million copies last year. The developers shared detailed player statistics, including that only around 5% of players completed the game without engaging in romantic storylines. Popular romance options drew millions of players, highlighting ongoing player interest in narrative relationships.

The game faced a challenging development cycle with content cuts, including the removal of some explicit scenes. Despite this, it achieved notable critical and commercial success, attracting substantial viewership on streaming platforms.

**Why it matters:** The success of Dispatch underscores the viability and popularity of narrative-driven interactive adventures. It also highlights how romantic subplots remain a significant factor in player engagement.
