People of Note: A Musical RPG with a Focus on Puns and Turn-Based Combat

People of Note is a turn-based RPG that centers on the theme of music snobbery, where the protagonist tries to bridge genre divides in a music-themed world. The game combines typical RPG mechanics with musical references, puns, and light rhythm elements. While the soundtrack and combat may not be groundbreaking, the game offers options to skip puzzles or battles, allowing players to tailor their experience. The design focuses on humor and thematic elements more than deep mechanical innovation. This approach provides a unique tone, where the enjoyment largely comes from the writing and playful use of music culture rather than complex gameplay systems.
