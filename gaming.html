**Arc Raiders Expands Anti-Cheat Measures to Steam Family Sharing**

Embark Studios is intensifying its fight against cheaters in the third-person shooter Arc Raiders by extending bans and restrictions to entire Steam Family Sharing groups linked to suspended accounts. Previously, banned players could circumvent penalties by using shared copies from family members. Now, if one account is banned, all other accounts sharing that license are automatically restricted or banned as well. This policy applies even if family members leave the sharing group until their punishment expires. The change follows community reports of cheating issues and developer efforts to restore fair play, including returning stolen loot to affected players.

*Why it matters:* This move addresses a common loophole in digital game sharing that allowed banned players to continue cheating through family accounts, helping maintain integrity and fairness in multiplayer matches.
