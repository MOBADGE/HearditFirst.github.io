LABS Works Announces New Platformer "Lovish" Releasing February 5

LABS Works, the studio behind the well-regarded 2021 metroidvania *Astalon: Tears of the Earth*, is set to release a new game titled *Lovish* on February 5. Unlike *Astalon*, *Lovish* is a single-screen platformer that combines straightforward combat with puzzle elements. The game will feature over 50 levels and randomised event scenes that add variety through character interactions, loot, and boss battles.

Artist and composer Matt Kap returns to contribute pixel art and soundtrack, bringing his distinctive chunky, high-contrast style familiar from titles such as *Castle in the Darkness*. A free demo is currently available on Steam, offering players a chance to experience the game ahead of its launch. This new title marks a shift from metroidvania exploration towards a more focused puzzle-platforming experience.
