Its approach to supply management and mission objectives emphasizes thoughtful squad composition rather than brute force, marking it as a notable contender for strategy enthusiasts.

**Airframe Ultra Brings Racing and Brawling Together at Steam Next Fest**

Following a shift away from their previous, darker style, the creators of *Rain World* have unveiled *Airframe Ultra*, a multiplayer hoverbike racing game with combat elements. Available to try in the Steam Next Fest, the game combines high-speed racing on futuristic tracks with on-foot brawls in arena sections. Players can dismount their bikes to fight opponents using a range of melee and ranged weapons before resuming the race. Besides its chaotic action, the game features deep mechanics, including advanced movement techniques and vehicle handling that reward mastery.

Notably, *Airframe Ultra* avoids common multiplayer monetization methods like battle passes or ranked modes, instead opting for a one-time purchase with potential for ongoing development. The game‚Äôs emphasis on quick, accessible races with a party-game atmosphere distinguishes it from typical racing or fighting games.
