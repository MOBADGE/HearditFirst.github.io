**Nutmeg Offers an Ambitious Football Management Deckbuilder with Mixed Results** *Nutmeg* is a deckbuilding football management game that evokes nostalgia for late 1980s and early 1990s English football culture. Players manage their team using cards to influence match events and strategic decisions, with some light meta-management aspects such as stadium upgrades and merchandising. The game captures an appealing retro atmosphere and offers a satisfying core match experience reminiscent of simpler football games of the past. However, supplementary systems like managing merchandising and club finances feel like clutter rather than meaningful additions, detracting from the main gameplay loop. The user interface is also criticized for being clunky and unintuitive. Overall, *Nutmeg* presents a solid core concept with room for improvement, especially in streamlining management features and interface design.
