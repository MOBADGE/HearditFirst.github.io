Bite-sized summaries of the latest gaming trends — without the hype.

Updated: March 09, 2026

Five Recent Steam Releases You May Have Missed Several new games arrived on Steam recently, offering a range of experiences across different genres. *Homura Hime* is a third-person action game reminiscent of classic titles like *Devil May Cry*, featuring a straightforward combat system with an anime aesthetic. *Planet of Lana 2* continues the cinematic platformer style of its predecessor with expanded environments and a mix of puzzles, stealth, and platforming. Racing fans have *iRacing Arcade*, a casual and cartoon-style offshoot of the realistic *iRacing* sim, aimed at a broader audience. Meanwhile, *Shinehill* blends farming simulation, fishing, dungeon crawling, and social elements, starring an alien spy fitting into a new community. Lastly, *Angel Engine* offers a point-and-click horror experience connected to an existing analog horror series, exploring dark themes through a retro style. These games highlight the diversity found in Steam's regular influx of releases.

Ultima 8 Retrospective Explores the Legacy of a CRPG Classic YouTuber Majuular released a comprehensive three-hour documentary on *Ultima 8*, part of an ongoing retrospective series about the Ultima franchise. The series examines each game’s narrative, design, and impact, placing them in historical context while recounting the development stories, including insights about creator Richard Garriott. While *Ultima 8* is not the most acclaimed in the series, the documentary shows its influence on later role-playing games and the CRPG genre as a whole. Understanding Ultima’s history is relevant given the current popularity and evolving standards of CRPGs, acknowledging the foundations that shape today’s role-playing games.

Hytale Emphasizes Modding and Community Integration The development team behind *Hytale* is actively encouraging modding within the game through initiatives like a modding contest offering significant cash prizes. Simon Collins-Laflamme, Hypixel’s founder, explained the focus on mod support is partly because the studio hires talent from its own modding community. This approach aims to keep the team agile and directly connected to the players who create popular modifications. Although this strategy requires upfront investment, it helps maintain a development culture that values creativity rooted in community contributions. Given the strong modding activity already existing around *Hytale*, this strategy may influence the game’s evolution significantly.

Crazy Taxi Cityscapes Now Explorable in Browsers A coder named Will has transformed the city environments from the Dreamcast-era arcade game *Crazy Taxi* into free-flying, explorable levels accessible through web browsers. The project uses an open-source recreation of the game’s original rendering techniques and has been added to noclip.website, a digital archive showcasing various iconic video game levels. This effort provides a new perspective on the game’s urban design, allowing players to appreciate its landmark-rich environments without gameplay pressure. The project also highlights technical challenges in mapping old game assets for modern viewing and offers a glimpse of classic game worlds from a fresh angle.

Banquet for Fools: A Unique CRPG Exiting Early Access *Banquet for Fools*, a recently released CRPG, combines claymation-style visuals with dark narrative themes and beat ’em up combat mechanics. The game is set on a contested island where the player, commanding a group of guards, investigates mysterious disappearances. Its design features include no quest markers, requiring players to explore and engage with characters carefully. Combat involves real-time action with pause options, with one character actively controlled while others fight autonomously. Moreover, players can choose to imprison defeated enemies instead of killing them, affecting the story and resources. The game left early access following a major update that added new content, offering a distinct alternative in today’s CRPG landscape.
