Void War DMCA Takedown Highlights Intellectual Property Concerns

Void War, a roguelike space strategy game inspired by FTL, was briefly removed from Steam after receiving a DMCA notice from Games Workshop. The issue stemmed from the depiction of armored characters bearing striking resemblance to Warhammer 40,000's Chaos Space Marines, particularly due to their oversized shoulder pads and distinctive backpack design. Developer Tundra Interactive complied by removing the contentious trailer and revising the game's store page. The case underscores the fine line between inspiration and infringement when game art closely echoes existing intellectual properties. Despite the removal, Void War remains available with the adjusted content, illustrating the importance for developers to navigate IP considerations carefully.
