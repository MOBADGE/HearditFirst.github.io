**Resident Evil Requiem Fuses Action and Survival Horror**

Capcom has released early impressions of *Resident Evil Requiem*, which attempts to blend the action-oriented style of recent Resident Evil titles with the survival horror elements fans appreciate in the older games. The game features two protagonists: the confident Leon Kennedy, who wields a chainsaw and retains action skills from *Resident Evil 4 Remake*, and Grace, a more vulnerable newcomer navigating tense hospital corridors reminiscent of classic Resident Evil settings. The game switches between Leon's more combat-heavy gameplay and Grace's cautious exploration and puzzle-solving, supported by crafting mechanics that utilize blood and scraps to create ammo and other items. This approach marks a notable effort to find balance between survival horror and action after the mixed reception of *Resident Evil 6*. If successful, it could realign the series with its roots while maintaining modern gameplay innovations.
