Sword Hero Demo Draws Attention for Exceptional Melee Combat

The indie RPG *Sword Hero* recently updated its demo on Steam, impressing players with melee combat that some are comparing favorably to FromSoftware titles. Set in an open-world ring environment blending medieval and sci-fi elements, the game offers a complex simulation with NPCs having daily routines and a procedural revenge system. The new demo features a tournament mode focusing exclusively on combat, with 35 fights across randomized arenas and gear.

The combat stands out due to its weight and precision, combining tactical movement similar to FPS games with a parry system reminiscent of *Sekiro*. Players must use deliberate combos with attacks tied to movement direction, and the game encourages emergent play through environmental hazards and a detailed dismemberment system. While melee combat shines, other playstyles such as magic and archery in the demo are still underdeveloped. The project is currently funded via Kickstarter, with development starting in late 2024 and a planned release in December 2027.
