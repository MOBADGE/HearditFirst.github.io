Anthem's Final Hours: Players Say Goodbye

After nearly four years since its launch, EA is shutting down the servers for *Anthem*, the multiplayer live service game by BioWare. Though met with lukewarm reception at its release due to an unclear identity between solo and cooperative play, *Anthem* maintained a small yet dedicated community through patches and updates. Today marks the last chance for players, known as Freelancers, to log in and experience the world of Fort Tarsis before the game goes offline permanently.

Players have been commemorating the game by sharing screenshots and achievements on social media and Twitch streams, recalling the game's tagline, "Strong alone, stronger together."
