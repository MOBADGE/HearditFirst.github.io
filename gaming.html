Tencent and Sony Settle Dispute Over Horizon-Like Game "Light of Motiram"

Tencent and Sony have resolved their legal dispute concerning Tencent's survival game *Light of Motiram*, which bore notable similarities to Sony's *Horizon* series. The settlement details remain confidential, but the lawsuit has been dismissed with prejudice, making it final. Following the settlement, *Light of Motiram* has been removed from both the Steam and Epic Games Store, though its website and social channels remain active. Tencent and Sony will each cover their own legal costs. This resolution matters because it closes a high-profile intellectual property conflict involving major industry players, with potential implications for how resemblance between games is addressed in the future.

Arc Raiders Adjusts Blueprint Drop Rates After Player Feedback

The developer Embark has issued a hotfix for *Arc Raiders* that slightly lowers the blueprint drop rates following a recent update that increased them significantly. Despite the reduction, drop rates remain higher than before the 1.7.0 update. Since launch, rare blueprint items had been hard to come by, making the temporary increase a welcome change for many players. Alongside this adjustment, Embark is offering $10 worth of premium credits for free during the holiday season. This patch aims to find a balance between rewarding players and maintaining game economy stability, addressing the mixed reactions to the initial loot abundance.
