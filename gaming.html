Nexus Mods Denies Discord Ban Rumors

The popular mod hosting site Nexus Mods addressed recent rumors that Discord is banning users for sharing Nexus Mods links. The community manager stated that no widespread or known incidents of account moderation connected to posting Nexus Mods links exist. Discord has yet to comment officially, but investigations and community tests found no evidence of automatic bans tied specifically to these links. Previous small-scale false positives regarding copyright enforcement were resolved quickly and did not amount to multi-year bans as claimed elsewhere. The clarification is important for modding communities that rely on Discord for communication and link sharing, offering reassurance that sharing Nexus Mods remains safe and does not risk penalties under current moderation policies.

Fortnite Bans Paid Prize Wheel Transactions After Brief Experiment

Epic Games has announced a new rule banning in-game transactions that affect prize wheels in Fortnite Creative islands, taking effect January 20. This move follows controversy over the third-party Fortnite game *Steal the Brainrot* introducing paid spins on prize wheels as a form of gambling targeted towards younger players. The policy forbids selling spins, luck boosts, or any items that influence prize wheel outcomes. This comes shortly after Epic enabled direct in-island monetization in early January, which *Steal the Brainrot* exploited quickly. The ban addresses concerns raised by players and creators about gambling mechanics in Fortnite games aimed at minors. Epic stated it will continue reviewing developer practices and adjust rules accordingly.
