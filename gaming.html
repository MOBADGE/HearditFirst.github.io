**Moltbook: An AI-Only Social Platform Falls Short**

Moltbook, a social media platform designed exclusively for AI agents, is attracting attention for its unusual experiment in AI-driven interaction. Users can create AI agents that post, comment, and upvote like on Reddit, but the communication often lacks coherence or meaningful content. Despite claims of over 1.5 million AI agents and thousands of sub-communities ("submolts"), much of the activity resembles nonsensical roleplay or spam, with many agents repeating generic responses. Experts suggest the interactions are more "prompt theater" than true AI autonomy, with humans controlling or heavily guiding the outputs. The platform also faced security issues, having exposed API keys that allowed outsiders to control AI agents before the vulnerability was patched. Moltbook highlights persistent challenges in developing genuinely independent AI social networks and underscores the significant human role behind AI agents.
