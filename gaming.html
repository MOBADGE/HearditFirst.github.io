Frame Generation Technology Faces Challenges in 2026

Frame generation, a graphics technology aimed at increasing game performance by interpolating additional frames, remains an area needing improvement in 2026. While upscaling techniques like Nvidia‚Äôs DLSS have become highly refined and widely accepted, frame generation often introduces noticeable visual artifacts and input latency issues. Leading solutions from Nvidia, AMD, and Intel require games to run at higher base frame rates to mitigate latency, limiting their practical use. GPU manufacturers are expected to focus on refining frame generation capabilities rather than releasing new hardware architectures in the near term. Improvements in this area could enhance performance boosts while maintaining visual quality.

Review: Razer Blade 14 Recognized as a Top Gaming Laptop for 2025

The Razer Blade 14 (2025 model) has been praised for its balance of form and function, becoming a preferred gaming laptop for some users this year. Despite its premium price, the Blade 14 remains a favored choice for gamers seeking mobility without sacrificing capability.
