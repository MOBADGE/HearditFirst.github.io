Luto: A Thoughtful Psychological Horror Game from 2025

Luto, released in 2025 by Broken Bird Games, is gaining attention as an underappreciated psychological horror game. Drawing inspiration from titles like P.T. and The Stanley Parable, it blends ambient dread with a narrator's cheeky and distant voice. Players control Sam, who is trapped in a house that resets days repeatedly, evoking themes of grief and emotional exhaustion. The gameplay centers around navigating a dimly lit, unsettling environment where escape attempts are constantly thwarted by the house's design and an antagonistic narrator. Though short, lasting around five to six hours, Luto effectively portrays an uneasy atmosphere and psychological distress, making it notable for fans of contemplative horror. It is available now on Steam.
