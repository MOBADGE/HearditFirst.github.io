**Final Fantasy 11 Game Masters Manually Fix Notorious Monster Bug**

Near its 24th anniversary, Final Fantasy 11 encountered a server glitch affecting notorious monsters in the Limbus battle zones. These rare enemies contribute to monthly loot rewards, but a data error caused defeated monsters to respawn early, misleading the server into thinking they were undefeated. This impacts loot quality for all players on affected servers. To resolve the issue, Square Enix dispatched Game Masters to manually defeat each bugged monster, ensuring the server correctly recognizes their defeat for the monthly tally. This unusual fix highlights the complexities of maintaining long-running MMOs with legacy systems.
