New Noir Thriller Mod Revives Original Half-Life Engine

A fresh total conversion mod called *The Last Goodbye* has been released, built on the original *Half-Life* GoldSrc engine. Created by the Guild of Inventors mod team, it offers a pulp noir thriller experience set in the fictional City of Prava. Players control Lucille, a gun-for-hire on a mission to assassinate a gangster named Bullseye. The mod combines shooting, puzzle-solving, and exploration elements, with a focus on storytelling delivered through in-game dialogue and stylized cutscenes inspired by Tele-snaps from old *Doctor Who* episodes.

*The Last Goodbye* currently features its first act, with two more planned. It marks a creative use of classic technology and offers an alternative noir narrative to bigger, contemporary titles. The mod is freely available on ModDB for fans of classic engine conversions and noir themes.

Ustwo Shifts Focus to PC-First Development After Platform Challenges

After *Monument Valley 3* was removed from Netflix just six months after launch, Ustwo, the studio behind the *Monument Valley* series, confirmed a strategic shift from mobile-first toward PC-first development for future titles. CEO Maria Sayans explained that being perceived as a mobile-first developer limits what can be achieved on PC and consoles, including compromises in design and marketing.

The experience with *Monument Valley 3* and its Netflix exclusivity prompted the studio to pursue multiplatform launches centered on PC to build more direct relationships with their audience. Ustwo is known mostly for its stylish mobile puzzle games but has also developed turn-based tactics and environmental games that have reached PC audiences.
