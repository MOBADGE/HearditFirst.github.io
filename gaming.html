Arc Raiders Accidentally Nerfs Guns, Leading to Mass Recycling Incident

Arc Raiders experienced unintended changes when a recent update reduced the rate of fire for certain weapons, including the Venator. Although this was a bug and not intentional, one player, frustrated by the change, recycled 109 Venator firearms from their inventory. The developer quickly addressed the bug, ultimately overshooting corrections and unintentionally buffing the fire rate of some guns.

This incident highlights the challenges developers face in balancing live games and communicating changes clearly. For players, such bugs can disrupt gameplay and inventory management. While the Venator is now more powerful than before the patch, the change is expected to be adjusted again. The recycling episode also amusingly freed up considerable inventory space, underscoring the scale of in-game item hoarding some players maintain.
