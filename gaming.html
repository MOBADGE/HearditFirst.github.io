**Sovereign Tea Turn-Based Tactics Game Now Free**

*Sovereign Tea*, a turn-based tactics game centered on a lore-driven conflict between Tea Princesses and the Coffee Empire, has changed to a permanently free-to-play model on Steam. Originally released with a price tag in 2021, the developers cited having made sufficient revenue to allow broader access to the game. The title features over 40 customizable Tea Minions with unique mechanics based on ingredient combinations and twelve distinct Princesses with different personalities and abilities. This move aims to increase the game's audience and invite new players to experience its strategic gameplay and narrative charm.

**Atom Zombie Smasher Receives 15th Anniversary Update**

*Atom Zombie Smasher*, a top-down zombie rescue simulation released 15 years ago, has unexpectedly received a substantive update on Steam. Changes include an expanded ability to slow down gameplay to 1% speed, redefining the game's pacing to near turn-based, usability improvements such as deselecting units with the Escape key, and bug fixes across platforms. The update also addresses a Mac startup issue and adds a crash reporting feature. Despite its age, the game remains available at a 50% discount on Steam and serves as an important early work by developer Blendo Games, who later created titles like *Gravity Bone* and *Skin Deep*.
