The End is Nigh Platformer Available at Discount

Tap Tap Loot Brings Combat to the Bongo Cat Clicker Genre

A new clicker game titled Tap Tap Loot introduces an action-oriented twist to the popular Bongo Cat concept by pairing the familiar on-screen cat with combat mechanics. Players control the cat as it fights waves of enemies, collecting weapons and equipment automatically while engaging in simple keyboard-based gameplay. The game features RPG-style item upgrades, co-op multiplayer, and multiple biomes, offering more depth than basic clicker games. Tap Tap Loot is currently available as a demo during Steam Next Fest, though some interface adjustments are needed for different screen setups. While it adds complexity compared to original Bongo Cat, it aims to engage players looking for more than passive gameplay.
