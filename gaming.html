Microsoft Office Security Vulnerabilities Addressed

Microsoft recently announced patches for two critical security vulnerabilities in Microsoft Office, identified as CVE-2026-26110 and CVE-2026-26113. These issues have high severity ratings due to their potential to allow code execution by anyone with local access to the machine. Fortunately, official patches are already available for Office versions from 2016 onward, as earlier versions like Office 2013 are no longer supported. While these flaws require local access to exploit and do not pose a remote attack risk, updating Office installations promptly remains important to protect against potential misuse.

Marathon Update Improves Gameplay Balance

Bungie released update 1.0.0.4 for its extraction shooter Marathon, addressing numerous player concerns primarily related to difficulty balance. The patch increases the spawn rates of medical supplies and ammunition on key maps, and boosts starting ammo in certain free loadouts. Thermal scopes received nerfs in terms of range and clarity to reduce frustrating long-range encounters. Additionally, certain powerful weapons now have restrictions on their availability to limit exploits. The update also slightly reduces enemy health levels to ease the resource burden without eliminating challenge. Other adjustments include extended sound ranges for gunfire and improved objective markers for better player awareness. This comprehensive update reflects Bungie’s responsiveness to player feedback in refining the game’s combat experience.

Rust Movie Discussions Underway

Facepunch, developer of the survival game Rust, has confirmed it is in early talks with interested parties about a possible film adaptation. According to the studio’s director, no concrete plans exist yet, and the development process toward a movie is complex and slow. Facepunch is cautious about rights agreements and emphasizes the need to trust whoever would produce the adaptation. Despite Rust’s open-ended sandbox nature and lack of a defined protagonist, the studio remains open to creative possibilities, though no timeline for production has been announced.

Clair Obscur Studio Ends Legal Dispute

Sandfall, the studio behind the role-playing game Clair Obscur: Expedition 33, has dropped legal action against the creators of the comic L’Académie Clair-Obscur. After initially issuing a cease-and-desist request concerning the comic’s sales, the studio reversed course, apologizing for delayed communication and clarifying it does not intend to oppose the graphic novel. Sandfall cited its commitment to supporting artists and fostering creativity. Both parties have expressed satisfaction with the resolution following the public dispute, which was noted for causing some community concern.

Framework Advises Purchase of High-Storage PCs Soon

Framework, maker of modular laptops and desktops, announced that it will soon increase prices on configurations with large storage drives (2TB and above), citing depleted stocks of lower-cost SSD inventory. Current prices for these high-capacity drives offered by Framework remain significantly below market averages, but the company expects adjustments as it refills stock at higher supplier costs. Framework also indicated ongoing price rises for system memory and some pre-built configurations are expected in the coming months due to a volatile memory market. Customers looking for high-storage setups are advised to purchase before these changes take effect.

Overwatch Mid-Season Update Brings Gameplay and Cosmetic Adjustments

Overwatch’s latest mid-season patch includes a few subtle character model changes and gameplay tweaks. Mizuki received a visual update with a sharper jawline in a specific skin, preceding plans to redesign the newly released hero Anran’s model, which has faced criticism. Gameplay adjustments include changes to Mizuki’s healing field radius and delay, as well as modifications to perks for other heroes to balance aggression and sustain. The update also reverted unpopular item icon changes in the Stadium game mode to improve readability, demonstrating ongoing efforts to fine-tune user experience based on community feedback.
