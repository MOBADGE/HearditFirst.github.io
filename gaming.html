Fortnite Developer Seeks Patience After Major Layoffs

Epic Games recently announced significant layoffs, cutting roughly 20% of its workforce, which means over 1,000 employees were let go. Despite Fortnite remaining one of the most popular games globally, Epic’s CEO Tim Sweeney acknowledged challenges in consistently delivering quality content for each season. Fortnite producer Robby Williams addressed fans directly on social media, urging patience as the remaining team "picks up the pieces." He admitted the full impact of these layoffs on Fortnite’s development and content updates for the rest of the year and beyond is still unclear. Epic plans to hold a company meeting soon to outline the roadmap ahead. This situation highlights the difficulties even successful live-service games face in managing resources and maintaining quality amid financial pressures.

IO Interactive’s Freedom Fighters Found Success Against the Odds

IO Interactive, mainly known for its Hitman franchise, took a risk early in its history by creating Freedom Fighters, a third-person shooter set in a Soviet-occupied USA. The game featured a unique squad-based battle system that felt dynamic and engaging. Initially, the studio struggled to convince publisher EA of their vision, producing mostly empty and unexciting levels. To overcome skepticism, IO Interactive crafted a makeshift demo using music and gameplay footage, which surprisingly impressed EA executives and secured the project’s continuation. This story sheds light on the challenges studios face when navigating publisher expectations, especially when branching out from established series, and how perseverance and creative presentation can secure a game’s future.

Owlcat Games Reveals Companions for New Mass Effect-Inspired RPG

Owlcat Games, known for isometric RPGs like Pathfinder: Kingmaker, is developing The Expanse: Osiris Reborn, a third-person shooter RPG drawing clear inspiration from the Mass Effect series. The studio shared early details about the game’s companions, emphasizing their distinct personalities, backgrounds, and combat roles. Set in the hard sci-fi universe of The Expanse, the game’s crew includes a diverse cast such as an identical twin character, a disgraced combat medic, a sniper, a hacker, and more, each with unique "Exploit" abilities that influence combat tactics. These companion details highlight the studio’s focus on combining narrative depth with tactical combat, marking a notable departure from their previous style and an attempt to capture the character-driven appeal of classic sci-fi RPGs.

Sony and Honda Cancel Ambitious Afeela Electric Car Project

Sony and Honda jointly announced the cancellation of their Afeela electric vehicle project, initially unveiled in 2023. This decision follows Honda’s recent reassessment of its electric vehicle strategy due to changing market conditions, including U.S. tariffs, fossil fuel policy shifts, and increased competition from Chinese manufacturers. The expensive price tag of the Afeela — starting at $90,000 — likely made it less competitive against more affordable EVs like the Hyundai Ioniq 5. While the termination of the project is disappointing, especially since Afeela aimed to blend mobility with entertainment, Sony and Honda indicated they will continue discussions on their joint venture’s future. The move illustrates how evolving policy and economic factors directly affect innovations at the intersection of technology and automotive industries.

How to Find the Frostcursed Armor in Crimson Desert

The Frostcursed Armor set in Pearl Abyss’s RPG Crimson Desert offers strong ice resistance and useful bonuses like daze immunity and extra experience gain. Players can find all five pieces in north Demeniss, generally hidden behind waterfalls that require using the Stab skill to access. The armor pieces are located in the Sanctum of Expiation, Chattering Rocks, Lunar Spirit Grotto, Well of Tragedy Grotto, and Mistshard Cave. This gear is notable because it does not require defeating difficult bosses, making it more accessible to players exploring the colder regions of the game. For players looking to improve cold resistance and experience gains, this armor set provides a practical and visually distinctive option.
