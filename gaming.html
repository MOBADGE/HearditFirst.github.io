Vince Zampella, Influential Game Developer, Dies in Car Accident

Vince Zampella, a key figure in the gaming industry and co-founder of Infinity Ward, has died in a car crash in the San Gabriel Mountains near Los Angeles. The 55-year-old was also head of Respawn Entertainment and Ripple Effect at Electronic Arts. Zampella played a major role in shaping popular franchises like Call of Duty, Titanfall, Apex Legends, and Battlefield. His passing is a significant loss to the industry. Electronic Arts and Respawn issued statements highlighting his profound impact on modern games and his legacy as a leader and creator.
