World of Warcraft: Midnight Launch Times Announced

The latest World of Warcraft expansion, *Midnight*, is set for release with early access beginning February 26 or 27 depending on your time zone. Players who purchased the Epic Edition will get a head start with early access at 3 pm PST on February 26 (Los Angeles), 6 pm EST (New York), 11 pm GMT (London), 12 am CET on February 27 (Berlin), and 10 am AEDT (Sydney). The general release for others will follow on March 2 or 3, with the same regional time structure. *Midnight* introduces major changes, such as removing most combat addons, significant class redesigns, player housing for the first time in the game's 20-plus year history, and reworked older zones. Early access allows dedicated players to begin exploring these features ahead of the full launch.
