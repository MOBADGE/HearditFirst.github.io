Modern MMOs Struggle to Balance Difficulty for All Players

Developers from titles like The Elder Scrolls Online acknowledge the near-impossible task of tuning overland and quest difficulty to satisfy everyone simultaneously.

For example, World of Warcraft‚Äôs upcoming ‚ÄúPrey‚Äù system will allow optional harder encounters in the open world, while Final Fantasy 14 designs its content with difficulty scaling to serve casual, midcore, and hardcore players alike.
