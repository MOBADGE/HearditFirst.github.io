Guides for Arc Raiders Quests: Eyes on the Prize and After Rain Comes

Two quests in the third-person shooter Arc Raiders that require bringing items into a match have been detailed recently. "Eyes on the Prize" tasks players with repairing a flooded solar panel on a secluded rooftop in Buried City. This requires carrying three wires into the match or scavenging them mid-game. Wires can be found by searching computers and tech containers across several map areas or recycling certain electronic items. The repair location is southwest of Plaza Rosa near the highway. Completing the quest rewards players with extended ammo magazines.

Similarly, the "After Rain Comes" mission involves fixing another flooded solar panel in Buried City, outside Grandioso Apartments. This quest demands five wires and two batteries brought into the match. Like the previous quest, the wiring components can be found in various tech-heavy areas or via recycling. Reward items include light sticks, cloths, and antiseptic supplies. Both quests emphasize preparation and inventory management since losing required items before completion can complicate the mission. These guides help players avoid pitfalls and streamline quest completion.

Samsung Advancing PCIe 6.0 SSD Production for Data Centers

Samsung is reportedly preparing full-scale production of PCIe 6.0 solid-state drives starting in the latter half of 2026, focused primarily on enterprise and AI datacenter use. These SSDs, including a 256 TB model unveiled last year, aim to meet growing storage demands driven by AI workloads. However, these high-capacity drives come with costs and features mostly suited for large-scale server environments rather than consumer gaming setups.

While PCIe 6.0 represents a generational performance leap, current gaming PCs typically perform well with PCIe 4.0 or 5.0 SSDs. Samsung and other manufacturers are thus prioritizing data center clients, and widespread desktop availability will likely come years later, if at all. This development underlines the distinction between storage tech progress for AI infrastructure versus consumer-level needs.

Disney Dreamlight Valley Salt Crystals Location

In the life simulation game Disney Dreamlight Valley, salt crystals are a somewhat rare cooking ingredient accessible only after purchasing the Storybook Vale DLC. Players can find salt crystals exclusively by fishing in the Everafter area, an eastern extension unlocked during the DLC.

To locate salt, fishing must be done in open water away from surface ripples, similar to how seaweed is found. Salt is used in various cooking recipes and several character quests, making it valuable for progressing friendship tasks and crafting. It also functions as a minor energy item or can be sold for in-game currency. This specific fishing requirement helps players focus their gathering efforts.
