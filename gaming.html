Hytale Development Challenges and Early Access Launch

Simon Collins-Laflamme, original co-founder of *Hytale*, recently reacquired the game from Riot Games after years in development under their ownership. He described the state of the game upon his return as "barely playable," with foundational systems like camera, movement, and combat needing extensive fixes.

The previous four years had focused heavily on rebuilding the engine rather than gameplay features, causing delays and loss of momentum. Since retaking control, the team has prioritized gameplay improvements and rapid shipping of features, planning an early access launch on January 13 with a $20 price point.

This approach aims to regain player trust through steady updates, despite the game's incomplete current state. Notably, *Hytale* will not be available on Steam at launch, instead offering access through its official store.
