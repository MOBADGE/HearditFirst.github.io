Fallout Co-Creator Tim Cain Addresses New Projects and IPs

Tim Cain, known for designing the original Fallout, spoke candidly about his stance on developing new games or sequels to his classic RPGs. Cain expressed frustration over repeatedly being asked when he would create new titles or revisit old IPs like Arcanum or Temple of Elemental Evil. He explained that while others have profited substantially from the intellectual properties he helped create, he personally sees limited benefit and control. Cain indicated he is content with his current work at Obsidian, where he holds non-lead roles, and prefers focusing on smaller personal projects rather than heading new big-budget initiatives. His comments shed light on challenges creators face around IP ownership and the complexities of game development careers.
