Fractal Design Scape: A New Standard for Gaming Headsets

The Fractal Design Scape wireless gaming headset has stood out among the many headsets tested in 2025, impressing with its blend of refinement, build quality, and user experience. A key highlight is its innovative charging stand, which uses Qi wireless technology combined with a magnetic docking mechanism. This design simplifies keeping the headset charged, encouraging users to dock it after use instead of leaving it scattered or obstructive on a desk. Sound quality is described as neutral and detailed, with software support for EQ adjustments.
