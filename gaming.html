Windows 11 Boot Failures Linked to Previous Update Problems

Microsoft has clarified that a recent Windows 11 boot failure bug, marked by the "UNMOUNTABLE_BOOT_VOLUME" stop error, stems from devices that failed to properly install the December 2025 security update. This improper update state then causes subsequent updates, like the January 2026 patch, to trigger boot issues.

The company is working on a partial fix to prevent further devices from encountering this problem during update installation. However, the fix does not restore systems already affected or prevent all improper states from occurring. This situation highlights challenges in updating widely-used operating systems and the difficulty of managing cumulative update issues.

Deadlock Adds Graves as Second New Playable Character

The multiplayer online battle arena game *Deadlock* has introduced Graves, a goth-themed character wielding supernatural abilities linked to a severed magical hand. Graves won second place in community voting over other candidate characters and is now available to players.

Her abilities include crowd control through grasping hands emerging from the ground, a spirit-damaging projectile called Jar of Dead, and an ultimate move summoning ghostly allies. Graves is noted for dealing area damage and strong teamfight presence, though she also features an auto-aim component that may appeal to less experienced players.
