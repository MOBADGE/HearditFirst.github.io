**Chinese DDR5 RAM Options Are Viable but Not Cheap**

The current high prices for DRAM continue to affect gaming PC builds, with brands like Corsair, G.Skill, and Kingston priced steeply. Attempts to find cheaper DDR5 RAM from Chinese manufacturers such as Cusu, Gugda, and Juhor on platforms like AliExpress and DHgate reveal that these alternatives do not represent significant bargains. For example, 16GB sticks range from $195 to over $400 depending on speed and seller. Testing by Hardware Unboxed on Chinese-made RAM modules indicates functionality is comparable to major brands, but longevity and reliability remain uncertain. Overall, the market pressures driving prices globally are reflected in these Chinese options, undermining the assumption that lesser-known brands offer substantially cheaper memory. Consumers seeking savings might find marginal reductions but should remain cautious about quality and after-sales support.

**Four Upcoming Birdwatching Games on Steam**

Birdwatching as a gaming niche continues to grow, with four upcoming titles offering various approaches to the hobby:
