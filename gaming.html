World of Warcraft Played Using Hot Dog Controllers

A player known as Addison2k has demonstrated that it is technically possible to play World of Warcraft Mythic Keystone runs using unconventional controllers made from hot dogs. These 3D-printed plastic controllers each contain four hot dogs wired for touch input, with one managing movement and the other handling abilities. While the setup proved playable for a Retribution Paladin, it came with limitations such as the inability to move the camera and difficulties dodging attacks, causing some reliance on the keyboard for specific keypresses. Despite the challenges, Addison2k successfully completed a run with teammates, though he noted that his hands smelled like hot dogs for hours afterward. This unusual experiment highlights the flexibility of modern gaming controls but is more of a novelty than a practical method of play.

Arc Raiders Sees Balanced Interest Between PvE and PvP Modes

Embark Studios shared player engagement data for their action shooter Arc Raiders, revealing that roughly 30% of the community focuses on Player vs Environment (PvE) content, another 30% on Player vs Player (PvP), and about 40% enjoy both. This balance surprised the developers, who had not anticipated such a passionate PvE-only player base. The game encourages cooperation through features like proximity voice chat, with over 95% of players using it, and challenges like the Matriarch enemy prompting spontaneous team-ups. Embark's executive producer Aleksander Grøndal noted that the game's hopeful setting, with nature recovering from ecological collapse, contributes to fostering cooperative player behavior. These insights are relevant for understanding player preferences in multiplayer shooters and may influence future content updates.

Euro Truck Simulator 2 to Add Türkiye in New Expansion

SCS Software announced Soul of Anatolia, a new expansion for Euro Truck Simulator 2 that will introduce the country of Türkiye as a playable region. This expansion will feature routes such as Istanbul to Aksaray, crossing notable infrastructure like the 1915 Çanakkale Bridge—the world’s longest suspension bridge—as well as cities including Ankara, Antalya, and Alanya. No release date has been provided yet. Euro Truck Simulator 2 has maintained steady popularity over 14 years, thanks in part to its relaxing gameplay loop and frequent content updates. This expansion marks the first major new country addition in 2026 and will expand the already extensive map with more authentic European locations.

PC Gaming Revenue Increasingly Comes from Games Outside the Top 20

Newzoo’s 2026 PC & Console Gaming Report indicates that more than 50% of PC gaming revenue in Western markets now originates from games ranked below the top 20 in sales charts. This long-tail effect has grown over recent years, with revenue for titles outside the top 20 increasing from 48% to 56% in 2025. Alongside this, playtime for these games rose by 44%, compared to fairly flat or slightly declining playtime for top 20 titles. Popular examples benefiting from this trend include enduring catalog games like Cyberpunk 2077, Elden Ring, Skyrim, as well as survival games such as Rust and DayZ, which continue to attract players through ongoing updates. The report contrasts this with console trends, where top-tier exclusive and sports franchises dominate playtime. This data highlights the diversity and longevity of PC gaming content beyond immediate hits.

Gloomwood Adds Official Controller Support

The stealth-focused immersive sim Gloomwood has received official controller support after years without it, improving playability on devices like the Steam Deck and other handheld PCs. The update includes fully integrated gamepad tutorials and extensive rebinding options. Although players could previously use custom or community-made gamepad layouts, the official support addresses usability and interface issues, such as showing correct button prompts. The update also introduces a new in-game shop location. Gloomwood launched in early access in 2022 and has received consistent updates and positive reviews. The addition of controller support may broaden the game’s accessibility to more players, especially those preferring couch play or portable gaming.
