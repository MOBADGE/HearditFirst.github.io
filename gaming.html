**Dino Crisis Returns on Steam with Controversial DRM**

Capcom has released the first two Dino Crisis games on Steam for the first time, priced at half their usual cost. These classic survival horror titles from the late 1990s bring nostalgic appeal but have been packaged with Enigma Protector DRM. This digital rights management tool has previously caused performance issues and community dissatisfaction in other Capcom titles, notably Resident Evil Revelations. Steam forum users have already voiced complaints about the DRM and the absence of achievements. The move aligns with Capcom's ongoing anti-modding policies, but since Dino Crisis lacks a substantial PC modding scene, the impact may be limited. While the games are now more accessible, the DRM choice may affect user experience.
