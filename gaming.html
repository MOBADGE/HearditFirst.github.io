Multiplayer Mod Brings Online Features to The Witcher 3

This mod allows players to connect on servers and interact with other players controlling characters from the game‚Äôs world, such as witchers, Yennefer, or Dandelion. Users can emote using console commands and engage in casual activities like fishing together.

However, the mod currently has significant limitations. It does not synchronize enemies, horses, or sailboats between players, meaning cooperative combat or travel is not possible. Features like Gwent remain single-player, though social interactions in taverns and similar locations are supported. Installing the mod requires several prerequisite mods and tweaks. While not providing full cooperative gameplay, Witcher Online offers a novel way to socialize within the universe of The Witcher 3.

Despite ongoing internal challenges, including project cancellations and organizational restructuring, Ubisoft plans increased investment in generative AI technologies.
