Vince Zampella, Influential Game Developer, Dies in Car Crash

Vince Zampella, co-founder of Infinity Ward and head of Respawn Entertainment and Ripple Effect under Electronic Arts, died in a car accident in the San Gabriel Mountains near Los Angeles. He was 55. Zampella played a key role in the creation of the Call of Duty series and later led Respawn on projects including Titanfall, Apex Legends, and Star Wars Jedi games. He had recently taken charge of the Battlefield franchise. His passing is regarded as a significant loss to the games industry. Electronic Arts and Respawn issued statements recognizing his influence and legacy. The cause of the crash remains unknown.
