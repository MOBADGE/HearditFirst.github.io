**Rainbow Six Siege Servers Return After Hacker Incident**

Rainbow Six Siege experienced a significant security breach over the weekend, during which hackers disrupted gameplay by randomly issuing bans and unbans, distributing rare skins, and granting players billions of in-game credits. Ubisoft responded by taking the servers offline to contain the breach. They later confirmed that no players would face penalties for the unauthorized credits, as all transactions during the incident would be reversed. While the servers have since been restored, the in-game marketplace remains offline. The event highlighted vulnerabilities in game security and disrupted the player community, though Ubisoft continues to investigate the scope and impact of the hack.
