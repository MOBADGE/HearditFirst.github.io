Closure of MOBA Supervive After Seven Months

Supervive, a MOBA-style battle royale developed by Theorycraft Games, is shutting down in February 2026, only seven months after its full launch. Despite being created by a team including former Riot Games veterans, the game failed to maintain a large enough player base to continue. Current active players number just over 200, which does not justify ongoing development costs. Theorycraft plans to pivot to a different type of game with a focus on quality and a narrower scope. Supervive will remain operational until February 25, with no further content updates planned. Refunds are available for recent purchases.
