Hooded Horse Publisher Bans Generative AI Art in Games

Tim Bender, CEO of game publisher Hooded Horse, has taken a firm stance against the use of generative AI art in game development. Bender expressed strong opposition to AI-generated assets, stating that if Hooded Horse is publishing a game, AI assets are not allowed under any circumstances. This policy includes forbidding AI art even as placeholders during game production, in part to prevent accidental inclusion in released builds. Bender called generative AI "cancerous" due to its infiltration into creative workflows without appropriate controls. The issue gains practical weight given recent incidents where placeholder AI art mistakenly appeared in live games, such as Ubisoft‚Äôs Anno 117 and the indie title Clair Obscur: Expedition 33. Witcher 3 DLC Rumors Gain Credibility

Rumors of a new DLC expansion for The Witcher 3 have strengthened based on a recent report from IGN Poland.
