Black Ops 7 Season 2: Weapon Meta and Loadouts

Season 2 of Call of Duty: Black Ops 7 has introduced powerful new weapons that are reshaping the multiplayer meta. The EGRT-17 assault rifle stands out for its combination of high fire rate and long-range damage, making it the current top choice across modes. A detailed loadout enhances its accuracy and recoil control, compensating for a slower reload speed. The weapon's unique feature includes bullet ricochet, especially with its prestige attachment that allows bullets to bounce multiple times, boosting damage potential.

Alongside the EGRT-17, the REV-46 submachine gun has impressed players with its fast fire rate and innovative folding stock mechanic, which allows quick switching between mobility and stability. Both guns have challenged the dominance of previous favorites like the Maddox RFB rifle and are anticipated to continue influencing gameplay, including the upcoming reintroduction of Blackout, a battle royale mode inspired by Black Ops 4.
