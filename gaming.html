Highguard Breaks Silence Ahead of Release

After a notably quiet period following its surprise reveal at The Game Awards 2025, the team-based hero shooter *Highguard* has announced a launch showcase for January 26, its official release day. Developer Wildlight Entertainment will provide a gameplay deep dive, year-one plans, and more during this livestream.

The initial reveal met considerable skepticism online, partly due to the lack of marketing and absence of public betas. This hesitation contributed to online cynicism, though the developers largely stayed silent amid criticism. The upcoming showcase will be the first substantial look at the game, which could address lingering questions and shape public opinion before launch.

Arknights: Endfield Offers Twitch Drops and Community Tools

*Arknights: Endfield*, an RPG blending combat and factory-building, continues to provide incentives for players through a Twitch Drops event running until February 18. Participants can earn in-game currency and items by linking their Twitch and game accounts and watching designated streams for set periods.

Additionally, the game's factory building has attracted a dedicated player base interested in optimizing production. A third-party simulator called EndfieldTools has emerged as a hub for sharing and testing factory blueprints, easing the complex task of factory management. This reflects how *Endfield* distinguishes itself from typical gacha RPGs by including substantial base-building mechanics.
