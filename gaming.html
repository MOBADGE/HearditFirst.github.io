Goblin Sushi: A New Cooking Sim About Goblin Chefs Indie developer Old Cake Factory announced *Goblin Sushi*, a cooking simulator starring a goblin who prepares sushi for other goblins. The gameplay involves combining various ingredients like rice, seaweed, fish, and dungeon creatures to serve an endless queue of sushi-loving goblin customers. Players must manage quick serving times and clean plates to keep customers happy and earn tips. The game adds whimsical and gross elements, such as special customers who enjoy unusual dishes like "poo," highlighting its quirky tone. The game emphasizes mouse controls over keyboard use and features a conveyor belt mechanics reminiscent of restaurant simulators. *Goblin Sushi* will be available in early access starting February 9, with a demo currently playable on Steam. For fans of cooking sims, it offers a playful and fast-paced experience with charming monster-themed twists.
