**Nvidia RTX 5070 Tops Steam Hardware Survey Amid Data Questions**

The February 2026 Steam Hardware Survey shows an unexpected surge in Nvidia’s RTX 5070 graphics card, jumping from 2.87% in January to 9.42%, making it the most popular GPU among Steam users. However, this sharp rise is likely influenced by regional factors rather than widespread upgrades, as such a rapid increase is unusual. The survey also notes a significant rise in users with Simplified Chinese language settings, up 30.74% to 54.60%, likely linked to China's Lunar New Year holiday period and recent Steam regional pricing changes. This influx of Chinese players, who may have newer hardware, could be skewing the data. Other Nvidia GPUs like the RTX 5060 and RTX 4060 also saw increases. Expect a return to more typical figures in future surveys.

**How to Find Advanced Electrical Components in Arc Raiders**

For players tackling the third Expedition in the action shooter Arc Raiders, acquiring Advanced Electrical Components can be tricky. These components are required resources but spawn inconsistently in the game world. The two most reliable methods to obtain them are: opening operational Arc Probes—special in-game objects that drop multiple components—and crafting them at a Level 2 Refiner. Crafting demands common materials such as Electrical Components and Wires, but upgrading the Refiner requires several other resources. If you lack a Level 2 Refiner, focusing on looting Arc Probes, which have a distinct sound and appear more frequently under certain map conditions, is your best option.

**Resident Evil Requiem’s Final Puzzle Solution Revealed**

After four days, the complex and lengthy Final Puzzle in Resident Evil Requiem has been solved by the community. The puzzle involves intricate steps including collecting a Marie doll, performing specific actions in the game’s basement—such as waiting by and interacting with a grinder while ensuring zombies are ground up—and flushing a toilet eight times to align with in-game clues. The doll must be carried through a full playthrough and into the next one to complete the puzzle by using it with a character named Emily in a particular location. The reward is 20,000 CP, a currency used for cosmetic and bonus content. While the solution is elaborate, the community effort provided a challenging puzzle experience.

**WoW’s Haranir Allied Race Faces Gear Clipping Issues**

World of Warcraft’s new allied race, the Haranir, introduced in the Midnight expansion, suffers from widespread model clipping problems with dozens of gear items. Players have reported armor pieces and helmets clipping awkwardly through the character models, particularly around the thighs and head. These issues were noted months ago during the Public Test Realm (PTR) period but have persisted into the live release. The clipping arises primarily because of the Haranir's unique body proportions compared to existing races. The continuation of these problems has raised questions about Blizzard’s quality assurance processes amid recent corporate changes, though the studio’s newly unionized QA team may offer some protection for affected employees.

March marks a busier month for PC game launches and industry events after a quiet start to 2026. These releases and events diversify the choices available to PC gamers this spring.
