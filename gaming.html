Ustwo Shifts Focus to PC-First Development After Monument Valley 3 Netflix Removal

Ustwo, the studio behind the popular Monument Valley series, has announced a strategic shift to prioritize PC-first development rather than mobile-first. This change comes after Monument Valley 3 was removed from Netflix just six months following its December 2024 launch. CEO Maria Sayans explained that being perceived primarily as a mobile studio imposed limitations on product design and market strategies, particularly on PC and console platforms. By focusing on PC as the base platform, Ustwo aims to build more direct relationships with its audience and community. This shift marks a departure from their previous approach of developing games for subscription services like Netflix and Apple Arcade before porting them elsewhere.
