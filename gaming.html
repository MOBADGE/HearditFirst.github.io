**PC Gamer Hardware Awards: Best Gaming Headsets of 2025**

The PC Gamer team has reviewed numerous gaming headsets throughout 2025 and shortlisted three standout models recognized for their audio quality, comfort, design, and features. Leading the nominations is the Fractal Design Scape, praised for its minimalist Nordic design and a wireless charging dock that uses magnetic alignment, allowing easy placement without cables. Its sound profile is neutral by default but customizable via software equalizer, delivering detailed treble and impactful bass when adjusted.

The Razer BlackShark V3 also received praise, particularly for its titanium drivers with a design focused on reducing distortion. Compared to its Pro sibling, it lacks active noise cancellation but is lighter and sports a low-latency wireless mode suited for competitive gaming. Completing the nominated trio are the EarFun Air Pro 4 earbuds. These affordable earbuds offer active noise cancellation, low latency for gaming, and a customizable sound profile, though microphone quality and touch controls are ordinary. The final winner of the headset award will be announced on New Year's Eve.

**Steam Emerges as a Strong Platform for Video Game Soundtracks**

Soundtrack releases frequently include high-quality audio formats, large collections spanning entire series, and often receive free expansions with new tracks or remixes.

Notable examples include the complete Virtua Fighter 5 soundtrack with over 530 tracks spanning nearly 18 hours, and awards-winning soundtracks like Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 which also provides free content updates.
