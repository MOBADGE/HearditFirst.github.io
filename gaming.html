Hollow Knight: Silksong Sells Over 7 Million Copies in Three Months

Team Cherry has announced that their metroidvania game, Hollow Knight: Silksong, has sold more than seven million copies since its launch a little over three months ago. This figure does not include the additional players accessing the game through Xbox Game Pass, where "millions more" have played, according to the developer. Silksong's success reflects the strong community cultivated since its announcement, noted for its art, mods, and creative gameplay strategies. While the sales number is significant, it's not the year's highest; other games like Battlefield 6 and the indie hit Peak have surpassed it in shorter time frames.

Amazon Sells In-Development MOBA March of Giants to Ubisoft

Amazon has transferred the development and publishing rights of its free-to-play MOBA *March of Giants* to Ubisoft, including the acquisition of the studio Amazon Games Montreal. The move continues Amazon's ongoing retreat from large-scale game development. The game's current status is still in closed alpha with limited community activity, and it remains to be seen how Ubisoft will develop the title. Ubisoft has expressed optimism about adding fresh content to the competitive MOBA market, aligning with its live-service game focus. Amazon will continue to publish other titles, including the new Tomb Raider games announced this year.
