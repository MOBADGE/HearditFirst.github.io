Death Howl: A Challenging Blend of Deckbuilding and Soulslike Elements

OLED Gaming Monitors See Significant Growth in 2025

OLED gaming monitors have experienced a 65% increase in shipments in 2025 compared to the previous year, according to data from Trendforce. Shipments reached about 2.62 million units in Q3 2025, marking an 84% year-on-year rise. Asus leads the market with a 21.9% share, followed by Samsung, MSI, and LG Electronics.

The wider adoption of OLED panels is notable given past concerns about burn-in and font fringing. The increase reflects more diversity in OLED applications, including productivity and gaming monitors. While Asus celebrates market leadership leveraging a broad product lineup, MSI's MPG 321URX QD-OLED remains highly regarded for gaming quality. The growth suggests OLED technology is becoming a mainstream option for gamers seeking improved contrast and color, despite some remaining technical limitations.
