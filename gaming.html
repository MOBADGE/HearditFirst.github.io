**US Immigration and Gender Policies Affect Developer Travel Plans**

Nearly one-third of non-US game developers have canceled travel plans to the United States due to concerns about immigration enforcement and restrictive gender identity policies, according to the GDC survey. This number rises to 47% among LGBTQ+ respondents. The US currently hosts many major industry events, including GDC, making this a significant issue for international professionals who face risks such as detention or discrimination. Additionally, 60% of non-US industry leaders say current US policies have affected their willingness or ability to conduct business with American companies. Although GDC organizers report no drop in ticket sales so far, these findings underline ongoing challenges around inclusivity and access within key industry hubs.
