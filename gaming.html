Steam Posts Record Revenue in December 2025

According to Alinea Analytics, Steam achieved its highest-grossing month in December 2025, generating $1.6 billion with over 100 million users active. This represents a 22.7% increase compared to December 2024 and surpasses previous records set during the pandemic in 2020.

Key contributors included the extraction shooter Arc Raiders, which sold over 1.2 million copies during the holiday period and has reached 12 million total sales. Steam's continued growth underscores its dominant position in PC game distribution despite competition from other platforms.
