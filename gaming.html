Psychological Tactics Help Players Survive Downed State in Arc Raiders

In the solo mode of the third-person shooter Arc Raiders, players often find themselves downed after intense combat. While traditionally this leaves them vulnerable, some players have found that applying psychological tactics can improve their chances of survival. Methods such as pleading for mercy by sharing personal stories or openly expressing despair have sometimes persuaded opponents to revive them or allow them to reach extraction points. Another approach is offering bribes with rare or valuable in-game items, or bluffing about having such items, to avoid being looted. This highlights the social dynamics in multiplayer shooters, where communication and player psychology can be as crucial as combat skills for survival.

Philips and AOC Unveil Gaming Monitors with Dual 1,000 Hz Modes

Philips and AOC introduced two new 27-inch monitors claiming to be the first to offer dual-mode gaming refresh rates: native 500 Hz at 1440p and an alternative 1,000 Hz at 1080p. While the exact panel technology remains unspecified, evidence points to advanced IPS variants featuring a static contrast ratio reportedly around 2,000:1. The high refresh rates aim to reduce motion blur and latency for competitive gaming, though image quality at 1,000 Hz mode may degrade due to non-integer scaling at the lower resolution and physical limitations of LCD response times. Although previous 1,000 Hz monitors have been announced, these new models add flexibility with dual resolution modes. However, the actual performance benefits and release timing remain to be seen.

Minecraft Updates Introduce Netherite Horse Armor and Spear Crafting

Minecraft players now have new crafting options thanks to recent updates from the Mounts of Mayhem drop. Netherite horse armor, stronger and more durable than previous horse armors and immune to durability loss, can be crafted by upgrading diamond horse armor at a smithing table using netherite ingots. Equipping it provides enhanced defense and knockback resistance, with additional sunlight protection for zombie horses. Alongside this, the spear weapon was added, craftable from sticks and various materials, including a netherite upgrade path. The spear features a longer attack range and two attack types: a quick jab and a charged thrust, suitable for mounted combat and different tactical approaches. These additions enhance both utility and combat variety for players.

Nautilus Underwater Mount Added in Minecraft

The new Nautilus mob introduced in Minecraft's latest update offers players an aquatic mount that increases underwater travel speed. Nautilus spawn in all ocean biomes below a certain depth and can be tamed by feeding them pufferfish or bucketed pufferfish. Once tamed, players can saddle the Nautilus, which grants a temporary breathing effect allowing for longer underwater excursions before resurfacing. Breeding is possible using any fish variant to sustain or grow the population. Notably, Nautilus cannot leave the water, emphasizing its role as an aquatic-only mount that encourages exploration of the ocean parts of the game world.
