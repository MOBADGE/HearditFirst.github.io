Limited Run Games Addresses Delivery Delays Amid Leadership Change

Limited Run Games, a physical games publisher owned by the Embracer Group, has recently acknowledged longstanding issues with delayed shipments and delivery uncertainties. This comes in the wake of former CEO Josh Fairhurst’s departure and new management taking over. The company sent a mass email to customers with outstanding preorders, admitting that previous delivery estimates were overly optimistic and caused disappointment. The message indicated a commitment to setting more realistic release schedules moving forward as part of an effort to increase transparency and regain customer trust. This marks an important step for Limited Run, which has faced criticism over delays and quality control for years. How effectively the new leadership can improve customer experience remains to be seen.

The Super Mario Galaxy Movie Becomes a Top 10 Video Game Film at Box Office

The Super Mario Galaxy Movie, released on April 1, 2026, has earned $372.5 million worldwide in its first five days, placing it as the ninth highest-grossing video game movie of all time. The film made $190.1 million domestically and $182.4 million internationally. Although slightly behind the 2023 Super Mario Bros. Movie’s opening numbers, it is notable for making the Mario animated franchise the only one to have two films opening with over $350 million each. This performance confirms the continued commercial appeal of video game-inspired movies, even though critical reception has been mixed. Nintendo also promoted audience engagement by encouraging moviegoers to scan official posters with the Nintendo Today app for exclusive digital content.

New Zelda Guide Book on Tears of the Kingdom Now Available for Preorder

Dark Horse has opened preorders for *The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom – Secrets of the Zonai*, a comprehensive hardcover book focused on the lore and development behind the popular 2023 Nintendo game. The book includes over 350 pages of official artwork, concept sketches, storyboards, and developer insights. Two editions are offered: a standard hardcover at $60 and a $120 Hero Edition that adds collectible extras like a slipcase, a world map, an art print, and a Zelda Tear replica. Scheduled for release in October, this guide adds to a series of Zelda books by Dark Horse that document the franchise’s history and art, appealing to fans seeking deeper context and collectible materials.

Fight Club 4K Blu-ray Steelbook Edition Announced

A new 4K steelbook edition of David Fincher’s 1999 film *Fight Club* is available for preorder, priced at $40 and shipping beginning May 12. This edition features a restored 4K transfer approved by Fincher, HDR10 support, and a DTS-HD Master Audio 5.1 soundtrack. The two-disc set comes with extensive special features, including multiple commentary tracks involving the director and cast, deleted scenes, behind-the-scenes featurettes, and interactive elements. The steelbook’s cover art is minimalist, featuring a bar of soap, with an interior jacket highlighting a key scene from the film. Fans of the cult classic now have a high-quality physical release offering a detailed look at the movie’s production.

Sales on Kindle Devices at Woot Highlight e-Reader Deals

Woot is currently offering significant discounts on various Amazon Kindle models for a limited time. These deals provide an opportunity for consumers to acquire e-readers at reduced prices, although availability may be limited as some models have already sold out.
