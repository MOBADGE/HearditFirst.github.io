Prominent Fighting Game Commentator Sajam Steps Away from EVO

Sajam, a well-known commentator in the fighting game community (FGC), announced he will no longer attend EVO events following the buyout of the tournament by the Saudi Arabian government. He cited concerns over the use of the event for sportswashing and the community-driven nature of the FGC being undermined by corporate ownership. Sajam acknowledged the financial impact of his decision but emphasized his personal principles. He also criticized major fighting game developers for accepting the buyout with little resistance. This move highlights ongoing tensions within esports regarding ownership, ethics, and community values amid growing international investments.

Remembering Vince Zampella

The games industry is mourning the loss of Vince Zampella, co-founder of Infinity Ward and Respawn, who died in a car crash. Zampella played a major role in shaping modern first-person shooters, notably through the *Call of Duty* franchise and later hits like *Titanfall* and *Apex Legends*. Tributes from colleagues and industry figures emphasize his leadership, respect for his teams, and lasting impact on the industry. His passing marks the loss of a highly influential and well-regarded developer whose work has shaped gaming experiences for millions.
