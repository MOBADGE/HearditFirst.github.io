**Unreal Tournament 2004 Released Free with First Patch in 20 Years**

*Unreal Tournament 2004* is now officially available as a free download, restored and updated by the dedicated fan project OldUnreal with the blessing of Epic Games. This marks the first public patch for the game in over two decades. The patch modernizes the title for Windows, Linux, and macOS, restoring compatibility with current systems and introducing a new SDL backend and a new renderer. Because of licensing, OldUnreal hosts only the installer that fetches the game files from the Internet Archive. The patch includes various fixes and improvements but notes some new bugs might be present. *Unreal Tournament 2004* is widely regarded as a high point of the series, combining improved visuals with a diverse set of game modes, including the vehicle-based Onslaught and the returning Assault mode. While multiplayer activity remains uncertain given the game's age, the new release may revive interest, especially with its capable AI bots.
