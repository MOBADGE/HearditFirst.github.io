Sovereign Tea Now Free to Play

Sovereign Tea, a turn-based tactics game themed around an ongoing war between tea and coffee factions, is now available for free on Steam. The game features strategic brewing mechanics with 12 unique Tea Princesses and over 40 customizable minions, each with different stats and abilities. Originally launched in 2021 as a paid title, the developers have decided to remove the price permanently. According to the developer 3 Halves, they have "made enough money from the game" and now want to broaden its audience by making it accessible to everyone.

The decision to make Sovereign Tea free is significant as it reflects a shift in how indie developers may handle mature projects, focusing on community growth and accessibility rather than continuing revenue from initial sales. Players interested in tactical strategy games with a unique theme might find this a good opportunity to try something new without cost.
