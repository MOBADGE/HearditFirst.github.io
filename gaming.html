Skyrim Mod Adds Bush-Hiding Stealth Mechanic

A new mod for Skyrim Special Edition called "Hide in Bushes" introduces the ability for players to become 50% harder to detect when crouching in bushes, with reduced bonuses near trees and stumps. This addition leverages the previously released Object Impact Framework mod, which enables increased interactivity with game world objects that were once static. Although grass remains unaffected due to being a texture rather than an object, this mod offers players enhanced stealth options that align with popular playstyles in Skyrim. It highlights how modders continue to expand gameplay possibilities in this decade-old title.
