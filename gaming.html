Highguard Breaks Silence With Launch Showcase Announcement

After months of silence following its surprise reveal at the 2025 Game Awards, the team-based shooter *Highguard* has announced a launch showcase for January 26, the same day the game is scheduled to release on Steam. Arknights: Endfield Twitch Drops Offer Free Rewards; Factory Building Gains Popularity

*Arknights: Endfield* is extending its launch support with an ongoing Twitch Drops event through February 18. Players can earn in-game currency and items, such as Oroberyl, Combat Records, and T-Creds, by linking their Twitch and game accounts and watching drop-enabled streams. However, players need to complete the tutorial and select their character before they can link accounts and claim rewards.

Meanwhile, the game's factory building aspect is gaining unexpected attention. Despite beginning as an anime-style RPG with combat, many players have embraced the robust factory management features. This shift in focus has inspired community tools like EndfieldTools, a blueprint simulator that helps players plan and share optimized factory layouts without in-game resource costs. The tool has seen heavy demand, reflecting the growing interest in the game's industrial side.

*Why it matters:* The Twitch Drops provide free resources that help new players progress, encouraging engagement. The emerging factory-building community adds depth to the game‚Äôs appeal beyond typical gacha mechanics, potentially influencing its longevity and player retention.
