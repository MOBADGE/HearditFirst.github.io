How to Find Candleberries in Arc Raiders

Players of the cooperative shooter **Arc Raiders** may find it challenging to collect Candleberries, a key forageable item used for crafting and event progression. There are two primary methods to obtain them:

1. **Gather from bushes:** Candleberries appear on green bushes with red berries during the Cold Snap map condition. Recommended harvest areas include the swamp region in Dam Battlegrounds and the west side near the Blue Gate. 2. **Earn via the Gathering Candleberries reward track:** Accumulate merits by playing during the Candleberry event, which can be redeemed for Candleberries. Players should manage exposure to cold environments since staying outside too long can be dangerous in-game.
