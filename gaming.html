**Western Digital Unveils Hard Drive Tech With Flash-Like Speeds** Western Digital has announced new hard drive technologies aimed at significantly increasing HDD throughput to levels approaching those of flash storage. This can raise data throughput from around 300 MB/s to roughly 1.2 GB/s, with future designs potentially reaching near 4.8 GB/s.
