Asus TUF Gaming A16 (2025) Review: Budget Gaming with Strong CPU but Modest GPU

The Asus TUF Gaming A16 (2025) is an entry-level gaming laptop featuring an AMD Ryzen 7 260 CPU with eight Zen 4 cores and a capable boost up to 5.1 GHz. It pairs this with an Nvidia GeForce RTX 5050 GPU, 16 GB DDR5 RAM, and a 1 TB SSD, priced around ¬£1,400. While the RTX 5050 is not as powerful as higher-tier GPUs like the 5060 or 5070, it holds its own reasonably well, competing with similar laptops on benchmarks and offering decent gaming performance at 1920x1200 resolution and 165Hz refresh rates. The device runs cooler and quieter than some competitors but is relatively large and heavy, better suited for desktop use. Battery life ranges from nearly eight hours on light use to just over an hour during gaming. While it offers solid build quality and a well-rounded port selection, the review recommends waiting for discounts, given some cheaper 5060-equipped alternatives on the market.
