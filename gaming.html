Bungie Takes a Firm Stand Against Cheating in Marathon

Following recent issues with cheating and exploitation in games like *Arc Raiders*, Bungie has announced a strict anti-cheating policy for its upcoming shooter *Marathon*. In a detailed blog post, Bungie emphasized its commitment to fair gameplay by introducing dedicated servers with authoritative control to prevent common exploits such as teleportation, unlimited ammo, or damage avoidance. The game will use a Fog of War system to limit map information exposure, addressing issues like wall hacks.

Importantly, Bungie states that anyone caught cheating in *Marathon* will face a permanent ban with no chance of reinstatement. This zero-tolerance position reflects lessons learned from other extraction shooters, particularly in combating economic exploits like item duplication that can break game balance. Bungie's approach signals a stronger focus on protecting both competitive integrity and player investment in their progression.
