Luto: A Thoughtful Horror Experience Exploring Grief

Luto, a 2025 psychological horror game by Broken Bird Games, offers a unique blend of narrative and atmosphere inspired by classics like P.T. and The Stanley Parable. Players control Sam, trapped in a house that resets the day repeatedly, preventing escape and deepening a sense of despair. The game uses environmental storytelling and an omnipresent narrator to explore themes of grief, loss, and mental exhaustion. Though only a few hours long, Luto evokes strong emotional responses by simulating the disorienting experience of unbearable absence and self-imposed confinement. Its subtle approach to horror and existential questioning makes it stand out among recent horror titles. Luto is available now on Steam.
