Crimson Desert Shows Early Playability on Intel Arc GPUs

A recent Intel driver update (version 32.0.101.8629 WHQL) has unexpectedly enabled the game *Crimson Desert* to boot on Intel Arc graphics cards, which previously did not support the game. Players report mixed experiences: some can start and play the game, while others encounter crashes or significant visual bugs, such as missing character faces and limbs. Testing confirmed severe graphical glitches and incomplete rendering issues remain. Developer Pearl Abyss has acknowledged ongoing work to support Intel Arc GPUs but recommends waiting for an official update before expecting stable gameplay. This partial functionality represents a step forward, but the game is not yet fully compatible with Intel Arc hardware.

Over the Hill Hits 1 Million Steam Wishlists; Closed Playtest Soon

The off-road exploration game *Over the Hill* has reached over one million wishlists on Steam despite having no set release date beyond the broad 2026 window. Developed by Funselektor and Strelka Games, the title focuses on peaceful exploration of rugged landscapes, drawing comparisons to games like *Mudrunner* with its environmental challenges and vehicle tools. To celebrate this milestone, the developers will hold a closed playtest from April 24-26, with registration open until April 16. Players interested in early access can sign up to give feedback ahead of launch. The game promises solo and cooperative gameplay across diverse terrains, starting on PC with console versions planned later.

New Don't Starve Game Announced: Don't Starve Elsewhere

Klei Entertainment revealed *Don't Starve Elsewhere* at the 2026 Triple-I Initiative Showcase. This new entry in the survival crafting series builds upon the multiplayer foundation of *Don't Starve Together* with expanded verticality and new creatures. Familiar characters such as Wilson and Wendy return, and the game features fluid movement mechanics like gap jumping. Though maintaining the core survival and base-building gameplay fans expect, the update refines exploration and environmental interaction. The title is now available for wishlisting on Steam, but no release date was provided.

Temtem: Pioneers Introduces Open-World Survival Crafting

Crema Games, known for *Temtem*, announced *Temtem: Pioneers*, an open-world survival crafting game blending creature collection with base building and exploration. Drawing inspiration from games like *Palworld*, it emphasizes resource gathering, crafting, and using creatures’ abilities to navigate biomes and defend bases. Unlike *Palworld*, it appears to avoid controversial features such as guns or harvesting creatures for resources. The game is in early development, with a Kickstarter campaign launched to involve the community in development. Tempered expectations remain, as details on gameplay systems and release timing are currently limited.

Valve’s SteamGPT AI Referenced in Code, Possibly for Support and Anti-Cheat

Leaks from Steam’s source code suggest that Valve is developing an AI system named "SteamGPT." References indicate it may assist with internal tasks such as customer support and account security, including anti-cheat functions linked to Valve’s Trust systems. While some speculate SteamGPT could become a chatbot for user interactions, current evidence points to tools for backend use rather than direct consumer-facing AI. Employing AI to improve support and cheat detection could help manage Steam’s considerable workload, but it raises questions about data access and privacy in anti-cheat monitoring. Valve has not formally confirmed details.

Fortnite Teases Upcoming Toy Story Collaboration

*Fortnite* has teased a new crossover event featuring *Toy Story* themes, showing Buzz Lightyear arriving at Pizza Planet in a short trailer. Fans have noted the absence of Woody in the initial reveal, prompting speculation about which characters will appear. Additionally, there is curiosity about whether players will be able to control the iconic green aliens, which remain unconfirmed. The collaboration’s scale and character sizes (human-sized vs. toy-sized) have also been topics of discussion. No release date or full roster has been announced yet.
