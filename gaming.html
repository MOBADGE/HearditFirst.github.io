**GTA 6 Delay Rumor Clarified**

Recently, speculation about a third delay for Grand Theft Auto 6 emerged online, causing anxiety among fans and a surge in related bets on prediction markets. The confusion began when Bloomberg journalist Jason Schreier mentioned on a podcast that GTA 6 might follow a delay pattern similar to Red Dead Redemption 2, which faced two delays. Schreier noted the game is "still not content complete," but he did not confirm any additional delays beyond the two already announced, nor definitively predict a missed November 19, 2026 release date. Despite this, news outlets and social media amplified the conversation into what appeared to be a confirmed third delay. Schreier himself called the resulting hype "stupid," emphasizing that no new official information has been released. The incident highlights how minor insider comments can quickly be misinterpreted and amplified in highly anticipated game releases.
