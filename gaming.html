Concerns Over Increasing AI Usage in Games

An opinion piece emphasizes growing apprehension about the escalating use of AI in video game development. The author criticizes studios for quietly integrating AI-generated assets, such as character portraits and voice lines, to cut costs. They argue that while some uses, like in the game *Arc Raiders*, involve contracted voice actors, many cases lead to a loss of human creativity and jobs. The author calls on the community to actively challenge and critique egregious AI usage, warning that unchecked adoption could make games less engaging and more homogenized. This discussion underscores the direction the industry may take in balancing technological tools with maintaining the human element in game artistry and production.
