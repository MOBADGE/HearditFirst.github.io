Microsoft CEO Highlights Need for Practical AI Use

Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella addressed concerns about the social acceptance of AI during the World Economic Forum, emphasizing that AI must produce tangible benefits to maintain public support. Nadella warned that using scarce resources like energy without delivering measurable improvements in areas such as healthcare, education, and industry competitiveness could result in loss of what he termed "social permission" to continue AI development at current energy costs. He encouraged widespread adoption of AI skills by workers, framing it as essential for real economic impact. However, he acknowledged industry challenges, such as the high costs of hardware and the limited returns many companies have experienced so far.
