Story of Seasons: Grand Bazaar ‚Äì A Fresh Take on Farming Sims

2025 saw the release of *Story of Seasons: Grand Bazaar*, which offered a different approach to farming simulators compared to titles like *Stardew Valley*. The game avoids common design pitfalls seen in previous *Story of Seasons* remakes, presenting a visually improved world with well-developed characters and engaging dialogue. This approach fosters a stronger connection to Zephyr Town and its inhabitants, encouraging players to participate in various seasonal events and festivals, which help deepen relationships with townsfolk. A weekly bazaar system guides gameplay with clear goals to maximize earnings, making the routine of farming feel purposeful and rewarding. Unique features like bug-catching add variety to player activities. Overall, *Grand Bazaar* demonstrates that farming sims can remain enjoyable without drastically reinventing the wheel.

The PC Gamer Hardware Awards have nominated several notable SSDs and monitors for best-in-category honors this year.

For SSDs, the nominees represent a diverse selection in form factor, capacity, and price. The *Crucial P510* offers PCIe 5.0 performance at a relatively budget-friendly price, reaching speeds up to 11,000 MB/s read and 9,000 MB/s write. The *Sandisk Extreme Pro* stands out as an external SSD delivering sustained USB4 speeds, providing flexibility for users juggling large game libraries across devices. In the monitor category, 4K and high-refresh-rate panels remain strong contenders amid rising prices for PC components. The *Philips Evnia 27M2N3800A* offers a versatile switch between 4K at 160 Hz and 1080p at 320 Hz, balancing price and performance. The premium *Alienware 27 AW2725Q QD-OLED* combines a 27-inch 4th Gen QD-OLED panel with a fast 240 Hz refresh rate and competitive stats, at a cost undercutting many competitors.
