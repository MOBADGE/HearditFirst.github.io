New GTA 5 Survival Mod Introduces Radiation Hazards

A new survival mod called "The Exclusion Zone" has been released for Grand Theft Auto 5, transforming parts of the game's map into irradiated zones. The mod introduces radiation poisoning mechanics with gradual health effects and organ failure if exposure is prolonged. Players must locate gas masks to survive high-radiation areas such as Humane Labs, Fort Zancudo Delta, and parts of the airport perimeter. The creator plans to add configuration options soon, including the ability to overlay radiation zones onto Pripyat-themed GTA 5 maps inspired by the Stalker series. This mod adds a new survival challenge element through a hazardous environment.
