**Pope Leo XIV Criticizes Priests Using AI for Sermons**

The Pope described AI as turning people into "passive consumers of unthought thoughts," emphasizing the importance of human reflection and spiritual connection in ministry. This statement is part of his ongoing caution against AI, which he previously called "an empty, cold shell" that threatens human dignity and justice.

**Logitech G325 Lightspeed: A Budget-Friendly Lightweight Headset**

Logitech has released the G325 Lightspeed, an $80 wireless gaming headset aimed at users seeking a lightweight, affordable option. The headset offers a flexible design, dual connectivity via 2.4 GHz dongle and Bluetooth 5.3, and 24 hours of battery life at moderate volume. Its built-in microphone provides serviceable quality for gaming, though it lacks a dedicated boom mic, which limits voice clarity. Sound quality is generally neutral and sufficient for casual gaming but does not deliver strong bass or immersive noise isolation.

*Why it matters:* The G325 serves as a practical choice for gamers needing a portable headset for both gaming and daily use without investing in premium hardware. Its compromises on sound and microphone quality reflect typical budget headset trade-offs but may suit users prioritizing comfort and convenience.
