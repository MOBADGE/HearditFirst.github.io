Monster Hunter Stories 3: How to Defeat Invasive Monsters and Where to Find Endangered Species

Monster Hunter Stories 3 centers on restoring the environment by dealing with Invasive Monsters—powered-up versions of regular creatures guarding endangered species' nests. These monsters require specific strategies to repel rather than simply kill, enabling players to collect rare eggs and advance in the game.

- **Invasive Monster Tactics:** For example, the Invasive Yian Garuga, encountered early in the eastern Azuria region, can be driven away by breaking its legs, with blunt weapons like hammers recommended. Other monsters need different approaches: the Invasive Plesioth’s head must be broken with fire attacks to prevent a devastating move; the Invasive Shogun Ceanataur requires raising a defense buff to survive claw attacks; and the Invasive Nerscylla demands a gunlance’s precise counterattack to its Death Scissors move.

- **Endangered Species Locations:** These special monsters are found across three main regions—Azuria, Canalta Timberland, and Serathis—each with its own set of Invasive Monster guardians. Notable endangered species include Rathian in Azuria, Canyne in Canalta Timberland, and Barioth in Serathis. Securing their eggs allows players to add powerful monsties to their team and complete related side quests.

Understanding these strategies is important for progressing in Monster Hunter Stories 3, as confronting Invasive Monsters is tied to key narrative and gameplay milestones.

Darktide’s New “Expeditions” Mode Introduces Outdoor Extraction Gameplay

The upcoming Beyond the Hive update for Warhammer 40,000: Darktide introduces a new gameplay mode called "expeditions," shifting from the series’ usual claustrophobic environments to open, outdoor scavenge zones on Atoma Prime.

- **Gameplay Changes:** Players must gather valuable Tech-Remnants and exit before time or health runs out. Unlike typical extraction shooters, this mode omits PvP but adds challenges such as roaming enemy patrols and environmental hazards like radiation that limits how long players can stay in one area.

- **Randomization and Strategy:** Although not fully procedurally generated, expeditions feature a high degree of randomness in layout and enemy spawns, encouraging tactical play to avoid overwhelming forces.

This mode marks a notable shift for Darktide, offering more open-area gameplay and new risks that require different player strategies.

The Simpsons Executive Producer Hints at Possible Hit & Run Revival

Matt Selman, executive producer of The Simpsons, commented in a recent interview that a revival of the 2003 game *The Simpsons: Hit & Run* is not out of the question.

- **Background:** Hit & Run is widely regarded as one of the better Simpsons games, known for its action-adventure gameplay and humor. Selman expressed surprise at the game’s enduring cult following and stated, “never say never” regarding a potential remaster or sequel.

- **Current Status:** No concrete plans are confirmed, but the acknowledgment reflects ongoing interest in making the game more accessible, considering it has not been readily available on digital storefronts.

For fans of the series, this tentative openness offers hope for renewed access to the title.

Open-City Game *Samson* from Just Cause Creator Faces Skepticism After Latest Trailer

*Samson*, a new open-city game by Liquid Swords—founded by Just Cause creator Cristofer Sundberg—is set to launch April 8. It combines driving and brawling within a smaller, focused urban environment designed around a debt-repayment narrative.

- **Design Intentions:** Unlike large-scale open-world games, Samson emphasizes a tighter gameplay loop with daily objectives and an “action points” system restricting mission choices, encouraging rapid decisions and chaotic encounters.

- **Trailer Reception:** While the driving mechanics appear solid, the melee combat shown in the latest trailer drew criticism for repetitiveness and lack of depth. Environmental detail was noted, but sparse population and limited shown mission variety raised concerns about overall game vitality.

The game’s approach is distinct, but the mixed trailer response suggests some features may need refinement before release.
