SK Hynix Reports Record Profits Driven by AI Demand

Memory chip manufacturer SK Hynix announced its best financial year in 2025, with revenues reaching approximately $67 billion and profits hitting $33 billion. This marks a 47% increase in revenues and more than double profits compared to 2024. The company attributes much of this growth to surging demand for AI-related hardware.

SK Hynix is expanding production of high-bandwidth memory (HBM4) for AI servers and data centers, as well as advanced DRAM and NAND flash technologies aimed at AI workloads. While the firm focused heavily on AI markets, it notably did not comment on consumer PC hardware. The company also declared a significant special dividend payment to shareholders.
