**Void War Receives DMCA Notice from Games Workshop**

The issue arose due to the game's visual similarities to Warhammer 40,000, specifically citing "oversized convex shoulder pads with a metallic rim" featured in one of the trailers. Although all the artwork was original, Games Workshop contended that the imagery could mislead players into associating the game with their brand. After removing the contested trailer, Void War was reinstated on Steam. This situation highlights ongoing challenges around intellectual property and inspiration within game design, especially when visual elements closely reference established franchises.
