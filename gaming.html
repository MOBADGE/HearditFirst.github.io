Themed Chess Sets Inspired by Popular Franchises

Chess has seen a recent resurgence as a tabletop hobby, with several themed chess sets on offer for fans looking for something beyond the traditional board. The Noble Collection, a company specializing in officially licensed merchandise, has released a variety of sets based on well-known franchises such as Batman, Lord of the Rings, Minecraft, Jurassic Park, Star Trek, Harry Potter, Minions, and Wicked. These sets typically include high-quality pieces and visually striking boards that reflect the themes and characters of their source materials. Prices generally range from around $40 to $130 depending on the set. This variety allows chess enthusiasts and collectors to enjoy the game with a personal touch linked to their favorite fictional universes.

Discounted Mini Sonic the Hedgehog Arcade Machine

A compact Sonic the Hedgehog-themed arcade cabinet by My Arcade is now available at a reduced price on Amazon. Once priced around $120, the mini arcade machine currently sells for about $86 for a limited time. The device features a 3.5-inch color screen, authentic Sega branding, and joystick controls, and it includes four preloaded Sonic games such as Sonic the Hedgehog and Sonic 2. Its rechargeable battery offers about five hours of gameplay. This product appeals to fans of retro gaming and Sonic enthusiasts who want a physical arcade experience on a smaller scale and at a lower cost than full-sized machines.

Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds Switch 2 Version Details

Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds is set to release on the Nintendo Switch 2 on March 26, with preorders open. This physical edition will include the original game data along with an upgrade patch optimized for the new hardware. The game offers 23 characters and 45 vehicles, featuring extensive customization options and a mix of solo and multiplayer modes, including online races with up to 12 players. A bonus Werehog cosmetic pack is included with the purchase but requires internet download. This release makes the game more accessible for Switch 2 users who prefer physical copies and improved performance.

Dead by Daylight Invites Fans to Shape New Content

Behaviour Interactive has started a fan voting event for its popular horror multiplayer game Dead by Daylight. Players can participate by choosing the appearance of a new hero and the form of an upcoming killer in the game's next chapter, The Grimoire. This approach of crowd-sourcing creative decisions is a way to engage the player base directly and create content that reflects community preferences.
