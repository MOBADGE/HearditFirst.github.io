Fractal Design Scape Named Best Gaming Headset of 2025

The Fractal Design Scape headset impressed reviewers with its refined design, comfort, and innovative charging stand featuring Qi wireless charging. Its matte black finish, well-designed controls, and a plush fabric interior distinguish it from other gaming headsets. Although it lacks noise cancellation and has an average microphone, its standout feature is the magnetic charging dock, encouraging users to keep it charged and ready effortlessly. Audio quality is balanced and detailed, suited for long listening sessions. Priced at around $200, it serves as a significant step in wireless headset design and is considered by some reviewers as the best gaming headset tested in 2025. This signifies a shift toward more thoughtful, user-friendly peripheral design in the gaming hardware market.
