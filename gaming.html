Baldur's Gate Mod Management Tool Overhauled

A popular web-based mod management tool for the original Baldur’s Gate games, EET Mod Forge, recently received a major update. The 2.0 version adds features such as NPC portrait previews to help users avoid conflicts when multiple mods alter the same assets. It also tracks in-game caps for certain mod categories, warning players if they exceed limits. A new preset modlist includes 475 mods with careful conflict resolution. This update simplifies modding for long-time fans and newcomers, helping prevent game-breaking issues in heavily modded installs. The sustained modding community shows continued interest in these classic RPGs.

Bethesda Adjusts DLC Integration Strategy Following Past Feedback

Bethesda reflected on earlier approaches to downloadable content (DLC) in their RPGs. Fallout 3’s Game of the Year Edition prominently displayed numerous pop-ups at launch indicating new DLC content, which was met with mixed reactions. Istvan Pely, who worked on many Fallout and Elder Scrolls titles, noted that the studio has improved how expansions are introduced. For the upcoming Starfield expansion, Terran Armada, DLC content will be integrated more organically into the game world, allowing players to discover it in a less intrusive manner. The DLC and a related update are set for release on April 7.

Resident Evil Requiem Gets Unconventional Vehicle Mod

In Resident Evil Requiem, one of the product placements includes Leon Kennedy’s Porsche Cayenne Turbo GT. Modder SinderyMonk created the "Patty Wagon" mod, replacing the Porsche with a drivable hamburger inspired by SpongeBob SquarePants. This is part of a growing trend of humorous mods for the game, including character swaps like Shrek and Thomas the Tank Engine replacing enemies. Such mods require the REFramework tool to load but add levity and customization options for players looking beyond the base game experience.

Elden Ring Cut Content Restored Through Mod

A cut NPC questline for Merchant Kalé, a character in Elden Ring, has been restored via a fan mod by Transquility. Originally, this quest tied into the game’s "Frenzied Flame" ending and provided deeper context on the game’s darker story themes. The mod recreates the full quest with datamined voice acting, allowing players to experience content omitted from the final release. Mods like this demonstrate the ongoing engagement with Elden Ring’s narrative layers and the community’s role in preserving unshared content.

Minecraft Announces New Underground Biome and Dungeons Sequel

Minecraft's upcoming update "Chaos Cubed," slated for a later 2026 release, introduces the Sulfur Caves—a new underground biome distinguished by poisonous pools and unique environmental hazards. The biome offers new blocks like cinnabar and sulfur, inspired by real sulfur hot springs, enriching cave exploration. Additionally, Mojang announced a sequel to Minecraft Dungeons, their Diablo-inspired spin-off, expected to launch later this year. The new title will continue the franchise’s lighthearted dungeon crawling, although gameplay details remain scarce.
