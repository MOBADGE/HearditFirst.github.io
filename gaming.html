Fallout Co-Creator Tim Cain Offers Definition for Immersive Sim Genre

Tim Cain, one of the original creators of Fallout, shared his thoughts on defining "immersive simulation" games, a term often used without consensus. Cain emphasizes three core elements: a consistent set of world rules, interaction of those rules producing emergent gameplay systems, and universal application of those rules to the player and environment. He suggests immersive sims share key design goals with good role-playing games, and that the genre exists on a spectrum rather than a fixed category. His clarification adds a thoughtful perspective to ongoing debates around the genre, providing a framework useful for both developers and players to discuss design approaches and game qualities.
