Crimson Desert Launch Issues Affect Pre-Order and Deluxe Content

Crimson Desert launched recently on Steam, attracting a peak of over 239,000 concurrent players. Despite this, many players have encountered a bug that prevents them from accessing the pre-order bonus and deluxe pack items. These rewards, including unique shields, horse armor, and the Kairos armor set, are currently unavailable in the in-game supply box. Developer Pearl Abyss has acknowledged the problem and is actively working on a fix. While the game’s initial player count is solid, it remains far from blockbuster success levels seen in similar open-world titles. The issue matters as it affects early customer satisfaction and sets expectations around content delivery for a new IP.

Fallout: New Vegas Now Available for 99 Cents During Steam Sale

Steam’s ongoing Spring Sale offers Fallout: New Vegas at a heavily discounted price of 99 cents. This Obsidian-developed RPG, often regarded as the best in the Fallout series for its intricate story and impactful choices, is gaining renewed attention partly due to the recent Fallout TV series featuring the New Vegas setting. The sale provides a low-cost entry point for new players and may encourage players to revisit the game with modern mods that improve stability and visuals, enhancing its playability on current hardware like the Steam Deck. The price drop highlights how older but well-regarded games continue to find an audience through periodic discounts.

Early Concern About PC Market Saturation Dates Back to 1989

An archival recording from the 1989 Computer Game Developers Conference reveals that Robert Garriott, co-founder of Origin Systems and brother to Ultima creator Richard Garriott, voiced concerns about the PC gaming market being oversaturated even 37 years ago. He pointed out that although computer game sales grew annually, the number of new titles increased at a faster rate, causing average sales per game to decline. Garriott also noted that consumer confusion and inconsistent game quality contributed to a market slump, rejecting the then-common notion that Nintendo alone was causing the downturn. His advice to focus on unique, high-quality PC games remains relevant today amid ongoing debates about discoverability and game quality in an oversaturated market.

Hoarder: A Darker Take on the Cleaning Simulator Genre

Hoarder, developed by one of Darkwood’s original creators, is an upcoming game blending simulation with dark horror elements. Unlike typical cleaning simulators, it places players in a cluttered, abandoned house where tidying up reveals increasingly ominous and surreal events, including a basement with impossible geometry. This creates a tone that combines methodical gameplay with unsettling atmosphere and occasional scares. Though it does not yet have a release date, Hoarder represents a niche within indie horror that favors psychological tension over jump scares, following the footsteps of titles like Darkwood and Iron Lung. It may appeal to players interested in unconventional survival horror experiences.

Seven Deadly Sins: Origin Offers Multiple Codes for In-Game Rewards

Netmarble’s free-to-play gacha RPG Seven Deadly Sins: Origin, set in the popular manga universe, currently has several active redeemable codes providing players with rewards such as hero draw tickets, gold, enhancement stones, and cube keys. The codes can be used either in-game after completing early tutorial quests or via Netmarble’s official website. These codes support early player progression and engagement, common practice in gacha games to maintain player retention and incentivize logging in. Players should redeem these codes promptly since expiration dates for some remain unspecified.

Strategies to Defeat Elder Dragons in Monster Hunter Stories 3

Monster Hunter Stories 3 features powerful Calamitous Elder Dragons that serve as challenging boss encounters, each with unique weaknesses and combat phases. Notable Elder Dragons include Namielle, Yama Tsukami, Velkhana, and the dual boss Wind Serpent Ibushi and Thunder Serpent Narwa. Players are advised to prepare with specific Monsties that exploit elemental and ailment vulnerabilities, equip gear that boosts relevant resistances, and master timing of attacks to win key Head-to-Head moments. These fights are critical for acquiring top-tier gear and progressing. Understanding each dragon’s mechanics and appropriate counters is essential for success.
