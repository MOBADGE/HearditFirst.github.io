Crimson Desert Launch Faces Pre-Order Bonus Issues

Crimson Desert launched on March 20, 2026, reaching a peak of over 239,000 concurrent players on Steam. However, some players have encountered a bug that prevents them from accessing pre-order and deluxe edition bonus items through the in-game supply box. Pearl Abyss, the developer, acknowledged the issue and is actively working on a fix. The bonuses include special shields, horse armor, and the Kairos armor set. Although Crimson Desert’s launch performance is solid, it falls short compared to other recent hits like Resident Evil Requiem and Black Myth: Wukong. The game appeals mainly to fans of large open-world action RPGs but has drawn mixed reactions due to its complexity and pacing.

Fallout: New Vegas Available for 99 Cents During Steam Sale

As part of the Steam Spring Sale, Fallout: New Vegas is currently priced at 99 cents, making it an accessible option for players interested in one of the most critically regarded RPGs by Obsidian Entertainment. The sale also includes discounts on other Bethesda and Fallout titles. New Vegas is praised for its rich storytelling, consequential choices, and a blend of humor and darkness that sets it apart from Fallout 3 and 4. PC Gamer highlights mods and guides that can improve the experience for modern players. The game's renewed interest is partly tied to plot elements featured in the recent season of the Fallout TV series.

Insights from Ultima Co-Creator on Early PC Game Market Challenges

Robert Garriott, co-founder of Origin Systems and brother of Ultima creator Richard Garriott, addressed industry challenges at the 1989 Computer Game Developers Conference. He noted concerns about an oversaturated PC game market even back then, which led to declining sales per title despite overall growth in the industry. Garriott identified product proliferation and quality inconsistency as major issues confusing consumers. He argued for developing unique, high-quality titles that leveraged the PC’s capabilities beyond consoles like Nintendo. This perspective from over three decades ago reflects ongoing industry dynamics where quality and differentiation remain crucial amidst large numbers of game releases.

Hoarder: A Dark Cleaning Simulator from a Darkwood Co-Creator

One of the original creators of the survival horror game Darkwood is developing Hoarder, a new game described as a dark, atmospheric cleaning simulator. Unlike light-hearted cleaning sims, Hoarder tasks players with decluttering and exploring a hoarder’s abandoned house, gradually revealing a mysterious and unsettling basement. The game mixes mechanical tasks with exploration and horror elements, stripping away guidance as the player progresses. Hoarder currently has no release date but is available for wishlist on Steam. This project continues the trend of indie developers blending genre boundaries for novel gameplay experiences.

Seven Deadly Sins: Origin Offers New Codes for In-Game Rewards

Players of Netmarble’s anime-inspired Genshin Impact-like gacha RPG, Seven Deadly Sins: Origin, have access to various redeemable codes giving bonus items such as hero draw tickets, gold, enhancement stones, and cube keys. Codes like "ORIGINSTEAM" and "WELCOMEORIGIN" can be redeemed via the in-game menu or Netmarble’s official website after completing initial tutorial quests. These rewards assist player progression in the gacha system. No expired codes are currently reported, but players are advised to redeem active codes promptly as some have unspecified expiration dates.

Strategies for Defeating Elder Dragons in Monster Hunter Stories 3

Monster Hunter Stories 3 features challenging Calamitous Elder Dragons as endgame bosses. These include Namielle, Yama Tsukami, Wind Serpent Ibushi and Thunder Serpent Narwa (a duo fight), and Velkhana. Each has specific elemental weaknesses and resistance patterns. Recommended Monsties like Malzeno, Magnamalo, and Ratha help counter their attacks. Players must adapt to forms, status ailments, and attack patterns while managing team defenses and exploiting Head-to-Head combat mechanics to break parts and reduce enemy health. Success rewards players with high-tier gear needed for further progression. This guide provides detailed tactics for each dragon encounter.
