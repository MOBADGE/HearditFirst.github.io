Stardew Valley 1.7 Update Aims to Enhance Family and Social Features

Eric Barone, creator of Stardew Valley, provided new details on the upcoming 1.7 update in a recent interview. One notable change addresses long-standing fan feedback about children in the game, who currently have limited activities and growth. Barone confirmed efforts to make in-game children "a little more interesting," though he did not elaborate on specific mechanics. Additionally, the update will introduce two new marriage candidates, expanding player options for relationships. Barone also discussed the possibility of a community-driven farm map editor, which could enable players to create and share custom farm layouts, significantly increasing replayability. While traditional new farm types will be added, the full scope of 1.7 will be clearer closer to release.
