Meta to Close VR Version of Horizon Worlds, Retain Mobile App

Meta announced it will shut down the VR version of its social platform Horizon Worlds on June 15, 2026. The app will be removed from the Quest store by March 31, after which official VR locations like Horizon Central and Events Arena will no longer be accessible. However, the mobile app version of Horizon Worlds will continue to operate independently. Meta explained this move as a step to focus the VR and mobile platforms separately, reshaping Horizon Worlds into a mobile-only experience going forward.

The change also affects Meta Horizon Plus subscribers, who will lose Horizon Worlds benefits such as Meta Credits and digital clothing with no replacement perks announced. This decision comes amidst broader shifts within Meta’s Reality Labs, which recently saw studio closures and layoffs, suggesting a strategic move away from VR social spaces toward mobile worlds and AI wearables.

Take-Two CEO Confident in GTA 6’s Broad Appeal

Take-Two CEO Strauss Zelnick expressed strong confidence in the upcoming release of Grand Theft Auto 6, scheduled for November 19, 2026, on consoles with PC details pending. In an interview, Zelnick stated he finds it hard to imagine any eligible adult gamer choosing not to play GTA 6, emphasizing the franchise’s enduring popularity across age groups.

Zelnick noted the company’s belief that the game will engage not only long-time fans who grew up with previous titles but also new, younger players. While this level of certainty may be optimistic, it reflects Take-Two’s high expectations backed by substantial marketing investment. The discussion underscores GTA 6’s position as a major release for the year and the continued importance of franchise loyalty in gaming.

Mewgenics Patch Includes Notable Bug Fix and Gameplay Adjustments

The latest update for roguelike strategy game Mewgenics, patch 1.0.20870, addressed various bugs and gameplay concerns. Among standard fixes, it resolved a rare crash triggered when a non-binary cat with dwarfism was named in Brazilian Portuguese, highlighting the complex interactions between localization and game code.

In gameplay, the patch limited some powerful spells used by the miniboss Arthur to reduce unpredictably difficult encounters. It also fixed how self-damaging abilities count towards synergies, enabling new tactical combinations. These adjustments aim to make certain fights fairer and improve overall play stability following the game’s recent launch.

Intel Releases Arrow Lake Plus Mobile CPUs with Up to 8% Gaming Performance Boost

Intel introduced new mobile processors in its Arrow Lake Plus line, including the Core Ultra 9 290HX Plus and Core Ultra 7 270HX Plus. Despite no changes in core counts or clock speeds compared to previous models, the new chips deliver up to 8% more gaming performance. This improvement is partly attributed to a higher uncore frequency and architectural refinements.

A key feature for these CPUs is Intel’s new Binary Optimization Tool (BOT), a software layer that optimizes game performance dynamically and is currently exclusive to these Plus models. The chips are now available to OEM partners and will soon appear in laptops without significant price increases relative to prior Arrow Lake processors.

Indie Publisher Finji Overwhelmed by AI-Generated Game Pitches

Finji, an indie game developer and publisher known for titles like Tunic and Night in the Woods, reports receiving an unprecedented number of game pitches, many including AI-generated content. CEO Rebekah Saltsman expressed frustration with the volume of AI-based games, art, and coding arriving in Finji’s inbox, signaling a growing trend in the indie scene.

Finji has updated its publishing contracts to allow termination if undisclosed AI is involved, citing concerns over copyright and originality. Saltsman emphasized the publisher’s preference for genuine creative work and skepticism toward AI-generated art and code, which are often seen as low-quality or legally risky. The studio remains focused on traditional development for upcoming projects.
