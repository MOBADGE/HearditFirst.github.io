Amazon Ends Publishing Deal with Maverick Games

Amazon Games has ended its publishing agreement with Maverick Games, the studio led by former Forza Horizon creative director Michael Brown. The move comes amid Amazon's broader strategic shift away from big-budget game development following a series of setbacks, including layoffs and project cancellations in 2025. Maverick Games confirmed they are now free to seek a new publishing partner and that their development is progressing steadily. Amazon related this decision to focusing resources on areas where it sees unique advantages, such as the Luna cloud gaming platform and existing franchises. This underscores Amazon's retreat from competing in AAA game development.
