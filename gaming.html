Path of Exile 2: Unarmed Victory and New Druid Class Highlights

This approach highlights the game's underlying "soulslike" combat mechanics, emphasizing player skill and timing over equipment. Fallen Aces Episode Two Delayed, Reveals First-Person Car Chase

The release of Fallen Aces' second episode has been postponed beyond 2025, as confirmed in a recent Steam development update. However, the update included a teaser showing a new gameplay segment featuring a chaotic first-person car chase. In this sequence, players wield tommy guns from both hands while controlling the vehicle with foot pedals, underlining the game's blend of immersive sim elements and over-the-top comic book action. The update also introduces new areas, story comics, and a controllable NPC companion named Delia, suggesting the sequel will expand the scope and interactivity of the original's neo-noir inspired setting. Fallen Aces remains in early access, joining a trend of New Blood Interactive titles taking several years before reaching full release.
