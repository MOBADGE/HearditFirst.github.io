Supervive MOBA Shuts Down Seven Months After Launch

Supervive, a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) styled as a battle royale and developed by former Riot Games employees, will close its servers in February 2026. Developer Theorycraft Games stated that while many players tried Supervive, retention was low, making continued development unfeasible.
