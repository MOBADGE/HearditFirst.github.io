**Marathon Expands with New Map, Weapon Dynamics, and Unfolding ARG**

Players diving into Bungie’s extraction shooter *Marathon* have shared fresh insights after significant time with the game. The recently unlocked third map, Outpost, offers a compact but intense compound setting surrounded by hazards such as “anti-organic” phosphorus barrages that damage players exposed to the sky. This map’s verticality and cover options encourage strategic use of movement abilities like double jump. The solo experience emphasizes stealth elements, such as the meaningful use of doors — players can close exterior doors to warn of intruders, adding tactical depth to looting.

The weapon balance is a focal point of discussion, especially regarding the double-barrel shotgun. Early impressions suggest it may be overpowered; a single point-blank blast can down fully healthy players with minimal shields. While its rarity and expensive ammo might justify its potency, its effectiveness in PvP has been notable.

Alongside gameplay developments, *Marathon*’s community uncovered an alternate reality game (ARG) component. Previously inactive terminals scattered throughout the Perimeter map have been activated, revealing eerie audio logs attributed to Durandal, an AI character from the original series. These clues lead to the Cryo Archive, a forthcoming fourth map promised to feature raid-like content. Bungie has acknowledged these findings by connecting players to a dedicated website replicating in-game security feeds. This approach integrates narrative discovery into the gameplay loop, blending looting runs with secret-hunting activities.

**Artist Whose Work Was Used Without Permission by Bungie Now Credited in Marathon**

An earlier controversy concerning artist Antireal’s work being used without permission in *Marathon* (and previously in *Destiny 2*) has seen a development. Bungie admitted that the infringement was committed by a now-former employee and promised to review assets to prevent further issues. Recently, Antireal confirmed that the dispute was resolved satisfactorily. Importantly, she is now officially credited as a freelance visual design consultant in *Marathon*’s credits posted on Bungie’s website.

Though the financial or contractual details remain undisclosed, this credit marks a formal acknowledgment of her contributions and may positively impact her professional standing. The situation underscores ongoing challenges in creative industries regarding intellectual property respect and artist recognition.

**Valve’s Steam Machines Face Potential Delay Due to Component Shortages**

Valve’s planned 2026 launch of its new Steam Machine hardware lineup — including the Steam Controller and Steam Frame VR headset — is meeting obstacles linked to supply shortages. The global scarcity and price increases of crucial components such as RAM and storage chips, exacerbated by AI-related demand, are affecting production timelines and pricing strategies.

Valve has stated it still hopes to ship the devices within 2026 but acknowledges the need to revise schedules and pricing to reflect the volatile market. As Steam Machines are aimed at living room PC gaming and will not be heavily subsidized like traditional consoles, cost-effectiveness is key to adoption. Industry observers now consider a launch slipping into 2027 a distinct possibility unless supply conditions improve.

**Upcoming Stardew Valley Update Introduces Sandy as Marriage Candidate**

With the forthcoming Stardew Valley 1.7 update, players will gain the option to romance Sandy, the Oasis shop owner located in the Desert area. While she is not currently a marriage candidate in version 1.6, players can build friendship by gifting her preferred items such as flowers (Sweet Pea, Daffodil, Crocus), certain fruits, and goat products. Avoid gifting items she dislikes like Holly or Red Mushrooms.

Sandy maintains a consistent schedule, mostly found at her shop in the Oasis, occasionally appearing with character Emily during seasonal events. Her inclusion expands Stardew Valley’s social and romantic options, introducing new recipes and crafting components linked to her friendship.
