Lowiro, the developer behind the mobile rhythm game Arcaea, is preparing to launch a new game called In Falsus for PC. Nightdive Studios CEO Stephen Kick Reflects on Dota 2 Dedication

Cities: Skylines 2 Patch from New Developer Focuses on Death Mechanics

Iceflake Studios, the new developer for Cities: Skylines 2, released its first update named First Frost. The patch addresses bugs related to citizen death timing and removes an immortality bug in easy mode that had about 80% of citizens never dying of old age. The update also increases the frequency of death calculations to reduce mass simultaneous deaths. Additional tweaks include reducing bicycle use, UI enhancements, graphical improvements, and enabling autosave by default. Early signs show an improvement in player reception, with recent reviews trending more positive.
