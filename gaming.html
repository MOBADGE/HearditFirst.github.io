**Splitgate: Arena Reloaded Developer Responds to Declining Player Numbers**

The studio behind Splitgate: Arena Reloaded addressed concerns over declining concurrent player counts on Steam, where the game has seen numbers fall below 1,000 players following its December 2025 re-launch. Developer 1047 Games noted that Steam metrics do not represent the full player base, which includes users on other platforms, and emphasized ongoing community engagement and content plans such as the upcoming Arena Royale mode.

Despite the reassurances, Steam remains the major platform for PC gaming, and the low concurrent user counts reflect challenges in regaining the level of attention the original Splitgate attained. The statement commits to continuous development despite a muted reception to the relaunch.
