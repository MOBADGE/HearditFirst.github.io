## Ayaneo Unveils Strix Halo Handheld PC with High-End and More Affordable Variants

Ayaneo has announced the shipping date and pricing for its new Strix Halo handheld gaming PC, also known as the Next II. The device comes in several configurations, with prices ranging from $1,799 for the AI Max 385 model to $4,299 for the top-tier AI Max+ 395 model after the early bird period ends. Notably, the AI Max+ 395 version features a large 116 Wh battery, which may pose restrictions for air travel due to its size. The AI Max 385 model, priced under $2,000, offers solid performance with Ryzen processors and integrated Radeon 8050S graphics, appealing to players seeking a balance between power and cost. The handheld sports a 9.06-inch 2400 x 1504 OLED display with refresh rates up to 165 Hz and includes advanced controls like TMR joysticks, Hall effect triggers, and dual touchpads. Ayaneo stands out by integrating a high-capacity internal battery, unlike some competitors who rely on external power packs.

## Deadlock Players Creating Unusual Character Mods, Including Linguini and Remy
