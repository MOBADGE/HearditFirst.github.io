Arc Raiders Patch Fixes Exploits as Weekly Updates Slow Down

Developers Embark released patch 1.6.0 for their PvPvE shooter Arc Raiders, addressing several map exploits. Notably, it removes a zipline in the Stella Montis map that allowed players to escape map boundaries, fixes a collision flaw in Blue Gate enabling wall breaches, corrects vision-blocking issues affecting bullet and arc interactions, and patches a gap that let players be shot through walls in the Spaceport Launch Tower.

This shift reflects the stabilization typical for live service games reaching a mature post-launch state.
