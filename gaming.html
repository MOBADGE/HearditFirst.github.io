Marathon Update: New Map, Gameplay Nuances, and Emerging ARG

After its launch, Bungie's extraction shooter *Marathon* continues to generate player interest. A recent player report highlights the third playable map, Outpost, available from level 12. Outpost is a compact, walled compound featuring vertical gameplay elements and a map-wide environmental hazard of phosphorus-like barrages that drain health when exposed outdoors. This design demands strategic use of cover and movement abilities such as double jump. Additionally, players are appreciating small gameplay details, like the loud sound doors make when opened and the tactical advantage of closing doors behind to alert others of intrusions.

On the weapons front, the double-barrel shotgun stands out for its high damage output and ability to quickly turn the tide in engagements, though some question if its power is balanced given its rarity and ammo cost.

Separately, the *Marathon* community has uncovered an alternate gameplay layer with the activation of previously non-functional terminals on the Perimeter map. These terminals deliver cryptic audio logs from Durandal, a character linked to the series’ lore, and point players toward a secret “Cryo Archive” map. Bungie has supported this by linking to a hidden website featuring multiple camera feeds of the Cryo Archive, suggesting an evolving alternate reality game (ARG) that could deepen player engagement.

Stardew Valley Introduces Sandy, New Desert NPC and Future Romance Candidate

With the upcoming 1.7 update, *Stardew Valley* will expand its NPC roster by adding Sandy, the owner of the Desert’s Oasis shop. While currently unromanceable, Sandy will become a marriage candidate post-update. Players can meet and befriend her now by reaching the Calico Desert, unlocked by repairing the bus. Sandy’s daily presence in her shop, along with occasional desert strolls, offers consistent opportunities to interact.

She has distinct preferences for gifts, favoring flowers like Crocus and Sweet Pea, various fruits, and specific cooked dishes such as Mango Sticky Rice. Notably, players should avoid giving her holly and red mushrooms, which she dislikes. Befriending Sandy to at least seven hearts grants access to her Tom Kha Soup recipe, which benefits other villagers and the player’s crafting options.

Sandy’s introduction reflects *Stardew Valley*’s continued effort to enrich its world and provides players with new content and social dynamics in the desert environment.

Resolution in Bungie Art Theft Controversy

Following revelations in 2025 that Bungie had improperly used artist Antireal’s work in *Marathon* and previously in *Destiny 2*, Bungie has formally credited her as a freelance visual design consultant in *Marathon*’s official credits. While financial details of the resolution remain private, the addition of her name marks a meaningful acknowledgement of her contribution and the resolution of the dispute.

Despite the resolution, Antireal recently discovered that elements of her art were used on a licensed Xbox controller without apparent additional authorization, indicating ongoing issues with the control of her visual work.

This situation underscores the challenges artists face in protecting their intellectual property within large game development projects and adds context to *Marathon*’s distinct art style.

Valve Faces Potential Delay for Steam Machines Amid Hardware Shortages

Valve’s planned launch of its Steam Machines—a new generation of compact living-room gaming PCs—may be delayed beyond the initially targeted 2026 window due to industry-wide shortages in memory and storage components. These shortages, primarily driven by demand from AI technology sectors, have caused supply constraints and price increases for RAM and SSDs.

Valve has publicly acknowledged these challenges in its 2025 Year in Review and indicated that while it hopes for a 2026 release, launch dates and pricing remain uncertain. The company also needs to ensure the Steam Machine’s price is competitive enough to gain mainstream adoption, without the subsidization strategies used by console manufacturers. This delay may push availability into 2027.

This potential setback highlights how current hardware market conditions continue to impact even established companies’ hardware plans.
