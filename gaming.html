**Ashes of Creation MMO Canceled After Team Layoffs**

Intrepid Studios has halted development on Ashes of Creation, an MMO crowdfunded in 2017 and once the most successful Kickstarter MMO campaign, raising about $3.3 million. The entire team was laid off after a management shake-up: founder Steven Sharif resigned over disagreements with the Board, followed by mass layoffs under WARN Act notices. This decision came shortly after the studio posted a letter promising continued investment and a Feb 13 update. Ashes of Creation had faced persistent issues with cheating, bugs, and performance throughout its five years of early access. The abrupt shutdown reflects ongoing challenges in ambitious MMO projects balancing creative goals and investor pressures. It also serves as a cautionary example about the risks of long development cycles and the uncertainties in crowdfunded game projects.
