UK Court Rules RuneScape Gold Theft is Legally Theft

A UK judge has ruled that virtual gold stolen in *Old School RuneScape* qualifies as legally recognized property in a recent case involving an ex-Jagex employee. The individual was accused of hacking 68 accounts and stealing game currency valued at over $700,000, which he then sold for Bitcoin. The judgment establishes that in-game assets, when traded both inside and outside the game, can be considered property under the Theft Act 1968. This case sets an important precedent for how virtual goods are treated in legal contexts, affirming that theft of such assets can have real-world consequences.
