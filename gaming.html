New Overpowered Hero Rogue Joins Marvel Rivals

Marvel Rivals has introduced Rogue as the latest hero for its season 5.5 update. Rogue's standout ability is her power to steal abilities from enemy heroes, enabling her to create shields, heal, or even turn invisible. So far, she can steal powers such as Thor's awakening, Doctor Strange's shield, Iron Fist's healing, Psylocke's invisibility, among others. However, she can only use one stolen power at a time and must get close to the opposing player to absorb it, which could put her at risk in team fights. Rogue's introduction changes the competitive landscape of Marvel Rivals as players will need to adapt to her threat of borrowing and leveraging enemy abilities.

Australia Implements Social Media Ban for Under-16s Including Twitch

Australia has enacted a new law preventing children under 16 from signing up for accounts on 10 major social media platforms, including Twitch, TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube. Platforms face fines up to AU$49.5 million if they fail to enforce age restrictions. The ban aims to reduce exposure to harmful content and screen time pressures on young users. However, some platforms with child safety concerns, such as Roblox and Discord, were excluded under current legislation, causing debate. Other countries like Malaysia and New Zealand are considering similar measures. While the ban has public support in Australia, questions remain about its impact on youth access to online communities and information.
