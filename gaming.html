**Noir Thriller "The Last Goodbye" Arrives as a Half-Life Engine Mod**

A new total conversion mod titled The Last Goodbye has been released for the original Half-Life engine (GoldSrc). Developed by Guild of Inventors, this mod immerses players in a pulp noir setting where you assume the role of Lucille, a gun-for-hire tasked with assassinating a gangster named Bullseye. The mod combines action with puzzle and exploration elements, emphasizing storytelling through cutscenes inspired by classic TV reconstructions. Combat involves firearms and melee, with a focus on challenge as Lucille has no protective suit but increased agility. The mod is currently available on ModDB with one of three planned acts completed; subsequent releases will add enemies and weapons. This mod highlights the enduring versatility of the original Half-Life engine and community interest in new, story-driven experiences layered with atmospheric aesthetics.
