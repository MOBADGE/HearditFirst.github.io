Capcom Reinstates Free Viewing for Street Fighter World Championship

Capcom initially announced that its Capcom Cup finals and Street Fighter League World Championship would be behind a $40 pay-per-view paywall, a shift from previous years when the events were free to watch. Following considerable backlash from Street Fighter 6 fans, the company revised its approach. Now, the pay-per-view price has been reduced to $10, and owners of Street Fighter 6 can watch the tournaments for free through an in-game streaming option. However, the free viewing does not include the commentary available in the paid stream. This development shows Capcom's responsiveness to community feedback and preserves accessibility for fans who prefer not to pay for competitive event content.
