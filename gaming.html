**Hades 2 Update Improves Dialogue, Romance, and Ares Boons** Supergiant Games released a patch for *Hades 2* that addresses several player concerns since its full release last year. The update adds new dialogue to deepen character interactions, improves romance storylines by easing conditions to access key scenes, and enhances gameplay by buffing Ares, whose in-game boons had been considered underpowered. These changes aim to polish the narrative and mechanical experience, responding to feedback that the original ending and some character interactions felt rushed or unsatisfying. For players invested in *Hades 2*, this patch provides richer story elements and better in-game balance.

**PC and Future Games Shows Set to Air in June** The upcoming months will bring back two major industry events: the PC Gaming Show and the Future Games Show, scheduled for early June. The PC Gaming Show is set for June 7 and will feature over 50 games, including world premieres and developer interviews, alongside demonstrations. Meanwhile, the Future Games Show, with a Summer Showcase on June 6 and a “Live From Los Angeles” segment, will also spotlight new content. These showcases are important for gamers and industry observers as they reveal upcoming titles, updates, and trends in both PC and console spheres ahead of summer.

**Acer 24-inch 1080p Monitor on Sale for $90** For PC gamers looking to expand their setup, the Acer Nitro KG241Y P3bip monitor has been highlighted as a strong budget pick for a second display. Priced at $90, it offers a 24-inch 1080p screen with a fast 180 Hz refresh rate and 0.5 ms response time, suitable for competitive and casual gaming alike. While the 1080p resolution is modest compared to higher-res options, the monitor balances price, size, and performance, making it a practical addition to dual-monitor setups. Those prioritizing better color accuracy might consider alternatives with IPS panels, but Acer's VA panel here provides good contrast and speed at this price point.

**MediaTek Sees Potential Improvement in Memory Prices in Late 2026** MediaTek expressed cautious optimism regarding discrete memory pricing, predicting some easing in costs during the second half of 2026. Despite rising consumer prices driven in part by AI demands, MediaTek maintains sufficient capacity for its products due to early agreements with manufacturers. However, the company acknowledges that rising memory prices could dampen consumer spending on devices like notebooks, potentially forcing a price correction. The outlook is tempered by the slow build-up of new manufacturing facilities, with major suppliers like TSMC, Micron, Samsung, and SK Hynix not expected to significantly increase supply until 2027 or 2028.

**RTX 5060 Ti 8 GB Review Highlights Good Value with Caveats** The Nvidia RTX 5060 Ti 8 GB graphics card offers solid 1080p and 1440p performance near its more expensive 16 GB counterpart, making it an attractive choice for gamers on a budget. Tested in a Palit Dual model, it provides competitive frame rates in several modern titles and outperforms AMD's RX 9060 XT 8 GB in many scenarios. However, the 8 GB VRAM limits performance in demanding, open-world, or high-resolution settings, where the 16 GB versions maintain an advantage. Thermally, the card runs warmer than AMD alternatives but remains within safe limits. The Palit Dual card is also noted for quiet operation and decent power efficiency. Pricing challenges exist due to market inflation, with some models selling above MSRP, but generally, the RTX 5060 Ti 8 GB balances cost and performance for mid-range gaming.

**Marty O'Donnell Accepts Trump Endorsement in Nevada Congressional Race** Marty O'Donnell, known for composing *Halo* and *Destiny* music and previously involved in politics, has received Donald Trump's endorsement in his run for Nevada’s 3rd Congressional District. Despite past statements calling Trump an "idiot" and expressing loathing, O'Donnell welcomed the support and indicated a shared outsider approach to politics. The endorsement positions O'Donnell among several Trump-aligned Republican candidates in a competitive primary scheduled for June. His political messaging continues to feature his background in game development and music, leveraging his industry reputation.
