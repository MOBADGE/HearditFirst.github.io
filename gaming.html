Mchose L7 Pro+ Gaming Mouse Offers Affordable Competitive Performance

The Mchose L7 Pro+ is an ultralight gaming mouse retailing at around $60, notable for bringing many features usually found in higher-priced models. It uses a PixArt PAW3395 sensor, weighs 43 grams, and supports USB-C wired, 2.4 GHz wireless, and Bluetooth connectivity. The mouse closely replicates the shape of popular mid-tier mice suited for claw grips but may be less comfortable for pure palm users. The accompanying software is flexible and well-designed, offering advanced sensor and polling rate adjustments. This mouse presents a competitive option for gamers on a budget.
