Classic Space Combat Sim Star Quest 1 Updated and Released for Free

Star Quest 1 in the 27th Century, a lesser-known space combat simulator originally released in 1995, has been updated to work on modern Windows systems and is now free to download on itch.io. Developed by Virtual Adventures Inc, the game was notable for seamlessly combining space and atmospheric flight decades before similar modern titles.

The 30th Anniversary Edition adds support for modern controllers, including HOTAS setups, improved keyboard and mouse inputs, and removes the need for DOS emulation. Despite its age, Star Quest offers a variety of missions that include dogfighting, racing, and tactical squad engagements across fully 3D shaded environments.

This rerelease offers a glimpse into early space sim design and serves as a free piece of PC gaming history.
