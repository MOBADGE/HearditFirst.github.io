Fallout Mod Project Unveils Trailer and New Name

A total conversion mod aiming to recreate the cancelled Fallout 3 sequel originally developed by Black Isle Studios has released its first trailer and announced a new name. Formerly known as Revelation Blues, the project is now called *The New West*. It builds upon elements from the cancelled "Van Buren" game, blending them into *Fallout: New Vegas* using shared assets like Caesar's Legion and the Big MT. The trailer introduces a plot involving escaping a prison controlled by an AI named Odysseus and a mysterious New Plague in the wasteland.

This mod is significant as it tries to revive the vision of a lost Fallout installment, offering fans a way to explore the story and themes that never made it into an official game. Given the lasting interest in *Fallout: New Vegas*, and the spotlight on the franchise from recent media adaptations, this project may gain considerable attention among the community. Progress updates and a playable demo are available online.

The Killing Stone Delays Full Release in Favor of Early Access

*The Killing Stone*, a deckbuilding game inspired by *Inscryption*, has announced it will launch into early access on February 18 rather than a full 1.0 release. Developer Jordan Thomas explained that the game evolved during development to include a more story-focused narrative, featuring mystery-driven elements and interactive dialogues in both modern and 17th-century English. Feedback from playtesting showed players wanted more story content and voice acting, which prompted more iteration before the final release.

The early access version will contain the main story arcs, optional missions, and multiple demon lord opponents but will exclude the substantial endgame content labeled "The Reckoning," which will come in the full version later. This decision allows the developers to refine the narrative and gameplay based on player responses while providing early access to interested players. The game will be available on Steam, where a demo is also offered.
