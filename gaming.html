Hollow Knight: Silksong Sales and Expansion Announcement Team Cherry has revealed that *Hollow Knight: Silksong* has sold over seven million copies in just over three months since its launch. Notably, this figure excludes the millions of additional players accessing the game through Xbox Game Pass. Despite its $20 price point, *Silksong* achieved strong sales that even caused Steam to crash on launch day. The studio expressed surprise and gratitude at the community's response, citing the vibrant player creativity and support found within the player base.

Adding to this momentum, Team Cherry announced a free expansion titled *Sea of Sorrow*, expected in 2026. This expansion will introduce new areas, bosses, tools, and a nautical theme, continuing to build on the already extensive game world. The announcement underlines Team Cherry's commitment to developing *Silksong* further and maintaining player engagement going forward. Meanwhile, the original *Hollow Knight* is receiving quality-of-life improvements on PC, including better aspect ratio support and inventory pausing.

Diablo 4 Players Discover Secret Boss Battle Players of *Diablo 4* have uncovered a secret seasonal boss event allowing them to collect rare purified body parts dropped by lesser demon lords. Collecting these parts and placing them on four altars opens a portal to a hidden arena where four bosses appear simultaneously. The discovery demonstrates ongoing developer efforts to include hidden content and maintain player interest between major updates.
