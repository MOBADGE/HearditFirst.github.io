**Hytale: How to Obtain and Use Iron and Repair Kits**

Two practical guides for new Hytale players have emerged to assist with survival and progression. Iron is found primarily in underground caves within Emerald Grove biomes. Players need pickaxes, preferably upgraded to copper, to mine iron ore, which then must be smelted in a Tier 1 Furnace along with fuel like sticks to produce usable iron ingots. These ingots are vital for crafting stronger tools and weapons.

Repairing damaged gear in Hytale involves crafting Repair Kits at a Tier 1 (or higher) Workbench using two linen scraps, one iron ingot, and one light leather. After crafting, players equip a Repair Kit and select the item they wish to repair from a menu. Because the materials involved can accumulate quickly, players are advised to use Repair Kits judiciously rather than repairing equipment prematurely.
