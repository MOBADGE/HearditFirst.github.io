Skyrim Co-Lead Designer on Cut Features: Dynamic Civil War and Real-Time Travel

Kurt Kuhlmann, former co-lead designer on *Skyrim* and longtime Elder Scrolls developer, revealed in an interview that significant features were cut from *Skyrim* before release. These included a fully dynamic civil war system where player actions would shift the balance of power across holds, potentially leading to large-scale battles beyond the scripted *Battle for Whiterun*. Additionally, Kuhlmann worked on real-time horse and cart travel that would allow players to journey between cities dynamically rather than using instant fast travel, with physics allowing players to dismount and intervene in events. These ambitious systems were shelved largely due to technical limitations, performance issues, and complexity on the original Xbox 360 hardware. Modders have since recreated elements of these ideas, keeping the concepts alive within the community.
