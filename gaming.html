EB Games to Close All Stores in New Zealand

EB Games announced it will shut down all 38 retail locations in New Zealand by the end of January due to sustained financial losses reported in the 2024 fiscal year. The company has attempted multiple strategies to stabilize the business without success. The closure will also involve shutting the distribution center by the end of February, but the online store will remain accessible to New Zealand customers starting March 2026. The shutdown reflects broader shifts in the retail landscape, including competition from online vendors and a decline in physical game sales. The Australian outlets remain open, though the company has also reduced store numbers in that market in recent years.
