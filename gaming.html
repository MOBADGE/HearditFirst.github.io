**High Rate of Layoffs Continues to Impact the U.S. Games Industry** The GDC 2026 State of the Game Industry report shows that 33% of U.S.-based game industry workers have been laid off within the past two years, with 17% experiencing layoffs in the last 12 months alone. This ongoing trend follows earlier waves of job cuts post-pandemic and amid corporate acquisitions. Industry professionals attribute layoffs largely to mismanagement during the post-boom contraction and shifting priorities, including investment in AI technology. These conditions contribute to uncertainty and concern among new entrants; 74% of surveyed students fear stiff competition for jobs, while 87% of educators expect difficulties for graduates finding employment.
