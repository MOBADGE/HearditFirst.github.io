The Midnight Walkers Early Access Launch Details

The zombie-themed cooperative FPS The Midnight Walkers will launch in early access on January 28 at 6 pm PST, as confirmed by developer Oneway Ticket Studio. Highguard Faces Mixed Reception but Deserves Consideration

The genre-mixing game Highguard has experienced a challenging launch, marked by an awkward reveal and a wave of mostly negative player reviews, despite reaching nearly 100,000 concurrent players on Steam. Review bombing and pre-judgments have contributed to its low rating, though some critics argue the game is flawed but interesting and attempts unique gameplay combinations. Mod Adds Sneaking Animation for Fallout: New Vegas Companion Dog

A new mod named Dog Sneak Animations, created by modder Wombat, addresses a long-standing visual inconsistency in Fallout: New Vegas by adding a sneaking animation for the canine companion Rex. Previously, Rex did not crouch or stealth with the player, which broke immersion during sneaking stealth sequences. The mod activates when players crouch outside of combat, with Rex shuffling quietly alongside. This addition has been well received in the community for enhancing the game's atmosphere. Wombat is known for several other animation-related mods that refine character movement and equipment behavior in New Vegas.
