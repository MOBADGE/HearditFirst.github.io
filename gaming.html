Space Marine 2 Introduces Free Techmarine Class

*Space Marine 2* has added a new free class called the Techmarine through its latest update, nearly 18 months after launch. This engineer-type character combines combat and support roles by deploying and repairing automated sentry turrets while wielding a large servo-arm axe for close combat. The Techmarine is designed to integrate into both PvE and PvP modes, with a thematic twist: when playing on the Chaos side, the class becomes a Warpsmith adorned in Red Corsairs colors.

This addition also brings a new PvE mission named *Disruption* and cosmetic content linked to different Space Marine chapters. The update extends post-launch support and content variety, helping maintain player engagement well after release.

Muri: Wildwoods Demo Offers Relaxing Cleaning Adventure

The demo for *Muri: Wildwoods*, featured during Steam Next Fest, blends elements of exploration and environment restoration with a satisfying cleaning mechanic reminiscent of *PowerWash Simulator*. Players control Pelle, a mouse-like creature armed with a water gun tasked with cleansing a corrupted mysterious island. The gameplay loop involves removing black corruption spreading across land, wildlife, and objects, which then visibly rejuvenates the environment.

The demo was praised for its soothing gameplay, cute creature design, and a natural progression system inviting players to explore new areas as they clean. This offers a quiet, low-pressure alternative to more intense games, appealing to those who enjoy methodical tasks and world-building through restoration.
