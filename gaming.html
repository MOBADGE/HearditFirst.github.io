**The Outer Worlds and Ancestors: The Humankind Odyssey Leaving GOG**

Starting December 15, the sci-fi RPG *The Outer Worlds* and the prehistoric survival game *Ancestors: The Humankind Odyssey* will be removed from the GOG store. Currently, *Ancestors* is available at an 80% discount, but this and all editions and add-ons for both games will no longer be sold after the delisting date. The exact reasons are unclear, though it likely relates to changes involving their publisher Private Division, which was sold off and restructured last year. This may be routine catalog housekeeping pending possible future return, but players interested should buy before the deadline.

**Battlefield 6 Winter Offensive Update Causes Stuttering Bug**

The *Battlefield 6* Winter Offensive update, released today, introduces a range of new content such as a snowy map variant and cosmetics but also a notable stuttering issue on PC. Players report periodic freezes of several frames disrupting gameplay. The developers acknowledge the bug and suggest a temporary fix for Steam users: turning off the Steam friends list or going offline in the friends menu. A proper patch is in the works but no firm release date for the fix has been provided yet.
