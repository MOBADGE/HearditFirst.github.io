Beyerdynamic Launches MMX 150 Wireless Gaming Headset with Focus on Audio and Comfort

Beyerdynamic released its MMX 150 Wireless gaming headset at $230, aiming to provide an entry point into the brand's audio quality without the premium price of models like the MMX 330 Pro. It features 40 mm drivers known for delivering warm and clear sound with well-defined bass and wide soundstage. The headset supports 2.4 GHz wireless connectivity, Bluetooth 5.3 for mobile use, and includes a removable Meta Voice microphone.

Comfort remains a highlight with plush velour earpads and a padded headband providing passive noise isolation, although the build quality and control materials show some cost-saving measures. Battery life is about 50 hours, which is reasonable but not impressive for the price range. The headset lacks active noise cancellation and software customization, which might be notable omissions compared to competitors like the Razer BlackShark V3 Pro.

Overall, the MMX 150 Wireless offers solid audio performance and comfort for its price but encounters stiff competition in a crowded market where some rivals provide more features and better build quality.

Highguard Developer Attributes Website Issues to Administrative Delay, Prioritizes Game Updates

The developer of Highguard, Wildlight, noted that the game's official website downtime is due to administrative complications involving domain transfer and simplification rather than technical or financial problems. A message from the studio indicated this issue is currently a low priority given ongoing efforts to focus on improving the game through updates and new content.

Highguard had a strong launch with nearly 100,000 concurrent Steam players, largely driven by a surprise reveal at The Game Awards. Since then, Wildlight has maintained a core development team for updates, including a recent content patch.

Despite website troubles and dwindling player engagement, the team seems focused on incremental improvements rather than immediate public relations or infrastructure fixes.
