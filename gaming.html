Marvel’s Wolverine Preorders Pending Further Gameplay Reveal

Insomniac Games, the developer behind the upcoming Marvel’s Wolverine game for PS5, has yet to open preorders despite the game’s confirmed September 16 release date. The studio has indicated it plans to showcase additional gameplay footage during spring 2026 before making preorders available. This approach suggests Insomniac wants to provide a more comprehensive look at the game to build consumer confidence and engagement ahead of launch. As one of Sony’s key titles this year, Marvel’s Wolverine is likely to feature in a future State of Play event within the next few months, potentially by the summer solstice.

New Insights on Canceled Breaking Bad VR Project from Firesprite

Reports have emerged revealing that Sony-owned Firesprite Studios once worked on a Breaking Bad VR experience in 2017-2018 aimed at PlayStation VR. The unreleased project was designed as more of a narrative “experience” rather than a traditional game, similar to Stranger Things: The VR Experience. Players would have explored iconic Breaking Bad locations such as Walter White’s backyard and the New Mexico desert, immersing themselves in the series’ story moments. Despite its cancellation, the report sheds light on Firesprite’s experimental VR efforts and the challenges of adapting TV properties into immersive formats.

How to Solve the Secret Chamber of the Golden Plains Puzzle in Crimson Desert

Crimson Desert players seeking to unlock the Secret Chamber of the Golden Plains will need to complete an involved mural puzzle located in the Demeniss region's Golden Plains area. The puzzle requires players to access a vine-covered ruined building by burning the vines or using an ability, then progressing carefully through flame traps and spiked pits inside. At the puzzle wall, players match mural blocks featuring symbols like grapes, horse, pomegranate, pigeon, and fish with in-world locations known for corresponding livestock or crops. Completing the puzzle grants the “Memories of Prosperity,” a consumable revealing hidden waterfall-locked caves, providing both a valuable reward and added world exploration.

Bungie Responds to Player Feedback on Marathon’s Cryo Archive Endgame

Following the launch of Marathon’s highest-tier endgame mode, Cryo Archive, Bungie has acknowledged player feedback concerning the activity’s strict entry requirements and limited weekend scheduling. Game director Joe Ziegler stated the team is reviewing comments and planning future iterations to improve accessibility and experience. Players have specifically requested options for solo play and more consistent scheduling. Bungie is also considering adjustments to loot drop rates for subroutines, crucial items in the activity. These potential changes indicate Bungie’s ongoing commitment to refining endgame content based on community input.

Xbox Announces Partner Preview Event with Game Pass Updates for March 26

Microsoft has scheduled an Xbox Partner Preview livestream for March 26, promising a lineup including games from Sega, GSC Game World, and Owlcat Games, alongside new reveals and Xbox Game Pass announcements. Confirmed titles include Stranger Than Heaven, STALKER 2: Heart of Chornobyl, and The Expanse: Osiris Reborn. This event continues Xbox’s regular cycle of partner showcases that highlight upcoming releases and service additions, providing players with updates on diverse genres and support offerings.

Capcom Clarifies Stance on Generative AI in Game Development

Capcom has confirmed it will not incorporate AI-generated assets directly into its video game content. The company stated this position during a recent shareholder meeting, emphasizing that while it plans to leverage generative AI technologies, it will use them to optimize development efficiency rather than creating in-game art or assets. This aligns with a broader industry trend where many developers are cautious in applying generative AI, preferring to maintain creative control and quality while exploring AI’s potential to streamline production pipelines.
