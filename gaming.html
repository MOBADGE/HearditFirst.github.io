Square Enix Faces Shareholder Criticism Over Management

Square Enix is currently under pressure from one of its major shareholders, 3D Investment, which holds a 14.36% stake in the company. The activist investor released a detailed 100-page presentation accusing Square Enix's management of missteps, including sluggish revenue growth, weak profit margins, and significant impairment losses on game development assets. The shareholder also criticized the company's medium-term business plan for lacking concrete goals, key performance indicators, and clear execution strategies.

3D Investment attempted to engage directly with Square Enix management but received only brief, non-specific responses, leading them to appeal publicly to other shareholders for their views on corporate governance and strategy. While 3D Investment‚Äôs involvement is typical of activist investors seeking improvements in underperforming firms, this move highlights possible internal challenges facing Square Enix as it struggles with fading momentum in flagship titles and underwhelming performance from newer games.

Psychological Strategies Help Survive PvP in Arc Raiders

In the multiplayer shooter Arc Raiders, players often find themselves downed in combat with limited defensive options. One unconventional but effective approach to surviving after being taken down is psychological tactics. Reports from players show pleading for mercy or "trauma dumping" about real-life struggles can sometimes persuade opponents to spare and revive them.

Alternatively, players may offer or bluff about valuable in-game items as bribes to avoid being finished off and looted completely. These informal social strategies supplement game mechanics such as defibrillators or special augments that extend the window for revival or escape, highlighting the emergent player behaviors that arise in PvP encounters.

Philips and AOC Announce Gaming Monitors with Up To 1,000 Hz Refresh Rates

Philips and AOC have unveiled new 27-inch gaming monitors, the Philips Evnia 27M2N5500XD and AOC AGON Pro AGP277QK, which feature 500 Hz native refresh rates at 1440p resolution but can switch to an alternative 1080p mode claiming up to 1,000 Hz. The panels likely utilize an advanced form of IPS technology, given the 1ms response time claims and 2,000:1 contrast ratio, which is higher than standard IPS but lower than typical VA panels.

These models represent one of several attempts by manufacturers to push refresh rates beyond what most gamers currently utilize. However, image quality at the 1,000 Hz mode may degrade due to non-integer scaling and hardware limitations. The practical performance gains of such ultra-high refresh rates remain debatable outside niche contexts like esports.

Crafting and Using New Items in Minecraft

Several Minecraft features continue to receive player interest, including crafting recipes and gameplay mechanics. Netherite horse armor is now craftable by combining diamond horse armor with a netherite ingot at a smithing table, offering high defense and additional benefits for zombie horses like protection from sunlight damage.

The game also introduced spears, crafted diagonally with two sticks and a material, available in multiple variants including wood, stone, copper, iron, gold, diamond, and netherite. Spears have a longer reach than swords and offer both a jab and a charged attack with knockback effects, providing new combat options.

Additionally, the Nautilus, a new underwater mount from the Mounts of Mayhem update, can be found in ocean biomes below level 38 and tamed by feeding it pufferfish. Once tamed, it can be saddled for faster underwater travel and grants temporary underwater breathing benefits.
