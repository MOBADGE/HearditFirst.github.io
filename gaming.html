PC Gamer Hardware Awards Spotlight: Best Motherboards of 2025

The 2025 hardware scene features a variety of motherboards catering to AMD and Intel's latest CPUs launched last year. Mainstream and high-end chipsets have matured with features now commonly including Wi-Fi 7, multiple M.2 SSD slots, robust rear I/O connectivity such as Thunderbolt 4 and high-speed USB ports, and improved BIOS/UEFI interfaces.

Three motherboards have emerged as top nominees:

- **ASRock B860 Steel Legend WiFi**: A budget-friendly option under $200, with four M.2 slots and solid connectivity but limited to Wi-Fi 6E instead of Wi-Fi 7.

- **Gigabyte X870 Aorus Elite WiFi7 Ice**: Offering a balanced feature set for about $260, it supports multiple PCIe 5.0 M.2 slots and extensive rear I/O ports, including USB 10 Gbps.

- **MSI MAG Z890 Tomahawk WiFi**: Priced slightly higher, this board includes Wi-Fi 7 and Thunderbolt 4 ports, with convenient quick-release mechanisms for installation ease.

These nominations reflect a focus on value while providing current technologies for gaming and expandability.

PC Gamer Hardware Awards Highlight: Best Gaming CPUs of 2025

The CPU market in 2025 has been relatively quiet after significant processor launches from AMD and Intel in 2024. Intel's Core Ultra 200S lineup arrived last October, with next-generation mobile processors (Panther Lake) expected in early 2026.
