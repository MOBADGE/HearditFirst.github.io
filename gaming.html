Cities: Skylines 2 Update Focuses on Improving Citizen Death Mechanics

The development of *Cities: Skylines 2* has shifted to Iceflake Studios following a change from the previous developer, Colossal Order. Iceflake released their first patch, *First Frost*, which addresses fundamental issues with the in-game death system. Previously, citizens died en masse during early morning hours, and a bug in easy mode caused approximately 80% of citizens to avoid dying of old age entirely. The patch fixes these problems by spreading out death events throughout the day and removing immortal citizens in easy mode.

Additional changes include reducing bicycle trip volume by 80%, introducing new UI icons for various road types and infrastructure, and improving graphical effects like weather-based fog and shadows. The update also enables autosave by default, a longstanding feature request. While not adding new gameplay features, these fixes have been positively received and may help improve the game‚Äôs overall player experience following a difficult launch.
