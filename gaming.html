Wasteland Bites: A Post-Apocalyptic Food Truck Simulator

Wasteland Bites is a forthcoming simulation game set in a post-apocalyptic universe where players run a food truck catering to mutants, monsters, and even aliens. High on Life 2 Review: A Mixed Reception for the FPS Sequel

High on Life 2, released February 13, offers a singleplayer first-person shooter with humor and surreal storytelling. The sequel takes a different tone following the departure of co-founder Justin Roiland, balancing crude jokes with moments of genuine narrative engagement. The game includes diverse mission types, such as a murder mystery segment, aiming to keep gameplay varied.

However, the combat mechanics and movement options received criticism for lacking depth and excitement, with weapon choices feeling redundant and the skateboard-based mobility leaving some players wanting. Technical issues affecting frame rates and bugs were also noted at launch. Despite flaws, High on Life 2 is recognized for its creativity and willingness to experiment within the FPS genre, providing a distinct if uneven experience.
