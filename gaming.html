Birdwatching Notebook Offers a Virtual Space for Relaxed Bird Observation

Birdwatching Notebook is a new idler game that simulates the experience of birdwatching from a decorated balcony. Players attract birds by placing items and food, and then collect treasures the birds bring for sale to fund further decoration and unlock more species. The game encourages careful observation and photography of various bird types, creating a calm and engaging loop despite its simplicity. The current demo is limited to a single location and is free to play, filling a niche for those seeking a low-pressure simulation of nature observation. Birdwatching Notebook could serve as a digital alternative for enthusiasts unable to get outdoors.
