**Cyberpunk 2077 VR Mod Removed Following Legal Action**

The popular R.E.A.L. VR mod for Cyberpunk 2077, created by modder Luke Ross, has been taken down after developer CD Projekt issued a DMCA takedown notice. The mod enabled VR support for Cyberpunk 2077 and multiple other games, but was distributed behind a Patreon paywall. CD Projekt's fan content guidelines prohibit commercial use of mods, allowing only reasonable donations rather than paid subscriptions. Ross expressed frustration with "corporate logic" that requires modders to offer content for free, especially when companies do not provide official VR versions themselves. The removal follows similar actions from publishers like Take-Two, which issued DMCA claims against the same mod for their games. Some community members have criticized both CD Projekt for the aggressive takedown and Ross for trying to profit directly from the mod. The incident highlights ongoing tensions between mod creators monetizing their work and publishers protecting intellectual property and revenue models.
