**Stephen King Adaptations: Strong on Screen, Sparse in Games**

This amounts to the most significant wave of King-related media since 2017. However, despite this renewed interest and the popularity of co-op horror games such as *Lethal Company* and *Sons of the Forest*, there remains a surprising lack of official Stephen King-based video games. Historically, only a few minor titles (mostly from the 1980s and 1990s), including games tied to *The Mist* and *The Dark Half*, have been released. Industry observers suggest this is a missed opportunity given King's extensive catalogue, and have proposed various game concepts inspired by his works. Whether this gap will be addressed remains to be seen.
