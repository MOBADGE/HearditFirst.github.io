**Valve Attributes Steam Deck OLED Shortages to Memory Crisis**

Valve has confirmed that shortages of the Steam Deck OLED model stem from ongoing global memory (RAM and storage) supply issues affecting many in the electronics industry. Memory and storage components have become scarce and expensive due to high demand from the AI sector, leading to intermittent out-of-stock situations. This scarcity has also delayed hardware launches and increased prices for other major companies like Sony and Nintendo.

As of now, Steam Deck OLED units remain available in markets such as the UK and Australia, but sales in the US are currently sold out. Valve also noted that the Steam Machine and Steam Frame hardware products face similar challenges, requiring the company to reassess release timelines and pricing. The shortage reflects broader supply chain pressures caused by AI-driven demand for large memory capacities in new GPUs and servers.
