New Voice Actress Announced for Lara Croft in Upcoming Tomb Raider Titles

Amazon has confirmed that Alix Wilton Regan will be the voice of Lara Croft for two forthcoming Tomb Raider games: a remake of the original titled *Legacy of Atlantis* and a new adventure called *Tomb Raider: Catalyst*. Regan, an experienced voice actor, has a broad portfolio including roles in *Cyberpunk 2077*, *Dragon Age*, *Assassin's Creed: Origins*, and *Lies of P*. She replaces Camilla Luddington, who voiced Lara in the previous trilogy of Tomb Raider games. Both new titles are scheduled for release in 2026 and 2027 respectively, with *Legacy of Atlantis* now available for wishlisting on Steam. This casting signals a fresh direction in voice performance as the franchise enters its next phase.

Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2 Loses at Game Awards but Maintains Good Spirits
