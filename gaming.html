Psychological Horror Game Luto Explores Grief Through P.T.-Style Ambience

The 2025 horror game *Luto* has drawn attention for its unique blend of narrative style reminiscent of *The Stanley Parable* and atmospheric horror similar to *P.T.* Players take control of Sam, trapped inside a house where escape attempts are mysteriously thwarted and days pass disjointedly. The game uses environmental storytelling and an antagonistic narrator to evoke feelings of detachment and loss, simulating the experience of grief over about five to six hours of gameplay. Unlike more jump scare-heavy titles, *Luto* focuses on creating tense dread and existential confusion, making it a notable entry in psychological horror. It is available now on Steam.
