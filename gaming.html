**Path of Exile Updates Long-Standing Endgame Atlas System**

Grinding Gear Games is releasing a sizable update to Path of Exile's Atlas of Worlds endgame system in the upcoming Mirage expansion, arriving March 6. The Atlas, a 10-year-old progression and map-running framework, will see key changes inspired by Path of Exile 2's newer design without abandoning its core structure. Instead of needing specific keys to access set maps, players will use generic keys to unlock nodes on the Atlas correlating to maps, simplifying progression and increasing player choice in which maps to run.

Additionally, new "astrolabe" items will introduce rifts that guarantee particular modifiers across sections of the Atlas, increasing difficulty and rewards. These rifts bring in league mechanics and encourage deeper character growth. This rework aims to clarify progression and offer more strategic options without overcomplicating the long-favored system. The PoE 2 endgame overhaul will follow later in April.
