Baldur's Gate TV Series in Development at HBO

HBO is developing a TV series based on the Baldur's Gate universe, set directly after the events of Baldur's Gate 3. The show will focus on a new group of adventurers while incorporating characters from Baldur's Gate 3. Craig Mazin, known for his work on HBO's *The Last of Us* and *Chernobyl*, is leading the project and has expressed his long-standing appreciation for Dungeons & Dragons and the Baldur's Gate RPGs.

This series primarily draws from the world and lore of Baldur's Gate 3 developed by Larian Studios, rather than the earlier BioWare titles. The original games are considered unofficial source material for the show. This development is notable as Larian Studios has shifted away from D&D and is now focusing on its own Divinity series. Meanwhile, Wizards of the Coast, owner of D&D, controls the Baldur's Gate 3 characters and setting.

The importance of this show lies in its direct continuation of a popular and recent RPG, potentially expanding the franchise‚Äôs reach beyond gaming audiences to television viewers, and deepening narrative content connected to the new game.

Nioh 3: Key Skills for Boss Fights

For players finding Nioh 3's bosses challenging, two skills, *Supreme Parry* and *Enduring Destruction*, are recommended. *Supreme Parry* restores health upon successfully performing a Burst Break‚Äîa counter to powerful enemy attacks‚Äîhelping sustain players in difficult fights without excessive reliance on healing items. It can be found in a chest early in the Hamamatsu Castle Town Crucible.

*Enduring Destruction* increases damage dealt to enemies following a successful Burst Break, purchased from a merchant near the Crucible entrance at the cost of in-game currency called Yokai Teardrops. The synergy of these skills enhances survivability and damage output during boss encounters, making them practical choices during progression.

Since both skills apply across combat styles, they provide flexible benefits regardless of player preference for Samurai or Ninja modes.

Nioh 3 Boss Guides: Tips for Yamagata Masakage and Jakotsu-baba

Two key boss fights in Nioh 3 come with practical advice:

- **Yamagata Masakage**: The fight emphasizes mastery of the Burst Break mechanic. Players need to counter his red-highlighted attacks to deplete his Ki stamina, allowing for a grapple to deal extra damage. Using Ninja mode can help due to better mobility and faster attacks that maintain pressure. Awareness of his grab and slam attacks is crucial for successful dodging.

- **Jakotsu-baba**: Appearing in the Hamamatsu Castle Crucible, this boss's attacks focus on Burst Break countering and avoiding damage to limit health reduction from the Crucible environment. Preparation includes acquiring *Supreme Parry*, *Enduring Destruction*, and strategic use of Guardian abilities and Living Artifact mode. Recognizing key attacks like grab attempts, slams, and elemental breath helps avoid damage and exploit openings.

These guides provide step-by-step combat advice which aids players navigating Nioh 3‚Äôs demanding encounters.

Unlocking Core Nioh 3 Mechanics: Parry, Respec, and Skill Capacity

- **Parry Ability**: Players can unlock the parry skill early by acquiring the *Deflect* skill from the Samurai skill tree after completing the prologue. Parrying in Nioh 3 differs from similar games by offering Ki and Arts regeneration without draining enemy stamina. It provides a defensive advantage but does not allow following up with a stagger or grapple.

- **Respeccing Builds**: Nioh 3 lets players reset their stat and skill allocations freely after reaching the open-world section post-prologue. Respec can be done at shrines to reassign stats spent with Amrita and through menu options to redistribute Samurai and Ninja skill points gained from special collectible locks.

- **Increasing Skill Capacity**: The number of slottable skills is capped by separate capacities for general, Samurai, and Ninja skills. These capacities improve incrementally by collecting texts and memorandums found via exploration, trading with merchants, or completing activities. This system encourages ongoing exploration and engagement with side content to enhance build options.

Understanding these systems enables players to experiment with different combat styles and adapt their approaches throughout the game.
