**Menace Revitalizes Turn-Based Tactics with Early Access Release** Menace, a new early access title from Overhype Studios, aims to reinvigorate the turn-based tactics genre. Combining elements from games like Jagged Alliance and X-Com with tabletop strategy conventions, Menace puts players in charge of a diminished space marine platoon stranded in a hostile planetary system. The game emphasizes squad-level tactics involving morale and suppression, where soldiers react realistically under fire and missions conclude after specific objectives rather than total enemy elimination. Despite being early in development, the game already offers detailed customization, meaningful tactical choices, and a promising blend of fast-paced strategy and simulation elements. Its structured supply system and mission design set it apart by requiring players to adapt continuously based on resources and squad readiness. Menace is available on Steam as an early access title.
