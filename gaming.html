**Random Heart Attacks Mod Adds Sudden Death to Red Dead Redemption 2**

The mod adds a surprising and sudden threat to the game, reflecting the historic reality that the main character, Arthur Morgan, being in his late 30s, could realistically face such a risk in 1899. While the motivation of the modder is not clearly explained, this addition could be seen as accelerating the character's inevitable fate and adds a new unpredictable element to the game. This mod joins a recent trend of unusual Red Dead Redemption 2 mods that transform the game into unexpected experiences, including versions inspired by Katamari Damacy and Super Monkey Ball.
