Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 Expands with Free DLC Following Award Success

Following its sweep at The Game Awards 2025, where it won nine awards including Game of the Year, *Clair Obscur: Expedition 33* has received a substantial free DLC update called the "Thank you" update. Total War: Warhammer 40,000 Confirmed, Fans Share Wishlist

Creative Assembly officially announced *Total War: Warhammer 40,000*, a new entry that will bring the grimdark universe of the Warhammer 40k setting to the popular strategy series. Among anticipated features are customizable army aesthetics, integration of cover and urban combat, epic endgame crises like warp storms or invasions, and inclusion of massive units such as Titans and Imperial Knights. Space and fleet battles are also important elements many fans expect to see incorporated given 40k's emphasis on interstellar warfare.
