**Menace Revitalizes Turn-Based Tactics with Early Access Launch**

After years of relatively stagnant offerings in turn-based tactical games, *Menace*, from the creators of *Battle Brothers*, has emerged as a promising new entry currently in early access. The game combines strategic campaign progression with faster-paced tactical battles reminiscent of *Jagged Alliance* and *X-Com*, enriched by influences from tabletop wargaming. Players command a team of space marines on a doomed mission in a hostile star system, balancing limited resources, morale, and combat suppression mechanics. Despite only featuring about a third of its planned content, *Menace* has been praised for its streamlined yet deep systems, extensive weapon and squad customization, and meaningful tactical choices. Developer Overhype Studios continues active updates to address early access issues. This title offers strategy enthusiasts a fresh alternative to the slowly evolving tactics genre.

**World of Warcraft to Delay Balance Changes After Midnight Expansion Launch**

With major class changes and the removal of many long-standing combat addons, Blizzard intends to gather early player data before making adjustments, reducing the frequency of patches during critical endgame periods. The first balance update is scheduled for March 17, coinciding with the start of the new season, with a follow-up patch planned just before the initial raid opens. This cautious approach aims to maintain a fair and stable environment for both casual players and the competitive raid scene.
