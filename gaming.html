World of Warcraft Played Using Hot Dog Controllers

A player known as Addison2k recently completed a mythic keystone run in World of Warcraft using custom controllers made from hot dogs. The devices were 3D-printed plastic grips wired to register touch input from inserted hot dogs, one for movement and another for abilities. While playable as a retribution paladin, the setup imposed challenges, such as inability to control the camera and having to use the keyboard for a tab-targeting keybind. Despite the quirky limitation, the run was successful, illustrating creative alternative controls in gaming. This event is notable as an example of accessibility innovation and community-driven experimentation, though it remains a novelty rather than a practical input method.

Arc Raiders’ Player Base Shows Balanced Interest in PvE and PvP

Embark Studios revealed that about 30% of Arc Raiders players focus solely on PvE content, while another 30% prioritize PvP, with the remaining 40% enjoying both modes. The developers expressed pleasant surprise at the passionate PvE-only community, given that Arc Raiders is primarily an action shooter designed for co-operation and competitive play. Features like proximity chat and boss enemies such as the Matriarch encourage teamwork, and players quickly adapted to unite against challenges, demonstrating a cooperative spirit in a game with inherent PvP elements. The game's thematic emphasis on nature recovering after ecological collapse also aligns with a hopeful community atmosphere, distinguishing its social dynamics from other survival games.

Euro Truck Simulator 2 Expands With New Türkiye Map DLC

SCS Software announced "Soul of Anatolia," a new downloadable content pack for Euro Truck Simulator 2, introducing an extensive map expansion covering the eastern region of Türkiye. Players will traverse landmarks including the 1915 Çanakkale Bridge—the longest suspension bridge globally—the capital Ankara, and popular tourist cities such as Antalya and Alanya. This update follows two major DLCs released late last year and extends the simulator’s geographic scope. The addition of Türkiye offers new routes and environments for players, reinforcing Euro Truck Simulator 2’s longevity and appeal as a relaxing exploration title. No release date has been specified yet.

PC Gaming Revenue Increasingly Driven by Titles Outside the Top 20

Industry analysis from Newzoo highlights a significant shift in PC gaming revenue distribution. In Western markets, over 56% of revenue now comes from games ranked outside the top 20 in sales, up from 48% in the previous year. Additionally, players are spending more time on these less prominent titles, with playtime growing 44% compared to a flat or slightly declining engagement with top-tier games. Established catalogue games such as Cyberpunk 2077, Elden Ring, and Skyrim continue to attract substantial attention alongside survival and action RPG genres that benefit from ongoing updates and balance changes. The findings emphasize the economic relevance of mid-tier and long-tail games on PC, contrasting with console markets where blockbuster exclusives and annualized sports franchises hold stronger dominance.

Gloomwood Receives Official Controller Support Update

Gloomwood, a stealth-oriented immersive sim available in early access since 2022, has introduced comprehensive controller support with its latest update. The addition facilitates better play on Steam Deck and other handheld devices, resolving previous reliance on community-made controller layouts. Updated tutorials and customizable keybindings accompany the feature. The update also adds a new in-game shop location, expanding gameplay content. Gloomwood’s positive reception and frequent updates make this controller support notable for players seeking an accessible, nuanced stealth experience beyond traditional keyboard and mouse setups.

Classic Indie Metroidvania Cave Story+ Gets Major PC Update After 22 Years

After over two decades since its original release, Cave Story+ received a significant update on PC to align features with the Nintendo Switch version introduced nine years ago. The patch adds two-player local co-op, widescreen support, enhanced visuals including animated dialogue portraits, and more soundtrack options. Mod support has also been implemented, focusing primarily on cosmetic modifications rather than new levels. Although the game’s difficulty remains challenging in places, the update renews interest in this influential indie metroidvania, recognized for shaping the genre and demonstrating the viability of solo-developed titles. Cave Story+ remains relevant both for its historical significance and as a playable platformer today.
