**Outlook on PC Hardware in 2026**

Despite ongoing challenges such as persistent memory shortages and high RAM prices, the PC hardware sector offers several developments for 2026. A notable trend is the increasing adoption of Tunnel Magnetoresistance (TMR) technology in peripherals, promising consistent response and lower power consumption, especially in gaming keyboards. However, major manufacturers like Cherry and Razer are cautious, focusing on their existing technologies.

Valve plans launches for three new products: the Steam Machine, Steam Frame VR headset, and a new Steam Controller, expected later in the year. The Steam Frame, powered by an Arm chip and running SteamOS, marks a shift toward wireless, lightweight VR hardware.

Meanwhile, the Arm architecture is gaining attention with devices like the Steam Frame adopting Snapdragon chips and enhanced Windows on Arm support via emulation layers.
