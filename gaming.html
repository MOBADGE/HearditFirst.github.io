How to Complete "A Toxic Trail" Quest in Arc Raiders

The Arc Raiders quest "A Toxic Trail" involves revisiting a location from a prior mission: the water intake drain near the Water Treatment Control building. Players begin by locating a toxic barrel in this area but unlike the earlier quest, they must then track down the origin of the spill. This requires finding a partially submerged truck northwest of the barrel location, photographing it, and searching it for clues. Interacting with the truck reveals a key item, the Spaceport Warehouse Key, which may be important for later tasks. Players are advised against using a free loadout for this quest to avoid losing crucial items. This walkthrough helps clarify steps that can be confusing in this multi-stage mission.
