Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles Join Magic: The Gathering

Wizards of the Coast announced a new Magic: The Gathering crossover set focused on the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (TMNT). This set will revisit New York City, featuring a pre-constructed Commander deck inspired by TMNT video games, with cards named Game Over, Level Up, and Arcade Cabinet. The set includes pixel art that evokes arcade nostalgia, alongside new mechanics like Sneak and Disappear, offering fresh gameplay strategies reminiscent of previous Magic mechanics.

Additionally, the set offers a Turtle Team-Up box for cooperative play, allowing up to four players to team up as individual Turtles to fight bosses. This aims to provide an accessible way for new players to learn Magic. The set also revives the Alliance mechanic from the Avatar: The Last Airbender series, emphasizing teamwork‚Äîa central TMNT theme. Cards with art by TMNT co-creator Kevin Eastman are included, appealing to fans of the original comics. This crossover marks another example of Magic expanding its Universes Beyond series, blending popular IPs with established card game mechanics to broaden appeal and provide novel deck-building options.
