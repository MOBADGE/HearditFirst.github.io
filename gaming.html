Cleared Hot Update Enhances Tactical Helicopter Gameplay and Port Support

The helicopter action game *Cleared Hot*, a spiritual successor to the classic *Desert Strike*, is preparing its next update titled Chapter 1B, due on March 17. Developed by Not Knowing Corporation, the update focuses on enhancing core gameplay mechanics and upgrade support for the Steam Deck handheld. Notably, it improves the winch grapple mechanic that allows players to catch enemy missiles midair and return them with increased damage, tripling their usual impact. Other additions include optional arcade missions such as survival and minigolf-style challenges, and a new water system that adds splash effects when debris interacts with bodies of water. While additional features like new helicopters and more customization were planned, they were postponed to prioritize campaign content. The game remains in Steam Early Access with future chapters, including a rainforest-themed environment, planned for later.
