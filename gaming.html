Minecraft Updates: New Crafting Guides for Players

Several new features have been added to Minecraft‚Äôs Mounts of Mayhem update, with detailed crafting and usage guides for players interested in expanding their gameplay.

**Netherite Horse Armor** Netherite horse armor offers one of the strongest protections available for horses. To craft it, players first need diamond horse armor, a netherite ingot, and a netherite upgrade, all combined at a smithing table. Apart from providing 19 armor points and additional toughness and knockback resistance, when equipped on a zombie horse it also prevents burning in sunlight. Acquiring the materials involves venturing into the Nether to harvest ancient debris for netherite scraps and exploring Bastions guarded by Piglins, which contain netherite upgrade packs. The durability of netherite horse armor is notable for being unbreakable, removing the need for frequent replacements.

**Crafting and Using the Spear** The spear, another addition from Mounts of Mayhem, is a melee weapon designed for longer reach combat. Crafting requires two sticks placed diagonally and a chosen material such as wood, stone, copper, iron, gold, diamond, or netherite. The netherite variant requires upgrading a diamond spear at a smithing table using a netherite ingot and upgrade. The spear introduces two attack types: a quick jab and a charged thrust that can knock enemies and mounts back. Additionally, the spear has a longer effective range than other weapons, extending to 4.5 blocks, making it useful for mounted combat and encounters at a slight distance.

**Taming the Nautilus Underwater Mount** Nautilus is a new underwater mount found in all ocean biomes below level 38, often appearing in small groups. Taming requires feeding it pufferfish or buckets of pufferfish. Once tamed and saddled, the Nautilus provides faster underwater travel and grants a temporary ‚ÄúBreath of the Nautilus‚Äù effect, which pauses the depletion of the player‚Äôs oxygen meter. Players can breed Nautilus using any type of fish, and raising a baby Nautilus involves feeding it ten fish before it automatically becomes tamed. The mount must remain underwater at all times, so players should plan aquatic bases or docks accordingly.
