The addition provides a more hectic option with increased player counts but is not intended to replace the original format. Project: Gorgon MMO Launches 1.0 with Extensive New Content

Project: Gorgon, an old-school style MMO known for its minimal handholding and deep skill system, has officially launched version 1.0 on Steam, exiting early access. The update introduces a large new area called Statehelm, described by the developer as the largest and most complex map yet. It serves as a hub for max-level players and includes 200 new quests. Other improvements include new character models, changes to combat experience systems, crafting enhancements, and economy tweaks. A demo and launch sale are available for new players interested in this nostalgic take on the MMO genre.
