Epic Games Store to Add Forums and Undergo Technical Overhaul

Epic Games Store is shifting focus to enhance user experience with new social features and a major technical update planned for later this year. The store plans to trial community forums centered around popular games, alongside adding player profiles, avatars, private messaging, voice chat, and cross-game party systems. This marks a change from previous Epic CEO statements that rejected the idea of internal forums.

Alongside social features, Epic is undertaking a deep technical overhaul of the Epic Games Launcher on PC to address speed and responsiveness issues. The goal is to provide a smoother, faster experience when accessing libraries and navigating the store. Epic continues to emphasize mitigating review bombing with its relatively controlled approach to user ratings and social tools, differentiating itself from platforms like Steam.
