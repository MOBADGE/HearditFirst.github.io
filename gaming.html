Hytale Launches Early Access, Becomes Most-Watched Game on Twitch

Hytale, a voxel-based sandbox RPG that has been in development for nearly a decade, officially launched its early access version on January 13. The game quickly surged to become the most viewed title on Twitch, with over 420,000 concurrent viewers across thousands of streams. Developer Hypixel shared that pre-purchases secured funding for two more years of development. Players have reported challenging early encounters, including aggressive wildlife threats. Some launch-day issues have emerged, such as server overload causing delays in account verification and game crashes, which the developers are actively addressing. The high initial interest suggests a strong player base eager to explore the game's world and features.

Judge Denies Interim Pay for Fired Rockstar GTA 6 Developers

A Scottish employment tribunal has denied interim relief for over 30 former Rockstar employees dismissed in October 2025 amid unionization efforts. The tribunal ruled that the fired workers did not sufficiently prove a likely unfair dismissal based on union membership at this stage of the case. Rockstar maintains the firings were justified, citing allegations of gross misconduct and leaks of confidential information. However, the union representing the workers criticized the dismissals as harsh union-busting. The decision to deny interim pay does not affect the ongoing legal process, which will address the core claims of unfair dismissal in a full hearing expected later. The case has drawn political attention and public protests in support of the dismissed employees.
