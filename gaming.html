Medieval MMO Gloria Victis to Return as Free-to-Play

*Gloria Victis*, a medieval MMO that shut down in 2023 after financial difficulties and developer burnout, is making a comeback under new ownership. The game originally offered a focus on realistic siege warfare and open-world PvP driven by player choices rather than scripted encounters.
