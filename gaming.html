Ultrakill Reaches Eighth Layer of Hell Next Week

New Blood Interactive announced that the popular early access FPS *Ultrakill* will add its eighth layer of Hell, named Fraud, next week. The update was originally planned for late 2025 but faced a short delay. While the exact release day remains unconfirmed, the developer encouraged fans to prepare for the launch. Each Hell layer in *Ultrakill* features a distinct visual style; Fraud will focus on themes of illusion and funhouse-like environments. This new addition brings the game closer to its full release, with one final ninth layer, Treachery, still to come. The update coincides with New Blood's anniversary sale starting February 23, making it a reasonable time to buy for newcomers.

People of Note Demo Offers Musical RPG with Pun-Laden Humor

*People of Note*, a turn-based RPG centered on music and genre fusion, recently released a demo showcasing its second chapter. Players follow Cadence, an aspiring pop star challenging musical snobbery in the rock city of Durandis by recruiting band members and fusing styles. The demo is notable for its comedic puns, including a pet "accorgion" (accordion corgi), along with hidden owl characters that turn battles into quizzes. Mechanically, combat is straightforward, mixing traditional turn-based elements with some rhythm-based timing. While the soundtrack is unmemorable overall, a standout duet performance adds charm. Some players may find the included puzzles repetitive, though an option exists to disable them, allowing players to tailor the experience to their preferences.
