Steam Winter Sale 2025 Begins with Discounts on Major Titles

The 2025 Steam Winter Sale is now live, offering discounts on several recent big releases including PC Gamer's Game of the Year, Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2, which is available at half price for the first time. Other notable titles on sale include Marvel's Spider-Man 2, Borderlands 4, Dying Light: The Beast, and Hades 2. The sale runs through January 2, 2026, and also features the opportunity to vote in the Steam Awards across 11 categories. While Steam is the main event for holiday discounts, alternatives like the Epic Games Store and GOG also have their own sales and giveaways running concurrently.

Fallout 76's Burning Springs Expansion Explores a New Region

Fallout 76's latest expansion, Burning Springs, has introduced a new desert-inspired region in Ohio, contrasting with the game's usual Appalachian setting. The expansion draws creative influence from earlier Fallout titles, featuring new locales such as Athens and Dino Peaks mini golf, alongside themed enemies like deathclaws and raiders. According to lead producer Bill LaCoste, each area in Fallout 76 is designed to have a distinct environment, effectively turning the game world into a kind of theme park. The update also ties in with the ongoing Fallout TV series, adding narrative depth and fresh exploration opportunities for players.

World of Warcraft to Whitelist Spells for Combat Addons

Blizzard is adjusting its stance on combat addons in World of Warcraft by beginning to whitelist select spells, allowing addon creators to access certain data again. This move comes after a major overhaul that restricted addons capable of providing competitive advantages. The whitelist currently includes spells related to resource tracking and cooldowns for classes like Demon Hunters. Blizzard encourages player feedback on which spells should be added next. Observers suggest this may be a temporary measure while improved official UI elements are developed to address player needs without compromising game balance.

Guide: Completing the Goblin King Quests in The Forge (Roblox)

Players tackling The Forge on Roblox can undertake a series of five Goblin King quests, each requiring gold and increasing amounts of rare ores such as Topaz, Diamonds, Emeralds, and Rubies. Successfully completing all five quests rewards players with a key that unlocks the Goblin Cave, a new area featuring unique resources and a side quest involving a lost cat. Accessing the final quest requires a pickaxe dealing at least 78 damage, which necessitates investing in higher-tier equipment.
