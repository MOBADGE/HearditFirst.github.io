Dawn of War 4 Playtesters Prefer Longer Combat to Enjoy Synchronised Animations

In recent closed alpha tests of *Dawn of War 4*, developer King Art Games received feedback asking for combat to last longer. The reason: players enjoy watching the game's enhanced sync-kill and melee animations, which go beyond what earlier titles in the series offered. Senior game designer Elliott Verbiest shared that players found these animations so compelling they wanted to see more of them in action.

This indicates that the game's visual storytelling during battle is a key part of the experience, not just the tactical gameplay. Additionally, Verbiest explained design changes made for multiplayer balance, such as modifying how the Necron faction’s resurrection ability works. Instead of instantly reviving units (which felt imbalanced), revivals are tied to a specific building, adding a delay and providing opponents an opportunity to counter.

The game will also feature characters exclusive to the singleplayer campaign, such as the Dark Angels' primarch Lion El’Jonson, emphasizing a separation between campaign and multiplayer to maintain balance. *Dawn of War 4* is slated for release in 2026.

Jeff Kaplan Reflects on His Departure from Activision-Blizzard

Jeff Kaplan, co-creator and former public face of *Overwatch*, spoke openly about his exit from Activision-Blizzard during a 2026 interview with the Lex Fridman podcast. Kaplan attributed his departure to the overwhelming pressure and misalignment between the demands of the *Overwatch* game development team and the expectations of investors, especially surrounding the *Overwatch League* esports venture.

He described how the league, hyped as potentially bigger than the NFL, placed unrealistic demands on the studio. These expectations led to development resources being diverted from core game content like new heroes and events, focusing instead on features tied to the league, such as team skins and spectator tools. Financial pressure increased to hit revenue targets, leading to difficult mandates, including an ultimatum from the company’s CFO that failure to meet revenue goals would result in mass layoffs, which Kaplan cited as a turning point.

Kaplan expressed disillusionment with the changing priorities at Blizzard and noted he once thought he would retire there, underscoring the impact of corporate pressures on creative teams. Since Kaplan’s departure in 2021, *Overwatch 2* eventually dropped its subtitle and returned to being *Overwatch* after a lukewarm launch and removal of key features.

Humble Bundle Offers Nine Classic and Modern CRPGs for Around $23

The latest Humble Bundle titled "Beamdog & Owlcat RPG Masters" includes nine computer role-playing games and several expansions, available for slightly over $20. Notable titles in the package include the classic *Baldur's Gate* series (including *Baldur's Gate*, *Baldur's Gate 2*), *Planescape: Torment*, *Icewind Dale* (all enhanced editions), as well as *Pathfinder: Wrath of the Righteous* and *Pathfinder: Kingmaker* with their season passes.

This deal exemplifies a significant value for players interested in CRPGs, offering hundreds of hours of gameplay. While most of these titles have strong reputations, the bundle also includes *Mythforce*, a first-person roguelite shooter from Beamdog, representing a departure from the primarily RPG-focused collection.

Buyers can opt for tiers starting from $5 for a subset of games, with the full package at $23. This bundle presents an opportunity for both newcomers and fans to acquire a robust selection of classic and modern RPGs at a discounted rate.

Blood Bowl Update to Arrive as Separate Game, Free for Current Owners

Cyanide Studios announced that the next update to its *Blood Bowl 3* digital adaptation will launch as a separate title called *Warhammer Blood Bowl*. Players who already own *Blood Bowl 3* will receive this new game without additional cost, keeping their existing accounts and purchased content.

The new game will incorporate the latest rules from the tabletop version and add two new teams: the Tomb Kings and Bretonnians, bringing the total team roster to 26. Key additions include a new "Rumble mode," featuring seven-player teams on a smaller pitch for faster-paced matches.
