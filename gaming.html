**Holiday FPS Mods Bring Festive Flair to Classic Shooters**

As the holiday season unfolds, the FPS modding community has crafted a variety of Christmas-themed add-ons for popular shooters. These mods infuse familiar games like Doom 2, Quake, and Half-Life with festive visuals and challenges. For example, "Doom Christmas" replaces in-game enemies and weapons with holiday-inspired versions, such as Santa hats on foes and shotgun Santas with unique death animations. Another highlight is "The Twelve Days of Doommas," a challenging Doom 2 level pack featuring themed enemy counts and level elements tied to the classic carol.

Quake fans can explore "Twisted Christmas," a mod set in an abandoned mall filled with holiday-themed enemies, while "Arcane Dimensions' Xmas Jam" offers a collection of Christmas-themed Quake maps created annually by modders. Even the stealth-focused Thief 2 has "A Very Thiefy Christmas Special," a mod rich with seasonal details and a narrative-driven experience.
