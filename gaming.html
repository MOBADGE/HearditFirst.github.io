How to Unlock New Mounts in Crimson Desert

Two new legendary mounts, the White Bear and the Silver Fang wolf, are available in Crimson Desert through specific quests and challenges. The White Bear can be found south of the White Wastes Sanctuary in northwest Pailune. Players need access to the Witch's Lair vendor, which is usually unlocked by Chapter 3, to craft a Sigil of Solidarity from the White Bear’s claws obtained after slaying and skinning it. This mount features combat abilities like multi-swipe attacks.

The Silver Fang wolf mount requires more extensive progress in the Hernand region. Players must complete several faction quests for House Celeste, House Roberts, House Alfonso, and others, culminating in the "Legendary Wolf" quest where the titular wolf is slain at the Howling Hollow. After killing and skinning Silver Fang, its fang can be used with the Witch's Lair vendor to craft the necessary Sigil of Solidarity to unlock the mount. Like White Bear, Silver Fang is also a battle-capable mount with strong attacks. These mounts enhance the gameplay by adding powerful rideable combat companions.

Pearl Abyss Advises Driver Rollback for Crimson Desert on ROG Xbox Ally X

Players running Crimson Desert on the ROG Xbox Ally X handheld have encountered issues launching the game with the latest GPU drivers. Pearl Abyss and Asus recommend rolling back to a previous AMD driver version (V32.0.21025.27003) available on Asus’ website until a fix is implemented. Microsoft and AMD are investigating the problem.

This issue highlights ongoing hardware compatibility challenges for Crimson Desert, which has previously struggled with Intel Arc GPU support. Although the game generally performs well on supported hardware, some users report graphical glitches. Developer efforts to address these technical problems will be important for ensuring broader and smoother accessibility across platforms.

Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 Surpasses Expectations

The indie RPG Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 achieved significant success after its 2025 release, much beyond the developer Sandfall's initial goals. The team originally aimed for an 85 Metacritic score, but the game received a 92, alongside strong user acclaim. The narrative and cinematic elements resonated strongly with players, contributing to the game's positive reception.

Expedition 33 sold over a million copies within three days of launch despite being available on Game Pass. This level of success was unexpected by the studio, reflecting both the quality of the game and effective engagement with its audience. The devs emphasize the importance of making games they themselves want to play, rather than purely aiming to please the market.

Hidden Free Gear in Marathon’s Priority Access Store

Marathon players might be missing out on some of the game’s best gear hidden within the Armoury’s Priority Access section. This section offers rotating daily free items from factions, including high-tier weapons and consumables like assault rifles and shotguns. Access requires completing initial faction quest blocks.

This feature allows players to obtain powerful weapons and items without risking their current gear or engaging in difficult encounters. Checking these free daily shops can help both new and veteran players better prepare for Ranked matches or challenging maps such as Cryo Archive.

Edible Thermal Paste Experiment Using Gold Leaf and Honey

A YouTuber created an edible thermal paste made from gold leaf and honey to test its effectiveness on CPU cooling. Gold’s thermal conductivity and honey’s adhesive properties were combined and applied to a CPU. Tests showed temperatures rising modestly—within acceptable limits during a ten-minute gaming session—though less efficient than standard thermal paste.

This experiment serves as an entertaining demonstration of thermal paste functions but is not a recommendation for PC maintenance. The honey-based paste is perishable and unsuitable for long-term use. Proper thermal paste remains essential to maintain optimal processor temperatures and system stability.

Disney Dreamlight Valley’s Next Update Adds Pocahontas

The update is likely to include nature-themed activities reflecting Pocahontas’s character, possibly involving new companions and collectible items. Players are encouraged to prepare inventory space in anticipation of new foraging materials and rewards linked to the new area.
