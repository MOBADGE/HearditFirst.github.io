Phantom Blade Zero Confirms September 2026 Release on PC and PS5

The action game *Phantom Blade Zero* from Chinese developer S-Game now has an official release date: September 9, 2026. The game will launch simultaneously on PlayStation 5 and PC, available on both Steam and Epic Games Store from day one.

The announcement came during The Game Awards, following earlier demos that impressed with their martial arts-inspired combat and branching world design. Despite comparisons to FromSoftware's titles, developers emphasize that *Phantom Blade Zero* is not a Soulslike; it lacks typical RPG systems like experience points or stat customization and instead leans toward fast, arcade-style action reminiscent of *Devil May Cry*.

The simultaneous PC and console launch is notable, given that Sony had been closely involved with promotion, raising expectations about cross-platform availability without timed exclusivity. The game has become one of the most anticipated action titles for next year due to its unique style and setting.
