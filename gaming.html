Crunch Returns at Naughty Dog Despite Industry Concerns

Naughty Dog, known for high-profile titles and a studio culture historically associated with intense crunch, is reportedly enforcing mandatory overtime again. According to a Bloomberg report, since late October staff have been required to work at least eight extra hours per week and return to full-time office presence to complete an internal demo for *Intergalactic: The Heretic Prophet*, a sci-fi game unveiled at The Game Awards 2024.

The mandatory overtime reportedly ended for most staff this week during demo finalization, but concerns remain about the intensity of crunch later in development. Naughty Dog acknowledged its crunch reputation in the past and pledged to reduce it, but as with other studios, implementation of such promises remains challenging.
