**Finding the Comet Igniter Enemy**

The Comet Igniter is a new enemy that drops a rare crafting material required for the Weather Monitoring System project. These large, rolling spherical foes are typically found in open spaces across various maps, such as the Plaza Rosa square in the Buried City and on the dam by the Control Tower in Dam Battlegrounds.

Comets aggressively pursue players before detonating in an area-of-effect explosion. They are recognizable by a single front light and a strip of illumination along their tops, which change color as they detect players. Players are advised to use heavy weapons like Ferro or Anvil firearms, or grenades, to dispatch Comets swiftly before they come close.

The drop rate for the Comet Igniter appears inconsistent. Sometimes the Comet explodes and leaves only a lootable metal ring, while other times it yields a core-like container as well. It is unclear whether this inconsistency is a bug or a game mechanic related to how the enemy is killed.

ASML Announces Advances That Could Boost Chip Production by 50%

ASML, the key supplier of extreme ultraviolet (EUV) lithography machines essential for manufacturing modern computer chips, has revealed a technological breakthrough expected to increase chip production capacity by approximately 50% by 2030.

The advancement focuses on boosting the power output of EUV light sources from 600 watts to 1,000 watts. Since EUV machines rely on extreme ultraviolet light to etch transistor patterns on silicon wafers, increasing light intensity allows wafers to be processed more rapidly without improving individual chip yields.

Currently, EUV light production involves complex processes, such as shooting molten tin droplets with powerful lasers, which produce the required photons. ASML‚Äôs improvement enables machines to process about 330 wafers per hour instead of the current 220.
