Arc Raiders Implements Behavior-Based Matchmaking

Developers of the multiplayer game Arc Raiders have confirmed that player behavior influences matchmaking. Observations from the player community suggested that aggressive players engaging in frequent PvP combat tend to be grouped in more hostile lobbies, while those avoiding conflict experience more cooperative matches. Robert Sammelin, art director at Embark, acknowledged the complexity of their matchmaking system and confirmed that behavioral analysis is part of the process. This approach aims to tailor player experiences and maintain a balance between PvP and PvE styles, improving overall engagement and fairness in lobbies. The system remains somewhat opaque, as developers continue to withhold detailed explanations on matchmaking criteria.
