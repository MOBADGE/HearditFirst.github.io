**Sins of a Solar Empire 2 Update Revamps Diplomacy and Economy Mechanics**

Ironclad Games issued a substantial update for *Sins of a Solar Empire 2* focusing on diplomacy and the economy, aiming to make wars more economically costly and diplomacy a more viable strategy. The update reworks AI behavior to promote alliances and forgiveness, while also improving tactical battlefield intelligence. Planets now generate resources based on population rather than automatically, meaning wartime devastation impacts long-term economic growth. Players can trade influence points and obtain intel through new diplomacy menus. The improvements respond to previous critiques that the diplomacy system felt superficial, enhancing strategic depth.
