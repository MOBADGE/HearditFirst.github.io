Personal Highlight: 8BitDo Ultimate 2C Wireless Controller

Among reviewed gaming gear, the 8BitDo Ultimate 2C wireless controller gained personal acclaim from a PC Gamer hardware editor. Despite a budget price around $30, it offers features often reserved for premium controllers, such as remappable buttons and Hall effect joysticks that resist stick drift. Its more compact form factor notably suits smaller hands better than many standard controllers. While it shares some construction drawbacks common to budget peripherals, such as creaky plastic under firm grip, it strikes a balance of function and affordability. A distinct lilac colorway also contributed to its appeal. This controller demonstrates that accessible gaming accessories can deliver satisfying experiences without a high price tag.

Desktop Pet Games: An Unexpected Cozy Trend of 2025

A noteworthy trend in cozy games during 2025 was the resurgence of desktop pet and partial-screen idler games. These titles evolved from simple animated companions on desktops into miniature idle games occupying small screen areas, providing low-effort entertainment during work or breaks. Examples include Ropuka's Idle Island, My Little Life, and Your Big, Cute Monster Farm, among others available individually or bundled on Steam. The trend fits current user behavior by allowing players to engage with light gameplay alongside primary tasks, echoing concepts like body doubling to boost productivity. This growth signals a niche but vibrant direction in cozy gaming that combines minimal demands with ongoing engagement.
