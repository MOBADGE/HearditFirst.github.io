Path of Exile 2 Exploit Enables Fast Profit in New Dungeon Mode

Players of Path of Exile 2 have found a way to accelerate progress in the game's latest feature, the Vaal Temples, introduced in the 0.4 update. This exploit involves keeping a character at a low level during the campaign, which is necessary since only low-level characters can benefit from the rewards. Grinding Gear Games (GGG), the developer, has not yet patched this method, likely due to their holiday break. This workaround highlights some frustrations with the complexity and randomness of the Vaal Temple system and raises questions about balancing and game design as the mode is considered for permanent inclusion.
