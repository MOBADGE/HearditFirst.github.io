**Baldur's Gate HBO Series Announced**

HBO is developing a new TV series set in the Baldur's Gate universe, continuing the story directly after the events of Baldur's Gate 3. The project is led by Craig Mazin, known for his work on the acclaimed The Last of Us series and the miniseries Chernobyl. Mazin, a longtime Dungeons & Dragons player, has invested significant time playing Baldur's Gate 3 and aims to blend new characters with familiar faces from the game.

The series will primarily draw from Baldur's Gate 3's world and characters, rather than the original BioWare RPGs, which are not considered official source material for this adaptation. The show plans to follow a new group of adventurers growing in power, interacting with established characters who will either assist or challenge them. The involvement of Baldur's Gate 3 voice actors is being considered, while Larian Studios, the game's developer, has moved on to its own Divinity series RPG. Ownership of the Baldur's Gate IP remains with Wizards of the Coast.

This development is notable as it directly links to the narrative of Baldur's Gate 3, potentially expanding the franchise‚Äôs story into a new medium and audience.

**Nioh 3 Combat and Skill Insights**

Further information has been shared about gameplay mechanics and progression systems in Nioh 3 that affect player performance:

- *Samurai Style and Ki Pulse:* Unlike the faster Ninja style, the Samurai requires managing stamina through Ki Pulses during combos. An early skill called Running Water enables players to perform Ki Pulses while dodging, aiding stamina recovery and combat flow. Increasing the heart stat and unlocking certain skills further improve stance-switching and Ki Pulse efficiency.

- *Skill Capacity and Upgrades:* Players have a limited capacity to equip skills, which can be increased incrementally by collecting specific texts and memorandums found throughout the game world, or by purchasing from merchants. These items raise capacity for general, Samurai, and Ninja skills separately, allowing more customization of playstyle.

- *Respeccing Stats and Skills:* Nioh 3 allows players to respec both character stats and acquired skills at any time after completing the prologue. This flexibility supports experimentation with different builds to better suit player preferences or specific challenges.

- *Parry Mechanic:* The defensive parry ability, termed Deflect in the Samurai skill tree, can be unlocked early in the game. Parrying reduces stamina consumption and restores energy but does not affect enemy stamina, differentiating it from similar mechanics in comparable titles. It requires precise timing and complements the offensive Burst Break counterattack.
