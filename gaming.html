Arc Raiders Eases Grind for Second Expedition Following Player Feedback

Developers Embark have responded to player concerns regarding the difficulty and grind of the first Arc Raiders Expedition by lowering the required in-game currency (Coins) needed for maximum rewards in the upcoming second Expedition. Additionally, a "catchup mechanic" allows returning players to earn skill points they missed from the first Expedition at a reduced cost. These changes aim to address player grievances about the cost-to-reward balance and improve the overall experience. The new Expedition starts on March 1, with sign-ups opening February 25.
