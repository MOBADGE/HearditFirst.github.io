**Steam Emerges as a Key Platform for Videogame Soundtracks**

Steam has quietly developed into a preferred platform for purchasing videogame soundtracks, including those from games not available on PC. Publishers like Capcom and Square Enix have put scores from many titles, including Monster Hunter Wilds and various Nier albums, on Steam‚Äôs digital store. The service allows easy access to extensive and sometimes previously difficult-to-find collections, often with high-quality audio formats and free updates. This shift makes game music more accessible to fans and collectors while sometimes offering better value than physical releases.

**Fractal Design Scape Headset Blends Quality and Convenience**

The Fractal Design Scape headset received favorable reviews as one of the best gaming headsets tested in 2025. With a minimalist design, plush fabric earcups, and a detachable, flip-to-mute microphone, the device emphasizes user comfort and practical features. Its standout attribute is the Qi wireless charging stand, which magnetically secures the headset for effortless charging, encouraging consistent use. Audio performance is described as balanced and detailed, with customizable EQ settings. While it lacks active noise cancellation and carries a premium price around $200, its refinement and convenience have made it a preferred option for extended gaming sessions.
