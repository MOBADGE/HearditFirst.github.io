Steam Hits Record Revenue with Arc Raiders Success

According to Alinea Analytics, Steam achieved its highest-grossing December ever in 2025, generating $1.6 billion in revenue from over 100 million users. This marks a 22.7% increase compared to the previous year and surpasses the pandemic-driven record in 2020.

Arc Raiders emerged as a significant contributor, selling 1.2 million copies over the holiday period and totaling 12 million units sold overall. Counter-Strike 2 remains the top seller by volume.
