**Arc Raiders Faces Duplication Glitch Involving Rare Rubber Ducks**

Players of the third-person shooter *Arc Raiders* have discovered a significant duplication glitch affecting valuable in-game items‚Äîspecifically, the rare rubber ducks introduced recently. While the exploit offers tempting in-game rewards, players are cautioned against using it due to potential account penalties.
