Code Vein 2 Launch Details

Code Vein 2, the sequel to the 2019 soulslike action RPG, is set to launch on January 29, 2026. The game will be available worldwide at staggered times, accommodating different time zones: for example, 3 pm PST in Los Angeles and 8 am JST in Tokyo the following day. Those who pre-ordered the deluxe edition gained early access three days prior to the public launch. Unlike many modern releases, Code Vein 2 does not support preloading for the standard edition, requiring players to wait for the official release to start downloading. The game introduces new elements such as firearms and a motorbike, differentiating it from its predecessor.

Sovereign Tea Now Free

Sovereign Tea, a turn-based tactics game about a conflict between Tea Princesses and a Coffee Empire, has transitioned to a free-to-play model on Steam. The game includes strategic mechanics involving 12 unique Tea Princesses and over 40 mixable minions with distinct abilities. Developer 3 Halves cited that they "have made enough money from the game" and decided to make it permanently free to reach a wider audience, including those who could not previously afford it. This shift may attract new players interested in a lightweight but strategic tactics experience.
