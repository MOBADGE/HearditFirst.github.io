Tomb Raider's Classic Mapping Community Keeps Creativity Alive

A dedicated community continues to build custom levels for the original Core Design-era Tomb Raider games, keeping alive the appeal of these challenging titles from the late '90s and early 2000s. The map-making scene began with early fan tools before the official Tomb Raider Level Editor (TRLE) was released around 2000. Since then, almost 4,000 custom missions have been created and shared via websites like TRCustoms and trle.net.
