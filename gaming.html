Skyrim Mod Adds Stealth Advantage When Hiding in Bushes

A new mod for *Skyrim* introduces a simple but effective stealth boost when players crouch in bushes or near trees. Called "Hide in Bushes," the mod makes players 50% harder to detect if crouching in a bush, with smaller shrubs providing lesser bonuses, and a 10% stealth advantage near trees or stumps. This feature enhances immersion and tactical play for stealth-focused builds.

The mod relies on the pre-existing Object Impact Framework, which enables new interactions with objects previously static in the game world. Though grass textures remain non-interactive, the mod marks a fresh step in environmental stealth mechanics for *Skyrim*. Players who often return to stealth archer playstyles may find this addition enriches their runs.

Witcher 3 Mod Restores Cut Dark Ending for Hearts of Stone Expansion

The "Brothers in Arms" mod for *The Witcher 3* has been updated to bring back a previously cut storyline involving the character Shani in the Hearts of Stone expansion. Although this restored ending changes the canonical outcome and introduces a grim twist with Shani's death by lightning, mod users can choose to enable it or stick to the official story. This update illustrates how modding communities continue to preserve and explore content removed during official development, offering players new ways to engage with established games.
