Crimson Desert Launch Faces Issues With Deluxe and Pre-Order Items

Crimson Desert, which launched recently, reached a peak of nearly 240,000 concurrent players on Steam—a solid start though not a breakout success compared to some recent open-world games. However, early adopters have reported a bug that prevents access to pre-order bonuses and deluxe edition special items. Players find the in-game supply box empty where these rewards should appear. Pearl Abyss acknowledges the issue and is currently investigating a fix.

The unavailable items include various cosmetic gear such as shields, horse armor, and a complete armor set for the protagonist. While the practical impact on gameplay remains unclear, the bug affects all Steam pre-order purchasers, which could affect player experience during the launch window.

Fallout: New Vegas Available for 99 Cents in Steam Spring Sale

Valve's Spring Sale is underway, and among thousands of discounted games, Fallout: New Vegas stands out with a notably low price of just 99 cents. This Obsidian-developed RPG is often considered the best in the Fallout series due to its balanced mix of narrative choices, humor, and darker themes.

Despite its age and technical issues on older consoles, New Vegas remains popular thanks to its replayability and engaging world. The sale makes it an accessible entry point for new players or those looking to revisit a classic. Mods and updated guides can modernize the experience on PC. The sale also includes discounted prices on other recent Fallout titles.

Retro Rewind Brings '90s Video Rental Store Experience to Job Sim Genre

Retro Rewind is a new job simulator that places players in the shoes of a '90s video rental store clerk. Unlike many sims that start slowly, this game immediately immerses players in daily routines such as stocking shelves, scanning returns, and managing customer orders. The game recreates the busy atmosphere of video rental shops from that era, including handling adult film rentals with a straightforward approach.

The gameplay loop combines task management with character interactions and has been praised for its engaging pacing and nostalgic details. For players interested in simulation games with a retro twist, it offers a fresh experience that contrasts with other slower-paced retail sims.

Moves of the Diamond Hand Demo Available Ahead of Early Access Launch

The sequel to the underrated RPG Betrayal at Club Low, called Moves of the Diamond Hand, is set for early access release on April 13. An updated demo featuring the first act of the game is currently available on Steam.

Shifting from the fixed camera of its predecessor to a first-person perspective, the game retains its signature blend of surreal storytelling, branching paths, and RPG mechanics reminiscent of Disco Elysium. The demo has been well received for its mix of absurd humor and noir elements, showcasing the developer’s evolution in narrative design.

Diablo 4 Director Discusses Ongoing Reworks as Normal for Action RPGs

Zaven Haroutunian, game director of Diablo 4, discussed the frequent major reworks that action RPGs undergo as a standard part of their lifecycle. Diablo 4 itself has seen multiple system overhauls since launch, including loot changes and level cap adjustments.

Haroutunian explains that evolving player expectations and unforeseen friction points arise over thousands of hours played, necessitating ongoing adjustments. While changes can overwhelm some players, the team prioritizes fixing underperforming aspects rather than leaving them obsolete. The upcoming Lord of Hatred expansion will continue supporting such iterative improvements.

Additional Note: How to Access The Singing Catfish in Crimson Desert

Players interested in exploring Crimson Desert can locate The Singing Catfish—an elusive sail ship near the fishing town of Vellua—by finding and using a small rowboat on the eastern beach of the cove. Navigating carefully to avoid sea mines and approaching from the front or back of the ship prevents cannon fire. Onboard, players face a battle against pirates before accessing a treasure chest containing the Pirate King Hat, which has a special treasure detection ability.
