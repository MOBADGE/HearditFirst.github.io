**Bungie Commits to Strict Anti-Cheat Measures in Marathon**

Bungie has outlined a firm stance on cheating in its upcoming extraction shooter *Marathon*. The developer emphasizes that all matches will run on dedicated servers with server-side control to prevent common exploits such as teleportation and unlimited ammo. Features like a Fog of War system will help counter wallhacks and other cheat tools. Importantly, anyone caught cheating faces permanent bans with no reinstatement. Bungie highlights its commitment to protecting competitive integrity and maintaining a fair experience, directly referencing recent exploit issues in *Arc Raiders* as a cautionary point.
