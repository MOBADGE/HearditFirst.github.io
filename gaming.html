New Samsung Gaming Monitors Offer Innovations in Refresh Rates and 3D Technology

Samsung has introduced several new PC gaming monitors ahead of the January CES event. Notably, the Odyssey 3D G9 G90XH is a 32-inch 6K display with glasses-free 3D support, a rarity in the market. **Why it matters:** These displays push the boundaries of refresh rates and resolution for gaming monitors, catering to both enthusiasts seeking smooth motion and those interested in 3D without glasses.
