Nutmeg: A Football Deckbuilding Game with Mixed Results

*Nutmeg* is a deckbuilding football management game that evokes 1980s-1990s English football culture through its presentation and gameplay. Matches consist of situational card plays affecting game outcomes. The management elements include quirky interfaces like a sticker album and club merchandise choices.

While the core card battles have engaging tactical depth reminiscent of classic football games, filler systems such as club finance management and stadium operations feel unnecessary and detract from the experience. User interface issues also hamper smooth play. *Nutmeg* shows promise but presently suffers from an uneven focus split between match strategy and peripheral management.
