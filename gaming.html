**Resident Evil Requiem Modding Expands with Novel Vehicle Swaps** Capcom’s recent action-horror title Resident Evil Requiem includes product placement such as Hamilton watches and Leon Kennedy’s Porsche Cayenne Turbo GT. A mod by SinderyMonk now allows players to replace Leon’s Porsche with a drivable hamburger modeled after the Krabby Patty from SpongeBob SquarePants. This mod adds a humorous twist to the game’s scenes. Additionally, the modding community has created a range of other playful mods, including character swaps (Shrek for Chunk, a chibi from Umamusume for The Girl) and even replacing a giant spider with Thomas the Tank Engine. Beyond these lighthearted mods, there are also challenging gameplay mods, such as one that intensifies Insanity mode by making zombie bites truly infectious. All modifications require the REFramework tool to load.

**Elden Ring Mod Restores Cut NPC Quest Providing Context to Game’s Darkest Ending** Modder Transquility has reimplemented the original questline for NPC Merchant Kalé in Elden Ring, which was removed from the final release. This quest offered deeper narrative insight into the Frenzied Flame ending, an option where the player commits omnicide. In the restored content, Kalé evolves from a friendly merchant to a figure of rage and despair motivated by the mistreatment of his people by Marika and the Golden Order. This restoration helps provide a stronger narrative justification for choosing the franchise’s most morally challenging ending, contrasting with Melina’s opposition to the Frenzied Flame. Previously, this content could only be experienced through datamined voice lines and videos; now it is fully playable through an easy-to-install mod that uses Mod Engine 2, maintaining compatibility with anti-cheat software.

**Minecraft’s Upcoming 2026 Update Introduces Sulfur Caves Biome** Minecraft has revealed the first features of its second 2026 content drop, named Chaos Cubed, highlighted by a new underground biome: the Sulfur Caves. This biome will alter the standard caving experience by replacing water pools with hazardous, poisonous sulfur pools that create a dizzying effect when approached. The biome also introduces two new blocks, cinnabar and sulfur, and features vibrant visuals inspired by real sulfur hot springs, including gas particles over the water surface. Additionally, a new mob called the Sulfur Cube will appear. Unique among Minecraft mobs, it can absorb blocks, altering its behavior depending on the material absorbed (e.g., sliding on ice or forming a ball with wood). This expansion aims to refresh and extend cave exploration, following the extensive changes from the Caves and Cliffs update.

**Minecraft Dungeons Sequel Announced with Early Teaser** Mojang has announced a sequel to Minecraft Dungeons, the Diablo-inspired action RPG spin-off. A brief teaser trailer revealed the game’s voxel fantasy setting under threat once again, hinting at a continuation of the story. While no gameplay footage or detailed information has been shared, the sequel’s Steam page features cinematic screenshots. Minecraft Dungeons received generally positive reviews as a streamlined dungeon crawler, though it didn’t reach the popularity of Minecraft’s mainline titles. Despite mixed reactions to the Minecraft Legends RTS spin-off, Mojang appears committed to expanding the Minecraft universe through multiple genres. The sequel is slated for release later in 2026.

**Grand Theft Auto Chain Game Community Prepares for GTA 6 Integration** An enduring community known as the GTA Chain Game has been collaboratively playing through multiple Grand Theft Auto titles using a relay format, where players take turns progressing a single save file toward 100% completion. Starting as a simple save-passing exercise, the community now spans the globe with hundreds of participants and organized events. Recent efforts have included GTA 4 and its Episodes From Liberty City content. The community values both nostalgia and the challenge of replaying these games with shared participation and no long-term individual commitment. With the forthcoming release of GTA 6 later this year, the community anticipates incorporating the new title into their relay format. The organizers plan a measured approach, focusing first on personal playthroughs before expanding the Chain Game to Vice City’s latest iteration.
