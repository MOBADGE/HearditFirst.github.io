Bethesda Improves DLC Presentation Following Fallout 3 Feedback

Bethesda has reflected on how it handled DLC pop-up notifications in its older RPGs, acknowledging it was not the best experience for players. Fallout 3’s Game of the Year edition notably overwhelmed players with repetitive notifications upon loading, a lesson Bethesda learned over a decade ago. Istvan Pely, now art director on Starfield, confirmed that the upcoming Starfield DLC, “Terran Armada,” integrates its content more organically. Instead of intrusive pop-ups, players will discover new content naturally in-game, improving immersion. This change matters for players who appreciate seamless expansions that respect the flow of gameplay, avoiding annoyance from constant alerts.

Modders Transform Resident Evil Requiem with Humorous Vehicle Mods

Modders have introduced playful options to replace in-game product placements in Resident Evil Requiem. A notable example is the "Patty Wagon" mod by SinderyMonk, which swaps Leon Kennedy’s Porsche with a drivable hamburger vehicle inspired by SpongeBob Squarepants’ Krusty Krab. Other recent mods add varied humorous swaps, such as Shrek replacing a character, or Thomas the Tank Engine substituting a giant spider. These mods highlight the vibrant creativity within the modding community and provide fresh, often comedic experiences in an otherwise serious survival horror game. Players interested in customizing their experience can experiment with these mods using the REFramework mod loader.

Elden Ring Cut Content Restored by Modder

A fan mod has reinstated a significant, cut NPC quest in Elden Ring involving Merchant Kalé, a character originally tied to the game’s darker “Frenzied Flame” ending. This restored quest adds depth to Kalé’s motivations and links him to some of the game’s most morally complex story elements, offering a richer context to the ending where you destroy the world. The mod recreates fully voiced scenes giving players a chance to explore content that was removed before release. For fans interested in Elden Ring’s lore and narrative, this mod provides new insight and a fuller understanding of the game's themes.

Minecraft to Add New Sulfur Caves Biome Changing Underground Exploration

Minecraft’s upcoming 2026 update, “Chaos Cubed,” will introduce Sulfur caves—a new underground biome featuring toxic-looking pools that emit poison effects when approached. The biome will include bright red and yellow blocks such as sulfur and cinnabar, inspired by real-world sulfur hot springs. A new enemy, the Sulfur Cube, will also debut; it has unique properties depending on the blocks it absorbs, such as sliding on ice. This update aims to diversify Minecraft’s underground biomes, making exploration riskier and more visually distinct, thereby refreshing an essential part of the Minecraft experience.

Minecraft Dungeons Sequel Announced for 2026

Mojang has revealed a sequel to Minecraft Dungeons during its Minecraft Live 2026 showcase. The announcement included a cinematic teaser and depicted the game’s world coming under threat again, setting the stage for new dungeon-crawling action. While no gameplay footage has been released yet, the sequel’s confirmation indicates continued support and development for the Minecraft spinoff in the action-RPG genre. This news will likely interest fans of the original game who appreciate its accessible take on the dungeon crawler format.

GTA Chain Game Community Prepares for GTA 6 Arrival

The GTA Chain Game, a community-driven project where players collaboratively complete Grand Theft Auto games by taking sequential turns with a single save file, continues to thrive in 2026. Running since several years and covering multiple GTA titles, it has evolved from a simple save-passing exercise into an international community with hundreds of players. The group is currently engaged with GTA 4 and is preparing to incorporate GTA 6 upon its release later this year. This long-running cooperative play model highlights the dedication of fans keeping older GTA games alive and anticipates extending this unique experience into the newest installment.
