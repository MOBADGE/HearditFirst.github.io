Arknights: Endfield Launch Details

Arknights: Endfield, a 3D strategic RPG and spin-off of the mobile-focused Arknights series, is set to launch globally on January 22 at 11 am UTC+8. The game will release simultaneously across PC, iOS, and Android platforms, allowing players to choose between playing on a dedicated PC client or mobile devices. Preloading is available now on PC through both the official Hypergryph site and the Epic Games Store, and on mobile platforms, which is advisable given the game's sizable 50GB download.

System requirements call for a minimum of Windows 10 or 11 64-bit, an Intel Core i5-9400F processor, GTX 1060 graphics, and 16GB RAM. Recommended specs include an Intel Core i7-10700k and RTX 2060 GPU. The game's cross-platform launch and preloading support aim to reduce server stress and improve accessibility once live.

At the World Economic Forum, Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella emphasized that AI must be harnessed for beneficial outcomes to retain public support, particularly considering the significant energy AI consumes.
