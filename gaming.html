Steam Replay 2025: Consistent Attention to New Releases

Valve's Steam Replay 2025 data shows players spent about 14% of their gaming time on titles released within the same year, a figure consistent with recent years. This suggests that despite many new releases, actual player engagement with new games remains a relatively small portion of total playtime. The median player only tries about 0.56 new games per year.

This metric is valuable as it highlights player behavior trends and might influence developers and publishers in planning releases and marketing strategies. It also reflects ongoing industry challenges such as live-service dominance and economic factors impacting gamers' willingness to try multiple new games annually.
