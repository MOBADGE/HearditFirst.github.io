Hytale's Modding Scene Shows Early Creativity and Ambition

Hytale continues to build momentum with its growing modding community adding diverse content to the game. The game currently hosts around 4,000 mods on CurseForge, spanning practical additions like HUD tweaks and mounts to impressive creative projects. Notable mods include an "MMO-style dungeon" by Runeteria featuring unique bosses and legendary weapons, praised by Hypixel founder Simon Collins-Laflamme for the talent behind it. Other standout mods add pilotable mech suits with weapons and programmable music boxes. While the game still has rough edges according to early impressions, the variety and ambition of mods highlight its strong modability and community engagement early on.

Stronghold Crusader: Definitive Edition Adds Steam Workshop Support and Co-op Campaign

Stronghold Crusader: Definitive Edition has received a significant update titled the Winter Update. The update introduces Steam Workshop support, enabling players to access and share custom content more easily. Along with this, the update includes a new editor tool to create and customize castles and AI opponents, plus the ability to design skirmish mission trails. For players seeking new challenges, there are 10 new maps and a 10-mission cooperative campaign where two players can team up against AI enemies. Additional features include a spectator mode and an extreme difficulty option. The update arrives with a 30% discount on Steam, making it an opportune time to explore the remastered classic.

Timberborn Announces March 1.0 Release Date Alongside New Lore Trailer

Beaver-themed city builder Timberborn is set to exit early access with a full 1.0 release scheduled for March 5. Developer Mechanistry has shared a new "lore trailer" featuring British narration and animated visuals of beavers building a city in a desert environment. The game has steadily evolved since entering early access in 2021, adding features like water pumps, robotic beavers, and new transportation methods. The upcoming release will include new environmental phenomena such as aquifers and geothermal fields, additional maps, and minor gameplay enhancements. The 1.0 launch marks a key milestone for the game and a notable moment for city-building enthusiasts this year.

Skyrim Mod Introduces Stealth Bonus for Hiding in Bushes

A new Skyrim mod called Hide in Bushes adds a gameplay mechanic that makes player characters harder to detect when crouching in bushes, with smaller detection bonuses near trees and stumps. This feature required a foundational mod called Object Impact Framework that enables interaction with environmental objects previously lacking such mechanics. The mod does not extend to grass, which remains non-interactive. While simple, this addition enriches stealth gameplay, appealing to players who favor sneaky archery builds. The mod reflects ongoing community efforts to deepen and diversify Skyrim‚Äôs longtime modding scene.
