United States Political and Legal Developments

The US government has partially shut down even though a last-minute funding deal was reached. The shutdown continues until the House of Representatives can vote on the deal, which is expected to be no earlier than Monday. This delay adds uncertainty to federal operations and public services.

Although the judge acknowledged that the ICE surge could have "heartbreaking" consequences, the state was unable to prove its case sufficiently. This decision allows ICE enforcement efforts to move forward in the state.
