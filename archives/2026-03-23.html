Aviation Accident under Investigation at LaGuardia Airport

Authorities are investigating a collision involving Air Canada flight AC8646 and a fire truck at LaGuardia Airport in New York. The accident resulted in the deaths of two pilots. US transportation chief Sean Duffy highlighted that seat belts played a crucial role in saving lives during the incident. The investigation aims to determine how the collision happened and what safety measures could prevent similar accidents in the future.

Political Developments in Italy

Italy’s government faces uncertainty following the defeat of a referendum on constitutional reform. The vote was widely seen as a test of support for the administration led by Giorgia Meloni. Despite the setback, Meloni has stated she intends to continue governing. This outcome may affect the government's stability and policy plans moving forward.

Energy and Market Reactions to Peace Talks

Oil prices have fallen while stock markets recovered after a statement from the US president confirming that "very good and productive" talks have occurred toward ending an ongoing war. Fuel Rationing Introduced in Slovenia

Slovenia became the first European Union country to impose fuel rationing amid supply concerns. Motorists are now limited to purchasing a maximum of 50 litres of fuel per day. The rationing will remain in place until further notice. This measure reflects ongoing challenges in energy supply management within the EU and aims to ensure fair distribution among consumers.
