This strategy aims to support economic growth and address workforce gaps, reflecting broader demographic and economic trends affecting developed nations.

Canadian Constitutional Debate Over Religious Symbols

The law, which has sparked significant debate, raises questions about freedom of religion and state neutrality. The court’s decision will be closely watched as it may set a precedent on how religious expression is balanced with public policy in Canada’s diverse society.
