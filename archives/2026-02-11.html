Devastation from Cyclone in Madagascar

Madagascar has been hit hard by a powerful cyclone, resulting in at least 31 deaths. The disaster management agency reports that many roads are now inaccessible due to fallen trees and downed power poles. The storm stripped roofs from approximately ninety percent of buildings in the affected port area, causing extensive damage and complicating relief efforts.
