Middle East Tensions Rise as Iran Responds to US Warnings

Iran has issued a warning that it will retaliate if the United States attacks, amid growing unrest within the country. This follows comments by President Trump stating that the US military is considering "very strong options" regarding Iran. The warnings come at a time when hundreds of people have been reportedly killed in protests across Iran, highlighting the country's internal and external tensions. The situation remains volatile and could have significant implications for regional stability and international relations.

Protests in Iran Result in Hundreds of Deaths

Iran has experienced widespread protests leading to the death of hundreds of people. The government’s response, combined with escalating external threats, has created an environment of uncertainty and heightened risk of further violence.
