Heard It First – Daily Digest

December 23, 2025

Conflict in Ukraine

Russia has launched a large-scale attack across multiple regions in Ukraine. The missile and drone strikes targeted several cities, causing widespread power outages. At least three people have died as a result of the attacks. The situation is unfolding amid ongoing tensions in the area, and the strikes could further destabilize the region and impact civilian life.

US Economic Growth

The US economy has shown stronger-than-expected growth, driven by robust consumer spending. This marks the fastest pace of economic expansion in two years, reflecting increased activity in various sectors. The growth suggests resilience in the US economy despite recent global challenges and may influence economic policies moving forward.

US Oil and Naval Plans

The US president announced that the country will either keep or sell oil that has been seized from Venezuela. Additionally, he said the US intends to retain control of tankers intercepted off Venezuela’s coast. In a related announcement, plans were unveiled to build a new fleet of battleships, dubbed the "Golden Fleet," named after the president himself. This initiative aims to revive American shipbuilding efforts to compete with China's naval capacity.

Security and Humanitarian Concerns

In central Nigeria, 28 Muslim travelers, including women and children, were abducted while en route to a religious festival. This incident adds to ongoing security challenges in the region. Meanwhile, thousands of people fleeing violence in the Democratic Republic of Congo are now in Burundi facing severe hardships. Aid agencies report dire conditions, especially for children and pregnant women who have often gone without food for days.

Accidents and Tragedies

Several tragic incidents occurred around the world. In Texas, a plane carrying a child burn victim crashed, resulting in five deaths. The aircraft belonged to the Mexican Navy and was involved with a charity caring for burn patients. In Laos, a ferry sank on the Mekong River, prompting frantic rescues. A local woman and two children are feared dead following the disaster. Also, an officer shot in the head during an attack in Bondi has made a remarkable recovery and returned home for Christmas, which his family called a “miracle.”
