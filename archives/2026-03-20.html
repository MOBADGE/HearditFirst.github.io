Incident on Delta Flight to Sydney

Three flight attendants required hospital treatment following the event. This incident highlights ongoing challenges in air travel safety due to unpredictable weather patterns and turbulence.

BTS Returns to Music with New Album

NASA’s Artemis Moon Mission Moves Forward

NASA has rolled its Artemis Moon rocket back to the launch pad in preparation for a possible mission scheduled for April. This mission aims to send astronauts around the Moon for the first time in over 50 years, representing a significant step in space exploration and NASA’s long-term goals for lunar and deep space activities.
