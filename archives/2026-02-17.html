New nuclear negotiations between the US and Iran began in Geneva with cautious optimism from both sides. The US president expressed a belief that Iran is interested in reaching a deal, while Tehran indicated that Washington appears to be adopting a more realistic stance. These talks could have significant implications for regional stability and non-proliferation efforts.

Ukraine's Demographic Challenges

Ukraine is facing a severe population decline, which officials describe as a demographic catastrophe. In response, a new law has been passed to fund the freezing of eggs and sperm for Ukrainian troops. This measure aims to help preserve the population during a period of ongoing conflict and social upheaval, highlighting the broader societal impacts of the war.
