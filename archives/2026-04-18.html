Middle East Tensions: French Peacekeeper Killed

A French peacekeeper was killed in southern Lebanon in an incident that has drawn international attention. President Macron blamed the attack on Hezbollah, an armed group backed by Iran. Hezbollah has denied any connection to the killing. This development could affect peacekeeping efforts and regional stability, as tensions between various groups remain high.

US Deportees Returned to DR Congo

The Democratic Republic of Congo has accepted the first group of people deported from the United States. This development reflects ongoing migration and deportation dynamics between the two nations, with implications for the detainees and broader diplomatic relations.
