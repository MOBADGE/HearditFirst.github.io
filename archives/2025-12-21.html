Heard It First – Daily Digest

December 21, 2025

Global News

Israel has approved 19 new settlements in the occupied West Bank. Far-right Finance Minister Bezalel Smotrich described the move as a way to block the establishment of a Palestinian state. This decision is likely to increase tensions in the region and complicate future peace efforts.

In South Africa, a violent attack at a tavern in Bekkersdal left nine people dead and 10 others injured. Gunmen opened fire and continued shooting randomly, according to reports. Authorities are investigating the incident amid growing concerns about public safety.

Human Rights and Legal Developments

Human rights organizations have condemned a record high number of executions in Saudi Arabia this year. At least 347 people have been put to death, according to UK-based campaign group Reprieve. The international community may face increased pressure to address concerns around the country’s use of the death penalty.

In the United States, there is ongoing scrutiny around the release of documents related to Jeffrey Epstein. Survivors of Epstein's crimes have expressed nervousness and skepticism about the release, which lawmakers say is incomplete. The files include references to several high-profile figures, including Bill Clinton and Michael Jackson, drawing public and political attention.

Australia and Venezuela

In Australia, a minute's silence was held to remember the victims of a recent attack at Bondi Beach. The memorial event was marked by tension as Prime Minister Anthony Albanese was booed upon arrival. This reflects lingering divisions and public emotions surrounding the incident.

Off the coast of Venezuela, the US seized a second oil tanker amid escalating tensions. This follows an order by US President Donald Trump to enforce a "blockade" on sanctioned oil tankers entering and leaving Venezuela. This move may impact regional relations and the Venezuelan oil industry.

Environment and Wildlife

A tragic accident in northern India resulted in the deaths of seven elephants. An express train collided with the animals as they crossed railway tracks in the state of Assam. The incident highlights ongoing challenges in balancing transportation infrastructure with wildlife conservation.

Culture and Media

Comedian Bowen Yang announced his departure from the popular TV show Saturday Night Live. Yang, who has gained prominence both on the show and through other projects such as a podcast and the Wicked film series, shared the news with his fans, marking the end of an important chapter in his career.
