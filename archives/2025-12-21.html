Heard It First – Daily Digest

December 21, 2025

International Energy and Sanctions

The United States is actively pursuing a third oil tanker linked to Venezuela as part of its efforts to enforce sanctions, according to a US official. This action follows a recent directive from President Donald Trump ordering a blockade on sanctioned oil tankers entering or leaving Venezuela. The move aims to tighten control over Venezuela’s oil exports amid ongoing political tensions, potentially increasing pressure on the Venezuelan government.

Conflict and Political Developments in Myanmar

In Myanmar, civilians living in rebel-held areas are facing severe challenges, including devastating air strikes. These attacks coincide with a government-mandated election scheduled for later this month, which is reported to be largely seen as a sham by many in the affected regions. The situation highlights the ongoing conflict and instability in Myanmar, with serious humanitarian consequences for civilians caught in the crossfire.

Israeli Settlements and Palestine

Israel has approved the construction of 19 new settlements in the occupied West Bank. Far-right Finance Minister Bezalel Smotrich stated that the move is intended to prevent the establishment of a Palestinian state. This decision is likely to exacerbate tensions in the Israeli-Palestinian conflict and pose additional challenges for future peace negotiations.

Human Rights Concerns in Saudi Arabia

Human rights groups have condemned the rising number of executions in Saudi Arabia, which has reached an unprecedented total of at least 347 people this year. The UK-based campaign group Reprieve has highlighted this record number of deaths, drawing international attention to Saudi Arabia’s use of the death penalty and concerns about human rights violations within the kingdom.

Violence and Tragedies Around the World

Several violent incidents have affected communities recently. In South Africa, a group of gunmen attacked a tavern in Bekkersdal, killing nine people and injuring ten others after firing randomly. Meanwhile, in Australia, the memory of a deadly shooting in Bondi continues to affect many physically and emotionally, adding to trauma from a stabbing attack there last year. Both events have left lasting scars on the local communities.

Wildlife and Safety Incidents in India

In Assam, a tragic accident occurred when an express train collided with a herd of elephants crossing the tracks. The collision killed seven elephants, raising concerns about wildlife safety and the impact of expanding transportation infrastructure on vulnerable animal populations. Local officials reported the incident, underscoring the ongoing tension between development and wildlife conservation efforts.
