Migration and Border Control in Greece

Reports from the BBC have revealed that Greek police have been using masked migrants to forcibly push other migrants back across the border. According to the investigation, mercenaries have been recruited since at least 2020 to participate in these illegal "pushbacks." The practice involves individuals who are themselves migrants being used to help prevent other migrants from entering Greece. This raises significant ethical and legal concerns around the treatment of migrants and the methods used by border authorities. The situation could prompt further scrutiny of Greece's border policies and compel discussions on migrant rights in Europe.

Philippine President Responds to Health Rumors

The Philippine President, Bongbong Marcos, addressed recent speculation about his health by inviting critics to join him for exercise. He demonstrated his fitness with star jumps during a gym session and challenged anyone doubting his well-being to verify it firsthand. This hands-on response comes amid rumors swirling about his physical condition. By engaging directly with these concerns through a public display of activity, Marcos sought to reassure the public and maintain confidence in his ability to lead. It remains to be seen whether this will effectively quell the rumors or fuel further discussion on the president's health.
