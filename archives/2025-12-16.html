December 16, 2025

Campus Shooting in Rhode Island

Two people were killed and nine others injured in a shooting at Brown University in Rhode Island on Saturday. Authorities have identified the two victims but have not released their names in public reports. The incident has prompted a large-scale manhunt for the person responsible. Police and emergency services responded promptly to the scene, and the search for the gunman continues as officials seek to ensure public safety on and around the campus.

Ongoing Investigation and Public Safety Measures

Law enforcement agencies are actively investigating the shooting, gathering evidence, and interviewing witnesses. Security measures have been heightened in the area to prevent further harm, and local authorities have urged residents and students to stay alert and report any suspicious activity. The community remains on edge as the search unfolds, with officials emphasizing their commitment to apprehending the suspect quickly to restore a sense of security.

Impact on the Brown University Community

The shooting has deeply affected students, faculty, and staff at Brown University, as well as the wider Rhode Island community. Counseling and support services are being offered to those impacted by the tragedy. University leaders and local officials have called for calm and cooperation while dealing with the aftermath of the incident. Plans for increased safety protocols and continued support for the campus community are expected in the coming days.
