Colombia’s Fight Against Drug Gangs

As the presidents of the US and Colombia met recently, BBC journalist Orla Guerin joined Colombian commandos battling drug gangs in the jungle. The police unit’s mission involves locating and destroying cocaine laboratories, highlighting the ongoing efforts to curb drug trafficking and related violence in the region.

Tragic Accident in Brazil

A bus carrying pilgrims overturned in Brazil, resulting in the deaths of 15 people, including three children. The accident occurred on the return leg of their journey, adding to the sorrow of the incident.

Nearly 40% of cancers are linked to factors that could be avoided, offering a significant opportunity to save lives through preventive measures.
