Social and Environmental Issues in Nigeria

In Lagos, Nigeria, residents living in lagoon shanties have witnessed demolitions and fires that have destroyed homes. Many suspect these actions aim to gentrify the waterfront area. However, officials deny that gentrification is the objective. The situation raises concerns about urban development and the rights of vulnerable communities in Nigeria’s largest city.

U.S. Immigration Policy Update

A U.S. judge has temporarily blocked the Department of Homeland Security from lifting deportation protections for Haitian migrants. The judge ruled that the DHS Secretary lacked sufficient factual or legal grounds to end the protections at this time. This decision offers temporary relief to Haitian migrants but leaves the issue unresolved as further legal and administrative steps are anticipated.
