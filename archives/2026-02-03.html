Technology and Regulation: China Bans Hidden Car Door Handles

China has become the first country to ban the use of hidden car door handles, a design popularized by Elon Musk’s Tesla vehicles. The move is motivated by safety concerns, reflecting Beijing’s approach to automotive regulation and possibly influencing vehicle design standards globally. This decision may prompt automakers to reconsider design features that prioritize aesthetics over safety.

Legal Decision on Haitian Migrants in the US

A US judge has temporarily blocked the government’s attempt to lift deportation protections for Haitian migrants. The ruling criticized the Department of Homeland Security Secretary for lacking sufficient factual and legal backing. This injunction prolongs protections for the migrants amid ongoing debates about immigration policy in the United States.

Business: SpaceX to Merge with Elon Musk’s AI Company

Elon Musk’s space exploration company, SpaceX, is set to absorb his artificial intelligence startup, combining their operations. The deal may reshape the landscape of private space and AI sectors under Musk’s leadership.
