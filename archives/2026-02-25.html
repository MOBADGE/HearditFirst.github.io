Zimbabwe Declines US Health Aid Deal Over Research Concerns

Zimbabwe rejected a proposed health aid deal with the United States, citing concerns about access to biological samples. Zimbabwean officials were troubled that the US did not commit to sharing benefits arising from research involving these samples. The decision highlights ongoing challenges in international health collaborations and data sharing agreements.
