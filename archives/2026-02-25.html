This military buildup highlights Japan's concerns about regional security and the shifting balance of power in East Asia, especially regarding Taiwan's contested status.

Rising Tensions in Sudan

Fighting has escalated in Sudan's Kordofan region, a key area rich in gold and oil resources. Drone strikes have intensified, turning this zone into a new major frontline in the conflict. The ongoing violence has caused many casualties, and despite efforts, peace remains out of reach, signaling continued instability and humanitarian challenges.
