Space Exploration

NASA's Artemis II mission has left Earth's orbit, marking the first time humans have ventured beyond Earth's orbit since 1972. The mission's goal is to travel to the far side of the Moon, continuing humanity’s efforts to explore the lunar surface and deepen our understanding of space. This significant step could pave the way for future missions aimed at sustained lunar exploration and potentially preparing for Mars journeys.
