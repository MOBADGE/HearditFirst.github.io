Space Exploration

NASA’s Artemis II mission has taken a major step by leaving Earth's orbit, marking the first time humans have traveled beyond Earth orbit since 1972. The spacecraft is now on its way toward the far side of the Moon. This milestone represents a significant advancement in human space exploration and could pave the way for future missions that might establish a more permanent presence on the Moon or prepare for crewed missions to Mars.

Political Changes in Myanmar

His appointment formalizes his control over the government amid a brutal civil war, which has led to significant political instability and humanitarian challenges in the country. This development may influence the future course of Myanmar’s internal conflicts and its relations with the international community.
