Space Exploration

NASA’s Artemis II mission has taken a major step forward by moving beyond Earth’s orbit as it heads toward the far side of the Moon. This marks the first time since 1972 that humans are leaving Earth’s immediate vicinity. The mission aims to return crewed spaceflights to lunar orbit, continuing work that could pave the way for future Moon landings and further exploration of space. This milestone highlights progress in space technology and human spaceflight ambitions for the coming decade.
