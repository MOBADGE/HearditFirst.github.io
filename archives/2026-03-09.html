Economic Challenges for Farmers and Migrants

West African cocoa farmers are struggling despite rising chocolate prices globally. Many farmers face economic hardship, which contrasts sharply with the high retail prices of chocolate bars. This situation underscores ongoing difficulties in global supply chains and inequities in agricultural markets.

In Spain, undocumented migrants are welcoming a new amnesty that will grant them legal status. The Spanish government cites humanitarian and economic reasons for this move, aiming to integrate undocumented workers legally and improve their living conditions. This policy shift reflects broader debates on migration and labor rights in Europe.
