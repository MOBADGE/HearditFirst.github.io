Energy and Environment

Japan’s operator of the world’s largest nuclear power plant suspended operations just hours after restarting the reactor. Officials report that the reactor remains stable and there is no radioactive impact outside the facility. This quick halt may affect energy supply and raises questions about the future of nuclear power in the region.

Technology and Society

Uganda experienced a significant internet shutdown during its general election, which has had widespread consequences. Many people lost income, particularly those who rely on digital platforms for work, while others were deprived of entertainment like online gaming. The shutdown illustrates the critical role of internet connectivity in daily life and the economic impact when it is disrupted.

Science and Space

This event provides scientists with valuable data about solar activity and its effects on Earth’s atmosphere, while offering a unique spectacle enjoyed by many.
