Security and Violence in Nigeria

Cultural Heritage and Legal Disputes

A court ruling has granted the family of Nelson Mandela the right to sell some of his personal items, including his prison key, sunglasses, and shirt. Authorities had tried to block their export, arguing these items were part of South Africa's national heritage. The ruling marks a significant moment in the debate over the ownership and handling of historical artifacts.
