Social Media and Child Safety in Australia

Australia’s eSafety regulator has raised concerns about major social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, TikTok, and YouTube. Despite a legal ban on children under 16 using these platforms, the watchdog fears that enforcement is inadequate. The call for better compliance reflects growing worries about online safety and the mental health of young users, likely prompting stricter regulations or oversight in the near future.
