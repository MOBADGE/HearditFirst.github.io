January 01, 2026

Fire at Swiss Ski Resort Bar

A fire broke out on New Year's Eve at Le Constellation, a bar in the Crans-Montana ski resort in Switzerland. Several dozen people are feared dead, with around 100 others injured in the blaze. The fire occurred during New Year's celebrations, and emergency services have been responding to the scene. The full extent of casualties and damage is still being assessed.

Violence in the Netherlands on New Year

Widespread reports of unrest and violence were recorded across the Netherlands on New Year's Eve. Police faced what has been described as "unprecedented" incidents involving the throwing of fireworks and other explosives. Authorities are managing the situation amid ongoing disturbances throughout the country.

Developments in Ukraine and Russia

Ukrainian leader Zelensky stated in his New Year address that a peace deal with Russia is about 90% ready, signaling potential progress towards ending the conflict. In contrast, Russian President Vladimir Putin used his New Year speech to express confidence in his troops and in their eventual victory in Ukraine, indicating continued determination on Moscow’s side.

US Military Strikes on Alleged Drug Boats

The US military reported that five people were killed in their latest strike on boats suspected of drug trafficking. This incident follows another attack a day earlier, after which the US Coast Guard is still searching for survivors. These operations are part of ongoing efforts to curb drug movement by sea.
