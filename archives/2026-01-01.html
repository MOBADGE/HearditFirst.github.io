International Incidents

A tragic fire has broken out in a Swiss resort bar during New Year's Eve celebrations, resulting in dozens of deaths. Emergency responders continue to assess the situation as authorities work to understand the cause of the fire and support the victims. Meanwhile, in Amsterdam, a historic church was engulfed in flames shortly after midnight on New Year's Eve. The fire was declared a major incident as firefighters battled to contain the blaze.

Ukraine Conflict Developments

Ukrainian leader Volodymyr Zelensky stated in his New Year address that a peace deal with Russia is 90% ready. On the other side, Russian President Vladimir Putin used his New Year speech to encourage Russian troops, expressing confidence in their eventual victory in Ukraine. As the conflict approaches its fourth year, many in Russia are reflecting on the war and their hopes for 2026.

Protests and Unrest in Iran

Security forces in Iran reported the death of one of their members amid ongoing protests triggered by a currency collapse. Unrest has spread across multiple cities, with demonstrators voicing frustrations over economic conditions. The situation remains tense as the government responds to the widespread demonstrations.

New Leadership in New York City

Zohran Mamdani was sworn in as the first Muslim mayor of New York City. The Democratic politician took his oath over a Quran at a unique location—the disused Old City Hall subway station. His inauguration marks a notable milestone in the city's political history.

Other Notable News

Former world boxing champion Anthony Joshua has been discharged from a Nigerian hospital following a car accident that claimed the lives of two of his close friends. Joshua is recovering from his injuries sustained in the crash. In a separate incident at a Disney theme park, a worker was injured while preventing a large rubber prop from falling onto an audience. The worker is now recovering after being knocked off his feet. Additionally, Marcus Alexander Muench Casanova, a 19-year-old described by his family as an avid outdoorsman, died after falling approximately 150 metres from a trail in California's Mt Baldy area.

Celebrations Around the World

Despite these serious events, many countries marked the arrival of 2026 with fireworks, public gatherings, and traditional activities. Images from around the globe show people embracing the New Year with a mix of joy and hope.
