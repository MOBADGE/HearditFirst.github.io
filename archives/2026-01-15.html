Labor Rights Concerns in Manufacturing

This development may prompt calls for better regulatory oversight and protections for factory workers.

Trade Disputes in Canada

Two Canadian provinces are in a dispute over the branding and approach to Crown Royal whisky, a product made in Canada but owned by a UK company. This disagreement comes as the whisky faces US tariffs, leading to efforts to form a united "Team Canada" response to protect the industry. The trade dispute highlights challenges Canadian producers face in international markets.
