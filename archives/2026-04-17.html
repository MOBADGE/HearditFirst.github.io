International Affairs and Conflict

The ceasefire aims to ease tensions in the region, although the situation remains delicate and ongoing diplomatic efforts are in place.

South Korea concluded a nine-day search for Neukgu, a two-year-old wolf that escaped from a zoo in Daejon. The search drew widespread public attention and even inspired a meme coin. The wolf was finally captured, ending a week of twists and community engagement around the event.
