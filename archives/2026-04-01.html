India’s Massive Census Underway

India has started the world’s most extensive census, a two-phase process involving three million officials who will count every person in the country. With a population exceeding one billion, this census aims to collect detailed demographic data to help shape future policies and resource allocation. The exercise is expected to take significant time and resources, highlighting the scale of India’s diversity and population complexity.

Health Concerns Rise in Chiang Mai Due to Air Pollution

In Chiang Mai, Thailand, residents are facing serious health issues as air pollution reaches hazardous levels during the peak fire season. The city has become one of the most polluted in the world, leading to problems such as frequent nosebleeds among children. This situation has raised alarm over the long-term effects of pollution on health and could prompt authorities to consider stronger measures to control fires and improve air quality.

Megan Thee Stallion Hospitalized During New York Performance

Rapper Megan Thee Stallion was taken to hospital after feeling very ill midway through a performance of Moulin Rouge in New York. Her representative reported her sudden illness but did not provide further details. The incident highlights the physical demands and pressures of live performances, though no additional information about her condition or the cause of illness has been shared yet.

US Army Pilots Suspended and Reinstated Over Helicopter Flights

Two US Army pilots were temporarily suspended after flying military helicopters near the home of musician Kid Rock. However, Defence Secretary Pete Hegseth later announced there would be no investigation and the suspensions were lifted within hours. This rapid reversal underscores ongoing tensions about military conduct and privacy but appears to resolve the incident without further action.

Excitement Builds for Artemis Moon Mission on Florida’s Space Coast

Anticipation is growing near the Kennedy Space Centre in Florida as large crowds gather to witness the upcoming Artemis moon mission launch. The event combines public enthusiasm for space exploration with festive activities, including merchandise sales dubbed “moonshots.” This mission represents a significant step in NASA’s efforts to return humans to the lunar surface, renewing interest in space travel and technology.
