Population Census in India

India has launched a massive two-phase census, involving three million officials tasked with counting over a billion people. This is the world's most ambitious census exercise, reflecting the scale and complexity of gathering data on the world's second-most populous country. The results will provide crucial insights for government planning, resource allocation, and policy-making over the coming decade.

The poor air quality is raising significant health concerns among residents, including reports of children suffering from nosebleeds. The situation underscores the challenges posed by seasonal fires on urban environments and public health in the region.
