Population and Environment

India has launched a massive two-phase census to count its population, which exceeds one billion people. This extensive effort involves three million officials tasked with conducting what is considered the world's most ambitious census exercise. The data collected will be vital for policy-making and resource allocation across the country.

In Thailand, the city of Chiang Mai is experiencing severe air pollution due to the peak fire season. The situation has raised health concerns among residents, with reports of children, including six-year-olds, suffering from nosebleeds. Chiang Mai's air quality now ranks among the worst globally, prompting calls for urgent action to address the environmental crisis.

This quick reversal highlights the sensitive nature of military operations near private properties and the decision-making processes within the armed forces.

As one of the largest firms in the technology sector, Oracle's downsizing signals ongoing challenges in the industry and could impact the broader job market and company operations.

Space Exploration

Excitement is building along Florida’s Space Coast ahead of the Artemis II mission launch at the Kennedy Space Center. Large crowds of locals and visitors have gathered to witness this historic moonshot. Community members describe the atmosphere as "phenomenal," reflecting the enthusiasm and anticipation surrounding NASA's planned human spaceflight mission. The launch represents a key moment in the United States’ space exploration efforts and could inspire renewed interest in lunar exploration.
