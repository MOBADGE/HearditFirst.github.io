Space Exploration

The Artemis II mission has reached the halfway point in its journey to the Moon. Aboard the Orion capsule, the crew shared a "spectacular" image of Earth, taken by commander Reid Wiseman. This milestone marks important progress in NASA's plans to further explore lunar space and potentially prepare for future missions.

Humanitarian Efforts in Iran

A rescue team is conducting a difficult and hazardous search in Iran for a missing US crew member. A former US marine emphasized that the highest priority for the recovery team is to find signs of life. The situation remains tense, with the search efforts facing significant challenges.

Social Issues in Nigeria

Residents who were evicted to make way for Nigeria's capital have voiced concerns that the government has not kept its promises. These individuals are seeking recognition and a say in matters affecting their land, highlighting ongoing tensions between displaced communities and authorities.

Gun Control in Canada

Canada's largest gun control initiative in decades faced setbacks as efforts to buy back tens of thousands of "assault-style" firearms fell short by half. Although the government banned 2,500 models of these guns, the reduced participation in the buyback program has raised questions about the overall effectiveness of the policy in reducing gun violence.

Cultural History of Bombay

A new exhibition showcases Bombay’s transformation from a fortified city into a bustling modern metropolis. Using paintings, photographs, and prints, the exhibition offers insights into the rich history of the city and how it has evolved through the centuries.
