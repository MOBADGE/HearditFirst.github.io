Crime and Public Safety

Two men have been arrested in connection with the shooting of a seven-month-old baby in a stroller in Williamsburg, New York. The incident has raised serious concerns about violence in public spaces. The US government has taken custody of the niece and grand-niece of Qasem Soleimani, the late Iranian general. They were detained by Immigration and Customs Enforcement officials. No further details have been provided on the circumstances or reasons for their arrest.

Conflict in Ukraine

A Russian drone strike hit a busy market in a southern Ukrainian town, killing five people and injuring 21 others. The attack happened on a Saturday morning, highlighting ongoing tensions and violence in the region.

Travel and Military Regulations

Germany is considering new rules that may require males under 45 to obtain military approval for extended stays abroad. The law emphasizes granting travel approvals, but enforcement details remain unclear. This measure appears linked to national defense policies.

Space Exploration

The Artemis II crew, en route to the Moon aboard the Orion capsule, has reached the halfway point in their journey. Commander Reid Wiseman captured a "spectacular" image of Earth during the flight, marking a significant milestone in the mission.

Domestic Issues and Economy

Australia is experiencing fuel shortages with hundreds of petrol stations running out of gas, driven by disruptions from the ongoing war in Iran and rising oil prices. Despite this, officials have encouraged the public to continue with Easter travel plans.

Political Developments

In Cameroon, President Paul Biya, who won his eighth consecutive term in a disputed election last year, will for the first time in his 43-year rule have a deputy. Biya, aged 93, is noted as the world's oldest leader.

Cultural Highlights

Chapal Bhaduri, once the biggest stage queen in Bengal, recently highlighted the changes in theater as women began to take on prominent roles, shifting the spotlight away from his previous prominence. This reflects broader cultural shifts in Indian performing arts.

Political Proposals in the US

Former Speaker of the House Nancy Pelosi criticized a proposal by Donald Trump to spend $152 million to reopen the Alcatraz prison, calling it a "stupid notion" and a likely waste of taxpayer money. The plan has sparked debate over its practicality and purpose.
