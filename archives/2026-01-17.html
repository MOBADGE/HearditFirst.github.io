Having led the country’s military government, he now faces the challenge of transitioning into a democratic leadership role. How he manages this shift will be critical for Guinea’s political stability.

Health Study on Paracetamol Use in Pregnancy

A new study has provided reassurance about the safety of paracetamol, a common painkiller, during pregnancy. The researchers conducted a thorough review and concluded that paracetamol does not increase the risk of autism, countering previous claims made by Donald Trump and others. This finding may help settle ongoing debates and guide medical advice for pregnant women.
