Iran Faces Growing Uncertainty Amid War Support

Many Iranians who initially supported their country's involvement in the war are now expressing doubts and fears about the future. Teachers, engineers, and shopkeepers told the BBC they worry that Iran could descend into chaos as a consequence of the ongoing conflict. This growing unease reveals the war’s deepening impact on everyday people and raises questions about Iran’s stability moving forward.

China Implements New Language Law for Ethnic Minorities

China has approved an "ethnic unity" law requiring children from minority groups to learn Mandarin starting before kindergarten and continuing through the end of high school. The law aims to promote national cohesion, but it has also stirred concerns about cultural preservation and the rights of ethnic minorities. The requirement could reshape education and daily life for millions of children across China.

Justice Delivered for Moscow Concert Attack

Nineteen people have been sentenced in connection with a deadly attack at the Crocus City Concert Hall near Moscow in 2024. Gunmen opened fire, killing 149 people and injuring more than 500. The convictions mark a significant moment of accountability for what was one of the deadliest mass casualty events in the region’s recent history.

NASA Probe Reenters Earth's Atmosphere

A NASA spacecraft weighing around 1,300 pounds, known as the Van Allen Probe, has re-entered Earth’s atmosphere. Most of it was expected to burn up upon reentry. NASA has indicated a low risk of any surviving debris causing harm to people on the ground. This event concludes the mission of a probe that was critical in studying Earth’s radiation belts.

Rising Death Toll from Ethiopia Floods and Landslides

In Ethiopia, the death toll from recent floods and landslides has risen to more than 100 people. Forecasters attribute the increasing severity of storms in the region partly to global warming. The disaster underscores the growing vulnerability of certain areas to extreme weather and reflects wider concerns about climate change’s local impacts.
