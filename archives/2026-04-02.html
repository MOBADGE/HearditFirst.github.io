April 02, 2026

International Relations

French President Emmanuel Macron described recent comments made by Donald Trump about his marriage as “neither elegant nor up to standard.” Macron’s remarks highlight ongoing tensions and sensitivities in public discourse involving the two figures.

In Venezuela, the United States has lifted sanctions on Delcy Rodríguez, who was previously recognized as an interim leader. This move is seen as part of improving relations between the US and Venezuela following the capture of Nicolás Maduro during a commando raid.

Natural Disasters and Safety

A man has died from flooding near Athens, Greece, as severe rain affected multiple areas. Additionally, the island of Crete experienced a Saharan dust storm, further complicating weather conditions in the region.

In Indonesia, a magnitude 7.4 earthquake struck between the islands of Manado and Ternate. While the quake caused one confirmed death, tsunami warnings that were initially issued have since been lifted.

Crime and Public Safety

In Uganda, four children were fatally stabbed at a school. The suspect has been detained, but the motive behind the attack remains unclear, according to police statements.

Legal and Social Changes

Brazil has passed a new law allowing joint custody of pets following the separation of couples. Lawmakers framed the change as recognition of the important roles pets play in people's lives.

Business and Technology

SpaceX, Elon Musk’s aerospace company, is preparing for a public stock listing that could value the company at $1 trillion. This listing may position Musk as the world's first trillionaire and mark one of the largest initial public offerings in history.

Arts and Culture

An iconic 19th-century Indian painting, "Yashoda and Krishna" by Raja Ravi Varma, sold for $17.9 million, setting a new record in Indian art sales. This surpassed the mark set previously by a work of MF Husain.

Health and Human Rights

Nobel laureate Narges Mohammadi, who is currently imprisoned in Iran, reportedly suffered a suspected heart attack. Her family has raised concerns about her life being in imminent danger and is calling for her transfer to a hospital.
