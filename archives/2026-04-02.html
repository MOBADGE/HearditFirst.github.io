International Relations and Conflict

French leader Emmanuel Macron criticized US President Donald Trump’s approach to the ongoing US-Israeli conflict with Iran, suggesting Trump should 'be serious' and not comment every day. This reflects tensions over differing strategies and messaging about the war, which remains a central issue in global diplomacy.

The situation adds complexity to the conflict and raises concerns about economic stability in the region.
