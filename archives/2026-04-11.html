Global Politics

In Hungary, Péter Magyar's opposition movement is gaining momentum with polls showing a lead over the incumbent government. Environmental Concerns in Argentina

A new, controversial law in Argentina has removed protections for glaciers, allowing mining activities to proceed in these areas. This decision has raised fears about the long-term sustainability of the country’s water resources, as glaciers are vital for water supply. Environmentalists emphasize that "every drop of water counts," highlighting concerns for future ecological impacts.
