International Incidents

A shooting occurred at the Israeli consulate in Istanbul, where one gunman was killed and two others injured. The consulate has been empty for over two years, and no Israeli diplomats are currently present in Turkey. Meanwhile, the World Health Organization (WHO) has suspended medical evacuations from Gaza after a contractor was killed by Israeli troops. Israeli forces said they fired at the vehicle believing it posed an immediate threat.

Transportation Accident in France

A fatal collision took place in northern France involving a train traveling at 99 mph and a lorry. The train driver died during the crash, and the lorry driver is now in custody. The incident is under investigation.

Space Exploration Update

NASA has released the first images from a recent fly-by of the Moon. The images include a view of the Earth rising over the lunar surface and a solar eclipse observed by the astronauts during the mission. These pictures provide new perspectives on Earth's place in space.

Political Developments

US Vice-President JD Vance made a visit to Budapest to support Viktor Orbán’s re-election campaign, delivering a strong endorsement and criticizing the European Union. In Vietnam, To Lam has consolidated power after a unanimous vote in the national assembly, becoming both the communist party chief and the country’s president, marking an unusual concentration of authority.

Legal and Crime News

Adriana Rivas, a Chilean woman accused of involvement in kidnappings during the Pinochet era, lost her legal battle to avoid extradition from Australia. Also, Ben Roberts-Smith, who is Australia's most-decorated living soldier, has been charged with alleged war crimes. He denies all wrongdoing and had previously lost a major defamation case related to these allegations.

Missing Person and Security Incidents

Authorities in the Bahamas are searching for a US woman who reportedly fell off a boat and was swept out to sea according to her husband. Separately, rapper Offset is hospitalized in Florida after being shot. He is in stable condition, and two people have been detained in connection with the incident.
