Heard It First – Daily Digest

December 27, 2025

Conflict and Security

Kyiv, the capital of Ukraine, was struck by a intense 10-hour missile and drone attack, resulting in two deaths and 32 injuries, according to local authorities. Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky said the attack highlighted that Russia "doesn't want peace," underscoring the ongoing tensions and violence in the region.

In Southeast Asia, a ceasefire has begun between Thailand and Cambodia after several weeks of deadly clashes. The recent fighting displaced nearly one million people following the breakdown of a previous truce. The ceasefire could provide relief and stability for those affected, although the situation remains fragile.

In West Africa, the United States launched strikes against the Islamic State group in Nigeria. While Nigerian officials have a different account, former US President Donald Trump told Politico he ordered the strikes as "a Christmas present." This development is part of ongoing efforts to combat militant groups in the region.

Crime and Law Enforcement

Italian authorities arrested nine individuals believed to have raised approximately €7 million (£6 million) over more than two years to support the militant group Hamas. The operation highlights ongoing international efforts to disrupt funding networks linked to extremist organizations.

Weather and Accidents

A significant winter storm hit New York, leading to hundreds of flight cancellations and over 3,000 delays. The heavy snowfall has disrupted travel plans and posed challenges for transportation across the region.

In central Greece, four hikers—a group of three men and one woman—were found dead following an avalanche. They had gone missing on Christmas Day, prompting a search operation. This tragedy highlights the risks of winter outdoor activities in mountainous areas.

Japan also experienced a severe traffic accident when a 50-vehicle pile-up occurred on a highway in the central part of the country. The crash killed two people and injured 26 others, according to police reports.

Politics in Africa

Elections recently held in Guinea and the Central African Republic have brought two figures—a general and a mathematician—into focus as they seek to consolidate power. The outcomes of these elections may shape the political landscape in both countries moving forward.

Science and Culture

A bright meteor was observed lighting up the night sky over Japan's Mount Fuji. This rare natural spectacle drew attention from residents and skywatchers, providing a moment of awe amid ongoing news events.

Social Media Trends

A discussion is emerging around the growing impact of artificial intelligence on social media fame. While some critics argue AI is damaging the online environment, others see it as a tool that can democratize how individuals become popular or influential, expanding opportunities for diverse creators.
