Heard It First – Daily Digest

December 27, 2025

Ukraine Power Outages Amid Russian Attacks

In Kyiv, Ukraine’s capital, about one-third of residents are without heating following a large-scale Russian attack. Authorities reported that the cold weather makes the outages especially difficult for tens of thousands of people. The loss of heat in freezing temperatures adds to the already challenging conditions for civilians amid ongoing conflict.

Ceasefire Starts Between Thailand and Cambodia

A ceasefire has come into effect after weeks of deadly clashes between Thailand and Cambodia. The renewed fighting earlier this month displaced nearly one million people after a previous truce collapsed. This new truce aims to halt hostilities and enable aid and recovery efforts in the contested border areas.

Severe Winter Storm Disrupts New York Travel

New York City is experiencing significant travel disruptions due to a heavy snowstorm. Hundreds of flights have been cancelled, and over 3,000 flights have been delayed. The storm is causing widespread inconvenience for travelers during the busy holiday season, as authorities work to clear roads and airports.

Security and Conflict Updates: Arrests and Strikes

Italian police arrested nine people accused of raising around €7 million (£6 million) over two years for Hamas, the militant group. This move reflects ongoing efforts to cut off funding sources for armed groups.

Meanwhile, the United States conducted airstrikes against the Islamic State in Nigeria. Former President Trump said he ordered the strikes as a “Christmas present,” a comment that contrasts with Nigerian officials’ public statements on the timing and impact of the attacks.

Natural Disasters and Accidents

In Greece, four hikers were found dead after an avalanche in the central part of the country. Three men and one woman went missing on Christmas Day, prompting a search that ended with the somber discovery.

In central Japan, a major highway crash involving about 50 vehicles resulted in two deaths and 26 injuries. Authorities are investigating the circumstances surrounding the pile-up during winter conditions.

Elections in Africa and Social Media in the AI Era

Elections in Guinea and the Central African Republic are underway, with key figures including a general and a mathematician competing for power. These polls could determine how leaders consolidate control in their countries moving forward.

On a different note, social media continues to evolve with the rise of AI influencers. While some criticize AI’s role in online fame, others highlight its potential to democratize opportunities for people to gain popularity on digital platforms.

Meteor Sighted Over Japan’s Mount Fuji

A bright meteor was seen lighting up the night sky above Japan’s Mount Fuji, offering a spectacular natural display. The event caught the attention of many and was captured on video, adding a moment of awe amid recent challenges in the region.
