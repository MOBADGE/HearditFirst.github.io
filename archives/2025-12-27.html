December 27, 2025

Ukraine Power Outages Amid Russian Attack

Thousands of residents in Kyiv, Ukraine’s capital, have been left without power following a major attack attributed to Russian forces. About one-third of the city’s population is currently without heating during freezing winter conditions. This disruption poses significant challenges for those affected and highlights the ongoing humanitarian impacts of the conflict.

Ceasefire Between Thailand and Cambodia

After weeks of intense clashes earlier this month, a ceasefire between Thailand and Cambodia has come into effect. Hostilities had resumed despite an earlier truce, displacing nearly one million people. The new ceasefire offers hope for improved stability in the border region, though the displacement crisis continues to affect many families.

Severe Snowstorm Hits New York

New York is dealing with a major winter storm that has brought heavy snowfall and caused significant travel disruptions. Hundreds of flights have been cancelled while more than 3,000 others were delayed. The storm highlights the difficulties in transportation logistics during severe weather and may lead to further interruptions as clean-up and travel recovery efforts continue.

Law Enforcement in Italy Targets Fundraising for Hamas

Italian police have arrested nine people suspected of raising approximately €7 million (£6 million) over two years for the group Hamas, described as a militant organization. The arrests reflect ongoing international efforts to disrupt funding networks associated with armed groups.

Deadly Accidents in Greece and Japan

Tragedy struck in Greece where four hikers—a group of three men and one woman—were found dead following an avalanche in the country’s central region. They had gone missing on Christmas Day, prompting a search operation.

Separately, a massive pile-up involving 50 vehicles occurred on a highway in central Japan. Two people died, and 26 others were injured. These incidents underscore the risks posed by winter weather and road conditions in different parts of the world.
