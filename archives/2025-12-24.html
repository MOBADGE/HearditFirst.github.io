US Denies Visas over Social Media Policies

This move is connected to disagreements over social media rules. Breton described the situation as a "witch hunt," highlighting tensions between the US and EU on digital regulation and access.

Pennsylvania Nursing Home Explosion

An explosion at a nursing home in Pennsylvania has resulted in at least two confirmed deaths. Several people have been rescued from the partially collapsed building, but five remain unaccounted for. Emergency crews are continuing search and rescue operations amid ongoing concern for potential survivors.

Brazil’s Supreme Court Allows Surgery Leave for Bolsonaro

Brazil’s Supreme Court has permitted former president Jair Bolsonaro to leave prison temporarily to undergo hernia surgery on Christmas Day. This ruling allows Bolsonaro to receive medical treatment while still detained. The decision may impact his incarceration and legal proceedings going forward.
