Ukraine Peace Plan Developments

Ukrainian leader Volodymyr Zelensky has proposed moving towards establishing demilitarised zones as part of a new peace plan. A significant aspect of this plan focuses on Ukraine's eastern Donbas region, where Zelensky suggested the creation of a "free economic zone" as a possible way forward. This approach aims to ease tensions and foster economic activity in the contested area. The plan is a notable effort to find a peaceful resolution amid ongoing conflict and could influence future negotiations between Ukraine and Russia.

International Tensions Over Oil Tankers

Venezuela has accused the United States of "extortion" following the US seizure of two Venezuelan oil tankers. The incident prompted an emergency meeting of the UN Security Council to address the escalating dispute. This development comes amid heightened strains in US-Venezuela relations and raises concerns about the broader impact on international trade and diplomatic ties. The Security Council's discussions may shape future actions regarding these and similar incidents.
