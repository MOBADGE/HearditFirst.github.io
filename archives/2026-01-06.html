Venezuela Political Crisis

Venezuela is experiencing a significant political upheaval as an interim president was sworn in amid ongoing conflict with Nicolás Maduro. Maduro has pleaded not guilty to undisclosed charges while facing opposition claims. Delcy Rodríguez, a Venezuelan official, accused the United States of "kidnapping" Maduro and his wife, reflecting heightened tensions. This development deepens the political crisis in Venezuela and could impact international relations and internal stability in the coming months.
