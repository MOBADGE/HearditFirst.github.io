Middle East Conflict Escalates as Iran-Backed Houthis Attack Israel

Fighting in the Middle East took a new turn as the Iran-backed Houthi group launched an attack against Israel. Political Divisions Emerge Over Iran War in US Conservative Circles

The war involving Iran is causing a split within conservative groups in the United States, highlighted by discussions at the Conservative Political Action Conference (CPAC). Meanwhile, pressure is growing on Donald Trump to clarify his stance or propose a way to end the conflict, signaling ongoing political debate around America's role in the region.
