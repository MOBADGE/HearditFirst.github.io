Disappearance of Savannah Guthrie's Mother

She went missing in the early hours from her home in Tucson, Arizona, with the last confirmed sighting on 31 January. Authorities continue their search and investigations into the case.

Aviation Incident in Somalia

A Somali passenger plane crash-landed on a seashore after failing to reach the runway. Despite the emergency situation, all 55 people on board survived, and the pilot has been praised for managing the crash landing under difficult circumstances. Details about the cause of the landing failure are still emerging.
