Developments in the Epstein Investigation

A former police chief recalled that in 2006, Donald Trump reportedly contacted Florida police during their investigation of Jeffrey Epstein. The ex-police chief said Trump expressed relief, saying, "thank goodness you're stopping him," and suggested that "everyone" was aware of Epstein's behaviour at the time. This statement adds to the ongoing public discussion about the extent of knowledge and response to Epstein’s criminal activities in previous years.

International Political Updates

In Zimbabwe, a bill has been introduced that could allow President Mnangagwa to extend his term beyond its scheduled end in 2028. This development has implications for the country's political future and its governance structure.

In Europe, the French president warned about increasing geopolitical threats from China, Russia, and the United States. He urged European nations to act more decisively and collectively as a global power. This reflects growing concerns about Europe's position and influence in an evolving global landscape.

Security and Human Interest

Meanwhile, in Somalia, a passenger plane experienced a landing failure near a shoreline but was safely crash-landed by the pilot. All 55 people on board survived, and the pilot has received praise for managing the emergency without loss of life.
