US-Canada Bridge Opening Faces Political Threat

However, Trump’s indication that he might block its opening introduces potential delays and political contention around this important infrastructure project.

US Tariff Exemptions for Bangladeshi Clothing

The White House has agreed to exempt certain Bangladeshi clothing products from US tariffs, specifically allowing exemptions for those made from US-produced cotton. This decision is likely to impact the trade relationship between the United States and Bangladesh, supporting Bangladeshi garment exports while protecting aspects of the American cotton industry. It may also influence broader trade discussions and economic ties.

New Video Game Explores Themes of Cultural Repatriation

A newly released video game set in the year 2099 imagines a world where a historic treaty to return African artifacts is breaking down. Players can explore scenarios involving the reclamation of looted cultural treasures. This game is notable for engaging with ongoing debates about the return of cultural property to African nations and reflects creative interest in issues of historical justice and cultural identity.
