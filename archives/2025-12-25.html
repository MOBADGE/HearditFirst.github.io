December 25, 2025

Security and Counterterrorism in Turkey

Turkish authorities have arrested 115 people suspected of planning attacks around New Year's celebrations. Firearms were seized during the operation, and officials are continuing efforts to identify and track other potential members of the group. This crackdown comes amid ongoing concerns about extremist threats in the region during holiday periods. The arrests aim to prevent attacks and enhance public safety as New Year's festivities approach.

Political Developments in Bangladesh

Tarique Rahman has returned to Bangladesh after spending 17 years in exile in London. His arrival comes just weeks before important national elections. Rahman is a prominent political figure whose return may influence the political landscape ahead of the vote. How his presence will affect the election outcomes remains to be seen, but it highlights the intensifying political activity in the country.

Contested Election Result in Honduras

In Honduras, a candidate supported by former US President Donald Trump narrowly won a recent election. The losing candidate, Salvador Nasralla, has said he does not accept the result but has called on his supporters to remain calm. This dispute over the election outcome could lead to political uncertainty and tensions in the country as the parties address the contested vote.

Attack in Nigeria’s Maiduguri Kills Five

A bomb exploded in a crowded mosque in Maiduguri, Nigeria, killing five people and injuring 35 others during evening prayers. Police reported the details of the blast, which adds to concerns about security and violence in the region. The attack has heightened fears of further violence and will likely prompt increased efforts to enhance protection for places of worship and civilians.

Christmas Celebrations and Traditions Worldwide

Around the world, people embraced a variety of festive traditions to mark Christmas Day. Highlights included waterskiing Santas and large cuts of meat in several countries. From London to Bethlehem, religious services and celebrations took place, reflecting the diverse ways communities observe the holiday and spread seasonal cheer.

Santa Tracker’s 70th Year Features Special Guest

On Christmas Eve, the North American Aerospace Defense Command (Norad) celebrated 70 years of tracking Santa Claus. This year, former US President Donald Trump joined in answering children’s calls about Santa’s location, adding a notable presence to the long-standing tradition that brings joy to many families during the holiday season.
