Heard It First – Daily Digest

December 25, 2025

Global Peace and Conflict

On Christmas Day, Pope Leo delivered a message urging courage to end the ongoing war in Ukraine. During his address at the Vatican, he also expressed concern over the conditions faced by Palestinians. His call highlights the continuing desire for peace in regions affected by long-standing conflicts and may influence international attention during this holiday period.

Meanwhile, Turkey announced the arrest of 115 suspected members of the Islamic State group who were allegedly planning attacks for the New Year. Authorities seized firearms and continue efforts to track down additional suspects. This action reflects ongoing security challenges in the region and the importance placed on preventing terrorist violence during high-profile times.

Political Developments

In Bangladesh, Tarique Rahman, a key political figure and front-runner in the upcoming elections, returned from exile after 17 years living in London. His arrival comes just weeks before pivotal national elections, signaling a potentially significant shift in the country's political landscape.

In Honduras, a closely contested election concluded with a Trump-backed candidate declared the winner. However, his main rival, Salvador Nasralla, has refused to accept the results but advised his supporters to remain calm. The situation remains tense as the nation awaits further developments, which could impact the political stability of Honduras.

Security and Violence

Tragic violence struck in Nigeria where a bomb exploded in a crowded mosque in Maiduguri during evening prayers, killing five people and injuring 35 others. This attack underscores ongoing security issues within the region and the vulnerability of civilians during religious gatherings.

In Texas, police are investigating a daring robbery where two suspects were caught on camera ripping an ATM out from inside a store. Law enforcement continues to search for the individuals involved. The incident adds to concerns about local crime and safety.

Christmas Celebrations and Traditions

Despite global challenges, Christmas celebrations continue worldwide with various festive customs taking place. From waterskiing Santas to large traditional meals in cities around the world, communities are marking the holiday season with cheer and cultural traditions.

Additionally, the 70th year of NORAD’s Santa Tracker was celebrated with President Donald Trump answering children’s calls to check on Santa’s location. This lighthearted event adds to the holiday spirit and emphasizes ongoing family traditions associated with Christmas.
