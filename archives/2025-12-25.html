December 25, 2025

South Asia Politics

Tarique Rahman, who has lived in exile in London since 2008, returned to Bangladesh recently. His arrival comes just weeks before crucial national elections scheduled to take place. Rahman is seen as a key figure in Bangladeshi politics, and his return after 17 years could have a significant impact on the upcoming vote and the country’s political landscape.

Central America Elections

In Honduras, a closely contested presidential election concluded with a candidate backed by Donald Trump declared the winner. However, the losing rival, Salvador Nasralla, has stated he will not accept the election results. Despite this, Nasralla has called on his supporters to remain peaceful and calm, signaling potential political tensions ahead.

Security and Violence in Nigeria

A bomb exploded in a mosque in Maiduguri, Nigeria, during evening prayers, killing five people and injuring 35 others. The attack in this densely attended place of worship highlights ongoing security challenges facing the region. Authorities are continuing their investigations as the community copes with the tragedy.

Holiday Celebrations Worldwide

Christmas festivities are underway around the globe, with various unique traditions marking the holiday. From waterskiing Santas to large festive meals, cities including London and Bethlehem are hosting services and celebrations to spread holiday cheer. These events reflect local cultures and global joy during the season.

Christmas Eve Traditions in the U.S.

This year marks the 70th anniversary of NORAD’s Santa Tracker, a program that lets children follow Santa Claus’s journey on Christmas Eve. President Donald Trump participated by answering children’s calls to the NORAD tracker, adding a notable moment to the long-running holiday tradition.

Crime Report: Texas ATM Theft

Police in White Settlement, Texas, are searching for two suspects who ripped an ATM from inside a local store. Security footage captured the robbery, and authorities are actively investigating the incident as they seek to apprehend the suspects.
