December 25, 2025

Bangladesh Politics

Tarique Rahman, who has been living in exile in London since 2008, has returned to Bangladesh ahead of upcoming elections. His arrival is significant as he is considered a leading candidate to become prime minister. Rahman’s return comes just weeks before a pivotal vote, marking his first time in the country in 17 years. The elections will be closely watched both domestically and internationally, as they could reshape Bangladesh’s political landscape.

Honduran Election Results Disputed

In Honduras, a tight presidential race ended with the candidate supported by former US President Donald Trump declared the winner. However, his opponent, Salvador Nasralla, has refused to accept the results. Despite his rejection, Nasralla called for calm among his supporters. This dispute could lead to political uncertainty and potential protests as the nation awaits further developments.

Security Incident in Nigeria

A bomb explosion at a mosque in Maiduguri, Nigeria, has killed five people and injured 35 others during evening prayers. Authorities are investigating the attack, which occurred in a crowded place of worship. This violent event adds to ongoing security concerns in the region and raises questions about efforts to protect civilians from attacks.

Christmas Eve Tradition with a Twist

On Christmas Eve, the North American Aerospace Defense Command (Norad) celebrated its 70th anniversary of tracking Santa Claus. President Donald Trump participated in the event by taking children’s calls to update them about Santa’s location. This lighthearted tradition continues to delight families and brings a festive spirit to the holiday season.

Crime Report from Texas

In Texas, police are searching for two suspects involved in a robbery where an ATM was forcibly removed from inside a store in White Settlement. Security footage captured the moment the robbers took the ATM. Authorities are reviewing evidence to identify and apprehend those responsible. This incident underscores concerns over local crime and the measures being taken to address it.
