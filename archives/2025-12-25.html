December 25, 2025

Global Politics

Tarique Rahman, a key political figure in Bangladesh, has returned home after 17 years in exile in London. His arrival comes just weeks ahead of important elections, signaling a possible shift in the political landscape. Rahman's return might influence the upcoming vote, though the full impact remains to be seen.

In Honduras, a close election has concluded with a candidate supported by former US President Donald Trump declared the winner. However, the runner-up, Salvador Nasralla, has rejected the result but called for calm among his supporters. This outcome suggests potential tensions ahead as the country navigates the post-election period.

Security and Safety Concerns

Turkish authorities have arrested 115 suspects believed to be linked to the Islamic State, accusing them of planning attacks around the New Year. The authorities seized firearms and are continuing to investigate and track additional suspects. This crackdown underscores ongoing security challenges in the region.

Meanwhile, Nigeria experienced a tragic bombing inside a mosque in Maiduguri during evening prayers. The explosion killed five people and injured 35 more, highlighting persistent risks of violence and instability in parts of the country.

Christmas Messages and Traditions

Pope Leo delivered his first Christmas address, calling for "courage" to help end the war in Ukraine. He also expressed concern about the situation faced by Palestinians. His message emphasized peace and reflection during the holiday season, inviting global attention to ongoing conflicts.

Around the world, Christmas celebrations were marked with unique local traditions. From waterskiing Santas to large communal meals, communities embraced festive activities in cities like London and Bethlehem, underscoring the varied ways people come together during the holiday.

Miscellaneous News

In Texas, police are investigating a crime caught on video where robbers forcibly removed an ATM from inside a store. The White Settlement Police Department is seeking information on two suspects involved in the incident.

Additionally, in a lighter moment related to Christmas, President Donald Trump participated in the annual NORAD Santa Tracker event, which recently celebrated its 70th year. Trump answered children's calls, adding a personal touch to the holiday tradition.
