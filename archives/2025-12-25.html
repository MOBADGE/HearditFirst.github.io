Heard It First – Daily Digest

December 25, 2025

Global Peace and Conflict

Pope Leo delivered his Christmas address from the Vatican, calling for courage to end the ongoing war in Ukraine. He also highlighted the difficult situation for Palestinians, emphasizing the need for peace and compassion during the holiday season. His message stressed the importance of ending conflicts worldwide and offering hope for reconciliation.

In Nigeria, a bomb exploded in a mosque in the city of Maiduguri during evening prayers, killing five people and injuring 35 others. This tragic event underscores ongoing security challenges in the region and raises concerns about safety in places of worship.

Political Developments

In Bangladesh, Tarique Rahman returned home after 17 years in exile in London. His arrival comes just weeks before key elections, making it a significant moment in the country's political landscape. Rahman is considered a front-runner in these upcoming polls.

Brazil's former president Jair Bolsonaro underwent successful hernia surgery. Bolsonaro is currently serving a 27-year prison sentence related to allegations of plotting a coup after his defeat in the 2022 presidential election.

In Honduras, a candidate supported by former U.S. President Donald Trump secured a narrow victory in a closely contested election. The losing candidate, Salvador Nasralla, has refused to accept the result but urged his supporters to remain calm amid the tension.

Security and Safety Concerns

Turkish authorities arrested 115 suspects linked to the Islamic State who were reportedly planning attacks for New Year's celebrations. Firearms were seized during the operation, and investigations to identify additional suspects are ongoing. This action highlights continuing efforts to prevent terrorism during the holiday season.

Cultural and Holiday Highlights

Christmas festivities are underway around the world, from London to Bethlehem, where services and celebrations are bringing communities together to mark the holiday spirit. Unique traditions include waterskiing Santas and large, festive meals, reflecting diverse ways people observe Christmas.

Meanwhile, the North American Aerospace Defense Command (Norad) celebrated the 70th anniversary of its annual Santa tracker on Christmas Eve. President Donald Trump participated by answering children's calls, adding a festive touch to the event.

Legal and Institutional Matters

In the United States, Representative Joyce Beatty, who serves on the board of the Kennedy Center, filed a lawsuit to reverse the recent renaming of the center after Donald Trump. Beatty argues this name change is unlawful because it was made without Congressional approval, setting the stage for a legal debate over the institution's future identity.
