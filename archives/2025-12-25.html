December 25, 2025

Global Conflicts and Calls for Peace

Pope Leo delivered his first Christmas address at the Vatican, calling for "courage" to end the ongoing war in Ukraine. He also expressed deep concern about the situation faced by Palestinians. In his speech, the pope urged leaders and communities worldwide to seek peace and resolution to conflicts, highlighting the importance of compassion during the holiday season. His message underlines ongoing global tensions and the desire for diplomatic efforts to bring stability.

Security Alerts in Turkey and Nigeria

Turkish authorities detained 115 people suspected of belonging to the Islamic State group and planning attacks during the New Year period. Firearms were seized in the operation, and investigations continue to identify other potential suspects.

In Nigeria, a bomb exploded in a crowded mosque in Maiduguri during evening prayers, killing five people and injuring 35 others. The attack adds to security concerns in the region, with authorities working to respond and prevent further violence.

Political Developments in Bangladesh and Honduras

Tarique Rahman, a prominent figure who has spent 17 years living in exile in London since 2008, has returned to Bangladesh. His arrival comes weeks before crucial national elections, marking a significant moment in the country’s political landscape.

Meanwhile, Honduras held a tightly contested election, with the candidate supported by former US President Donald Trump declared the winner. However, his opponent, Salvador Nasralla, rejected the result but called for calm among supporters, signaling a tense post-election atmosphere.

Holiday Festivities and Lighthearted Moments

Around the world, Christmas celebrations continued with vivid and joyful displays. From waterskiing Santas to special services in cities like London and Bethlehem, communities embraced the festive spirit in diverse ways.

Additionally, President Donald Trump participated in holiday cheer by answering children's calls on the North American Aerospace Defense Command (NORAD) Santa tracker, which celebrated its 70th anniversary this year.

Crime Report from Texas

In Texas, police are investigating a daring robbery in the town of White Settlement, where two suspects forcibly removed an ATM from inside a store. Authorities have released footage of the incident and are searching for the individuals involved.
