December 14, 2025

Major Shootings in Australia and the US

A tragic shooting at Bondi Beach in New South Wales has left 11 people dead, along with the gunman. The attack targeted the Jewish community. Eyewitnesses captured a moment where a man tackled and disarmed the shooter, preventing further harm. This incident has shocked the local community and raised concerns about targeted violence.

In the United States, a shooting at Brown University in Rhode Island resulted in two deaths and nine injuries. Authorities have detained a "person of interest" related to this incident. All victims are reported to be students, although their identities have not yet been released. Investigations continue to understand the motives and circumstances surrounding this event.

Security Threats in Europe

In Germany, five people have been arrested for plotting an attack on a Christmas market. Local media reports that one suspect is an imam at a mosque and that police allege he called for a vehicle attack. Authorities are likely increasing vigilance around public events, especially during holiday seasons, to prevent such planned attacks.

Release of Belarusian Political Prisoners

Following the lifting of US sanctions against Belarus, several political prisoners were freed, including Maria Kolesnikova, who spent over five years in detention. Kolesnikova described her release as "a feeling of incredible happiness." This development signals a potential easing of political tensions and could affect relations between Belarus and the international community.

End of an Era in Wrestling and Entertainment

John Cena, known both as an actor and wrestler, has announced his retirement from wrestling after a 24-year career. He has been one of wrestling’s biggest stars during this time, and his final match marks a significant moment for the sport. Cena’s departure may lead to changes in wrestling's landscape and open opportunities for new talents to emerge.

Challenges for Somali Deportees

The BBC spoke with a former Uber driver recently deported from the US to Somalia. The individual expressed fear concerning jihadist threats in Mogadishu. His story highlights the difficulties faced by Somali migrants and deportees who return to unstable conditions, raising questions about the broader humanitarian and security implications for the region.
