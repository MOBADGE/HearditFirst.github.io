December 14, 2025

Campus Shooting at Brown University

A shooting occurred at Brown University in Providence, Rhode Island, where a gunman opened fire inside a building during exam time. The incident resulted in two deaths. Authorities are conducting a manhunt as the suspect remains at large. The university and local officials are working to secure the area and provide support to students and staff affected by the tragedy.

Ongoing Investigation and Security Measures

Law enforcement agencies have increased their presence in and around the campus while continuing to search for the suspect. The situation has caused significant concern among the university community. Authorities are urging anyone with information to come forward to help apprehend the gunman. Meanwhile, Brown University is coordinating with law enforcement to assess safety measures and prevent further incidents.

Impact on Students and University Operations

The shooting has deeply affected students, faculty, and staff, with disruptions to classes and exams. Counseling and support services have been made available to those impacted. The university is reviewing its emergency response procedures to improve preparedness for future incidents and is expected to provide further updates as the investigation progresses.
