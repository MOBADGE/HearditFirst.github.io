December 14, 2025

Bondi Beach Shooting

A tragic shooting took place at Bondi Beach in New South Wales, where 11 people were killed, along with the gunman. The attack targeted the Jewish community. Police reported that a bystander tackled and disarmed the shooter. This incident has shocked the local community and raised concerns about safety and security in public spaces.

Brown University Shooting

In Providence, Rhode Island, a classroom shooting led to two deaths and nine injuries. Police are currently searching for the suspect involved in this incident. Authorities have detained a person of interest as the investigation continues. The shooting has deeply affected the university community and prompted discussions on campus safety.

Arrests Over German Christmas Market Plot

Five people were arrested in Germany over an alleged plot to attack a Christmas market. Among those detained is an imam, who police say called for a vehicle attack. The arrests prevent a potential act of violence during a busy holiday season, highlighting ongoing challenges in counterterrorism efforts.

Release of Belarusian Political Prisoners

Following the US lifting sanctions on Belarus, several political prisoners, including Maria Kolesnikova, were released after long detentions. Kolesnikova described her release after more than five years as "a feeling of incredible happiness." This development marks a significant moment in Belarusian politics and international relations.

John Cena’s Retirement from Wrestling

John Cena has retired from professional wrestling after a 24-year career. Known as one of the sport’s biggest stars, Cena’s final match marks the end of an era in WWE. He is also recognized for his work as an actor, making his retirement notable both within and outside the wrestling world.

Challenges Facing Somali Deportees

An article highlighted the difficult situation faced by some Somali migrants deported from the US. One deportee, formerly an Uber driver, spoke about fears of jihadist threats after returning to Mogadishu. This story sheds light on the human impact of immigration policies and security concerns in Somalia.
