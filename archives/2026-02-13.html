Global Security Concerns

Germany's chancellor warned at the Munich Security Summit that the global rules-based order "no longer exists," signaling growing instability amid rising tensions between major powers. This perspective underscores concerns about the future of global diplomacy and the potential for increased conflict without shared frameworks.

The move may deepen existing tensions in the region and affect future negotiations.

Public Health Investigation in France

Authorities are examining whether the companies are responsible for distributing potentially toxic products. This probe aims to ensure safety standards are upheld and to restore public confidence in infant nutrition products.
