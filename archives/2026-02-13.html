A major change in US climate policy was announced recently, removing the legal foundation for much environmental legislation. Political Developments in Hungary and Bangladesh

In Hungary, Peter Magyar, a rival to Prime Minister Viktor Orbán, accused opponents of plotting to release a sex tape involving him from August 2024. This accusation comes ahead of upcoming elections, indicating a tense and contested political environment.

Meanwhile, Bangladesh held its first election since a Gen Z uprising, resulting in a victory for the Bangladesh Nationalist Party. Voters expressed hope for a return to democracy after 15 years under the authoritarian rule of Sheikh Hasina, marking a potentially significant political shift in the country.

Middle East: West Bank Control and Safety Concerns

This deepens tensions in the region and raises concerns about the future governance and humanitarian conditions for Palestinians in the West Bank.
