Public Safety Actions in Paris

Infant Remains Found in Trinidad

Police in Trinidad are investigating the discovery of around 50 infant bodies dumped at a local graveyard. Early inquiries suggest this may involve the unlawful disposal of unclaimed corpses. The case has raised serious ethical and legal questions regarding the handling of remains in the country, prompting calls for a thorough investigation.
