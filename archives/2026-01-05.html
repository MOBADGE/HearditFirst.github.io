Venezuela Political Tensions

Former US President Donald Trump has issued a warning to Venezuela's interim president, Delcy Rodríguez. Trump suggested that Rodríguez could face serious consequences, potentially even greater than those of Nicolás Maduro, if she does not "do what's right." Maduro is expected to appear in court soon, amid ongoing political struggles in the country.

Cuban Involvement in Venezuela

Cuba's president stated that Cuban military and intelligence operatives are actively providing protection to Nicolás Maduro, who is currently captured. This revelation highlights Cuba's continuing involvement in Venezuela's political and security situation, which may impact regional stability.

Swiss Bar Fire Victims Identified

Swiss authorities have identified all 40 victims of a deadly fire that occurred at a bar named Le Constellation. Among the victims confirmed is 16-year-old Arthur Brodard. This tragedy has drawn attention to fire safety and emergency responses in public venues in Switzerland.

US Interest in Greenland

The US president has reiterated a longstanding interest in Greenland, expressing a desire for the island to become part of the United States. This statement continues a conversation about the strategic importance of Greenland, which could affect US-Denmark relations and Arctic geopolitics.

South Korea-China Relations

South Korea's Lee Jae Myung met with China's Xi Jinping to discuss several important issues, including trade, regional security, and Beijing's unofficial restrictions on Korean pop culture. The meeting signals South Korea's efforts to improve its relationship with China amid various diplomatic and cultural challenges.

French Intelligence Training

A French university near Paris, Sciences Po Saint-Germain, is known for training current French secret agents and recruiting potential new ones. This institution plays an important role in preparing intelligence personnel for national security work in France.
