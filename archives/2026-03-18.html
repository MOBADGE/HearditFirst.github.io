Tragic Ski Accident in Switzerland

A ski gondola accident occurred at the Engelberg resort in Switzerland, resulting in one fatality. Rhino Population Returns to Uganda

In a conservation success story, southern white rhinos have been reintroduced to the wild in Uganda for the first time in over 40 years. The species had been wiped out locally by poachers decades ago. This reintroduction marks a hopeful step for biodiversity restoration and wildlife protection efforts in the region.
