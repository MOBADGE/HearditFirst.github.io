Animal Behavior Research and Incidents

Researchers studying chimpanzees in Uganda have identified a long-lasting violent conflict within the Ngogo community, a group once known for strong social bonds. This intra-group "civil war" has persisted for eight years, providing new insights into chimpanzee social dynamics.

In South Korea, efforts are underway to locate a wolf that escaped from a zoo in the city of Daejeon. Authorities have deployed thermal imaging cameras in the search for the male wolf, named Neukgu. The situation highlights concerns over public safety and animal welfare.
