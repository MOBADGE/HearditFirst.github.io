Geopolitical Tensions Over Greenland

Former US President Donald Trump has reignited discussion about Greenland by insisting that the US needs to "own" the island to prevent Russia and China from gaining influence there. He suggested that America would acquire Greenland either "the easy way" or "the hard way." However, Denmark, which currently oversees Greenland, has firmly stated that the territory is not for sale. Meanwhile, Greenlanders themselves are expressing concerns about their future and are calling for more autonomy, wanting a larger role both in their relationship with Denmark and on the global stage. This geopolitical tension highlights the strategic importance of Greenland amidst broader international competition.

Deadly Landfill Collapse in the Philippines

Rescue teams in the central Philippines are racing to find dozens of people missing after a deadly landslide at a landfill site, where a large mountain of rubbish collapsed. The disaster has prompted urgent search and rescue operations as concerns grow for those still unaccounted for. This incident underscores the dangers associated with large waste sites and the importance of safety measures to prevent similar events.
