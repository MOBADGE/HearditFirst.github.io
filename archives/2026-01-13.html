Tragic Landslide in the Philippines

A landslide at the Binaliw landfill in the Philippines has resulted in the deaths of at least 11 people. The incident occurred while more than 100 workers were present at the site, making it a significant workplace tragedy. Rescue and investigation efforts are ongoing to determine the causes and prevent future disasters at landfill locations.
