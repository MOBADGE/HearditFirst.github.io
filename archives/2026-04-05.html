Cambodia Honors Landmine-Sniffing Rat with Statue

Cambodia has unveiled a statue honoring a famous African giant pouched rat that detected over 100 landmines during its lifetime. The statue recognizes the rat’s vital role in making former conflict zones safer. This tribute highlights innovative approaches to humanitarian demining and raises awareness of ongoing efforts to address the legacy of landmines in affected countries.
