Tribute to Landmine-Detection Rat in Cambodia

Cambodia unveiled a statue honoring an African giant pouched rat celebrated for detecting more than 100 landmines during its lifetime. This tribute recognizes the unique contributions of animals in humanitarian efforts, especially in areas affected by conflict and unexploded ordnance. The statue stands as a powerful symbol of innovation and courage in making dangerous regions safer for residents.
