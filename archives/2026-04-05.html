US Airman Rescued from Iran

A seriously wounded US airman, who was part of the crew of a US F-15 fighter jet shot down over southern Iran, has been rescued. The operation followed joint search efforts by both the United States and Iran, leading to the recovery of the missing crew member. The rescue marks a significant development in a high-profile incident involving sensitive military and diplomatic dimensions.

Hungarian Gas Pipeline Plot Allegation

Hungary has accused unknown actors of planning to sabotage a gas pipeline just a week ahead of the national elections. Authorities warned that such an operation could be intended to influence voters amid an already tense political environment. The government is investigating the alleged plot as part of its broader security measures during this critical electoral period.

Pope Leo XIV’s First Easter Address

In his first Easter Mass as pontiff, Pope Leo XIV spoke to thousands gathered in St Peter's Square, calling on global leaders to choose peace. His address emphasizes the Vatican’s hope for harmony and stability worldwide during turbulent times. The message sets a tone for his tenure, focusing on reconciliation and moral leadership.

Artemis II Mission Shares Lunar Views

Astronauts aboard the Artemis II mission are currently orbiting the far side of the Moon, capturing stunning images and delivering a unique perspective on lunar geography. These images combine scientific value with breathtaking visuals, highlighting the ongoing exploration efforts beyond Earth.

Cambodia Honors Landmine-Detecting Rat

In Cambodia, a new statue has been unveiled to honour the late African giant pouched rat recognized for detecting more than 100 landmines during its lifetime. The statue celebrates the animal’s contribution to saving lives and raising awareness about landmine hazards. This tribute reflects ongoing efforts in regions affected by landmines to remember innovative solutions in humanitarian work.

Community Rejects Detention Centre Proposal

A small farming town has refused a proposal by US immigration authorities to build a detention centre locally. While residents expressed support for the administration’s immigration policies overall, they opposed the detention centre being located in their community. This decision highlights the complex dynamics between national policy agendas and local community concerns.
