Cambodia has unveiled a statue to honor a famous African giant pouched rat known for detecting over 100 landmines during its lifetime. The statue serves as a tribute to the animal’s contribution to safety and humanitarian efforts, reflecting the ongoing challenge of landmines in the region and the innovative ways in which they are being addressed.
