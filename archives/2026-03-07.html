Natural Disasters: Flooding in Nairobi

Heavy rains in Nairobi have led to severe flooding, killing at least 23 people. The floods have inundated key roads in the Kenyan capital and disrupted flights at Nairobi Airport, highlighting the intensity of the weather event and its impact on transportation and daily life. The situation underscores persistent challenges in urban infrastructure to handle such extreme rainfall events.

Political Developments in Switzerland and Latin America

In Switzerland, voters are preparing to decide on a proposal supported by the right-wing Swiss People’s Party to sharply reduce the licence fee that funds the public broadcaster. The party argues that the fee is unfair given the current high cost of living, setting the stage for a significant debate about media funding and public services.

Meanwhile, in Latin America, US President Donald Trump is meeting with ideologically aligned leaders in an effort to counter China’s growing influence in the region. China’s years of relationship-building in Latin America have created new diplomatic and economic dynamics, prompting the US to seek a stronger foothold and to reassert its presence on the continent.
