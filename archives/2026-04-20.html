Heard It First – Daily Digest

April 20, 2026

International Relations and Security

The United States has maintained a naval blockade at the Strait of Hormuz and announced it will not lift it until a deal is reached with Iran. This follows the US interception of an Iranian ship attempting to enter the Gulf. These developments come amid uncertainty over Iran's participation in upcoming peace talks scheduled to take place in Pakistan. Meanwhile, the US and Israel continue to impact the region's stability, as energy markets experience heightened volatility since attacks on Iran in late February.

In Lebanon, tensions have surfaced following the vandalism of a statue of Jesus by an Israeli soldier. The Israeli military condemned the act seriously, and the US ambassador called for swift consequences.

Technology and Business

Apple has named John Ternus as its new chief executive, succeeding Tim Cook. Cook will transition to the role of executive chairman while Ternus takes over day-to-day operations at the technology company. In other technology news, Blue Origin, a space company founded by Jeff Bezos, has grounded its rockets after a satellite launch failed. Investigations into the mishap are ongoing.

Crime and Safety

A tragic shooting in Louisiana has resulted in the deaths of eight children, reportedly related to a domestic dispute involving their father. In the desperate situation, a woman and a child escaped by jumping off a roof to evade the violence.

In Mexico, a Canadian tourist was killed and several others injured in a shooting at the ancient Teotihuacán pyramids, a well-known tourist destination. Mexican officials are investigating the incident.

In Brazil, a police gunfight with a favela gang in Vidigal blocked access to Morro Dois Irmãos, leaving around 200 tourists stranded on the hilltop during the operation.

Humanitarian Efforts

In the Democratic Republic of Congo, more than 200 people have been rescued from a camp linked to the Allied Democratic Forces, an IS-affiliated group. Among those freed is a girl as young as 14 years old. The Uganda army, involved in the operation, reported the rescues as part of ongoing efforts against the militant group.

Energy Markets

Oil prices have seen significant fluctuations following the recent geopolitical incidents involving Iran. The US seizure of an Iranian ship and the ongoing Gulf blockade contribute to the uncertain outlook for global energy supply and prices.
