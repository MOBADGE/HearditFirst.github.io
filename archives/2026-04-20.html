Bulgarian Parliamentary Election Early Results

Early results from Bulgaria's parliamentary election indicate a strong lead for Rumen Radev's party, Progressive Bulgaria, which stands at 45% of the vote. This is significantly ahead of the liberal PP-DB coalition, which has received about 15%. The outcome suggests a possible shift in Bulgaria's political landscape, with Progressive Bulgaria poised for a dominant position in parliament.
