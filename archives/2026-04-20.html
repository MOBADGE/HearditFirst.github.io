Oil prices have risen following news that a US naval force intercepted an Iranian-flagged cargo ship in the Gulf. Former US President Donald Trump announced the seizure, which is part of a broader naval blockade related to ongoing tensions between the US, Israel, and Iran since February 28. These developments have caused significant volatility in energy markets globally.

Flood Emergency Declared in Wellington, New Zealand

Wellington has been declared in a state of emergency as severe flooding hits the area. Videos circulating online show vehicles underwater, uprooted trees, and houses damaged by landslides. Local authorities are responding to the disaster and working to assist affected residents while monitoring weather conditions for further risks.

Bulgarian Election Results

With 87% of votes counted, the Progressive Bulgaria party, led by Rumen Radev, has secured a parliamentary majority in the recent election. This outcome gives the party a key role in governing Bulgaria and shaping policy moving forward. Further results and potential coalition talks may clarify the political landscape in the days ahead.

Deadly Factory Blast in India

A firecracker factory in Tamil Nadu, India, experienced a devastating explosion that killed at least 25 workers, most of whom were women. The incident underscores ongoing safety challenges in industrial workplaces. Authorities are investigating the cause of the blast and have begun efforts to provide support to survivors and the victims’ families.

Dramatic Incident on Australian Bus

A boy in Australia was trapped by the back door of a moving school bus and dragged approximately 350 metres. Fortunately, the child was unharmed. The incident was captured on video, highlighting safety concerns related to school transport and prompting calls for improved safety measures.
