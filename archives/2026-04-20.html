Industrial Accident in India

A blast at a firecracker factory in Tamil Nadu, India, has caused the deaths of at least 25 workers, most of whom were women. The explosion underlines the risks faced by workers in hazardous industries and may lead to calls for stronger safety regulations and enforcement in such workplaces. Authorities are investigating the cause of the blast and continuing rescue and recovery operations.
