Middle East Ceasefire Agreement

Trump has invited leaders from both countries to Washington for further discussions. Meanwhile, Israel's prime minister has stated that Israeli troops will remain in southern Lebanon despite the ceasefire agreement. This pause in hostilities could open a path to longer-term negotiations, though the presence of Israeli troops signals ongoing security concerns.

Violence in Turkey and Ukraine

Turkish authorities have detained 162 people in connection with online posts about recent school shootings. The detentions reflect heightened security and concern about potential further violence.

Russia responded to a Ukrainian drone attack that reportedly killed two people in Russia. These events highlight the fragile and ongoing conflict in the region.
