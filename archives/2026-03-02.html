Oil prices have increased following attacks on ships near the Strait of Hormuz, a key international shipping route. Security Challenges in Africa’s Sahel Region

The Sahel region of Africa experienced a sharp rise in the kidnapping of foreigners in 2025, marking one of the worst years on record for such incidents. Growing insecurity in this largely lawless area has contributed to the increase, presenting significant challenges for governments and international organizations working to stabilize the region and protect civilians.
