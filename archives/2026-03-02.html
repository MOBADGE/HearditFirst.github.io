Energy and Global Markets

Oil prices surged following an attack related to Iran that threatens the Strait of Hormuz, a critical chokepoint for global oil shipments. Given the Strait of Hormuz’s strategic importance, any sustained disruption could affect fuel supplies and global markets.

Security Challenges in Africa's Sahel Region

The Sahel region in Africa experienced a sharp increase in the kidnapping of foreigners in 2025, marking one of the worst years on record. The rise in abductions reflects growing insecurity and lawlessness in the area. This trend poses challenges for regional stability and international efforts to support economic development and safety in the Sahel.
