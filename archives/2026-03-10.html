Human Rights and Sports Protest

Five Iranian footballers who protested during the national anthem have been granted visas to Australia. Their action—failing to salute the anthem—sparked serious backlash at home, including accusations of being "wartime traitors." With fears growing over their safety, the decision to allow these athletes into Australia reflects concerns about their treatment following the protest.

China’s Economy and New Legislation

China's exports have surged in the first two months of 2026, defying tariffs imposed during the Trump administration. Critics worry this legislation could threaten minority rights and reflects President Xi’s approach to strengthening central control over diverse ethnic groups within the country.
