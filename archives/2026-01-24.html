International Relations and Trade Tensions

Tensions between the United States and Canada have escalated as Trump has threatened to impose 100% tariffs on Canadian goods if Canada "makes a deal with China." This warning adds strain to the relationship between Trump and Canadian Prime Minister Mark Carney. Meanwhile, Danish Prime Minister Mette Frederiksen is in Greenland to show support for the territory after Trump’s recent warnings that he could take control of it. These developments highlight growing friction surrounding trade and territorial issues involving the US and its allies.
