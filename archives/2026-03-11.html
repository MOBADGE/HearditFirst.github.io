Major Evacuation in Dresden Over World War II Bomb

Dresden has conducted its largest evacuation in history after the discovery of a 250kg World War II British flying bomb near a collapsed bridge. Authorities carried out precautions to ensure public safety, reflecting ongoing risks from unexploded wartime ordnance found in populated areas decades later.

Canadian Political Shift as Fourth MP Joins Liberals

In Canadian politics, Prime Minister Mark Carney has moved closer to a parliamentary majority following the defection of Lori Idlout from the New Democratic Party (NDP) to the Liberals. This marks the fourth lawmaker to cross the floor during the current parliament, potentially reshaping the political balance and affecting legislative dynamics.

Upcoming Runoff Election in Georgia

A runoff election is scheduled for April 7th in Georgia to fill the seat left vacant by Marjorie Taylor Greene. Republican Clay Fuller and Democrat Shawn Harris are projected to compete.
