Evacuation in Dresden Due to WWII Bomb Discovery

Dresden authorities have ordered a large-scale evacuation, the largest in the city's history, after discovering a 250-kilogram British flying bomb from World War II near a collapsed bridge. The evacuation measures are aimed at safely defusing the bomb and preventing potential harm to residents. This event highlights ongoing challenges cities face in dealing with unexploded ordnance from past conflicts.
