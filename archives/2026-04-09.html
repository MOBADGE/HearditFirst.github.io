Nature and Wildlife

Allan was celebrated for his contribution to nature documentaries, winning eight Emmy Awards for his work, including on the acclaimed Blue Planet series alongside Sir David Attenborough. His passing marks the loss of a significant figure in wildlife filmmaking, known for bringing the natural world into homes around the globe through stunning visuals.
