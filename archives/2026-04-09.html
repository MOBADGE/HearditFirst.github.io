Tribute to Wildlife Cameraman Doug Allan

Renowned wildlife cameraman and photographer Doug Allan has passed away in Nepal. Allan earned eight Emmy Awards for his work on celebrated series such as Blue Planet, collaborating with figures like Sir David Attenborough. His pioneering techniques and captivating storytelling significantly contributed to wildlife filmmaking, inspiring a generation of viewers and professionals. His death marks the loss of one of the field’s most influential figures.
