Environment and Energy

Argentina has passed a bill loosening protections on its glaciers. The legislation will make it easier for provincial governments to approve mining projects in glacier regions. This marks a significant shift in environmental policy, potentially impacting glacier conservation efforts and raising concerns about the balance between economic development and environmental protection. Additionally, petrol and diesel prices have risen again, with motoring groups warning that drivers should not expect significant relief soon. The increase has fueled worries related to the broader impact of geopolitical stability on energy costs.

The BBC’s Science Editor Rebecca Morelle conducted a live conversation with the crew ahead of their expected splashdown on Saturday, offering the public a closer look at space exploration progress. The mission highlights continued international interest and investment in lunar research.
