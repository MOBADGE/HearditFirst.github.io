Protests After Fatal Minneapolis Shooting

Protests erupted in Minneapolis following the shooting death of Alex Pretti, a 37-year-old nurse and U.S. citizen, who was shot by federal agents. Demonstrators gathered near the scene, chanting Pretti's name and expressing their grief and anger. The incident marks the second fatal shooting by federal agents in the city this month, raising concerns about law enforcement practices and community safety. The unrest reflects wider national debates on police use of force and accountability.
