Fatal Shooting of Alex Pretti in Minneapolis

Federal agents fatally shot Alex Pretti, a 37-year-old nurse and US citizen, in Minneapolis. Officials stated that the agents acted in self-defense during the incident, which has sparked protests in the city. Pretti's parents have strongly disputed the official account, calling it "sickening lies". This shooting marks the second time federal agents have fatally shot someone in Minneapolis this month, intensifying public outcry and demands for accountability. Videos capturing moments around the shooting have circulated, fueling ongoing debates about law enforcement actions in the city.
