Middle East Conflict Escalates

Recent conflict in the Middle East intensified after US and Israeli air strikes targeted locations across Iran on March 1, 2026. Iranian state television reported a strike on a school resulting in at least 148 deaths. Iran blamed the United States and Israel for the attack. The US is currently investigating the incident, while Israel’s Defense Forces said they are not aware of any operations in that area. In the chaos of these strikes, Iran's supreme leader was reported killed, ending his long-standing hold on power.

Iran’s Retaliation and Regional Impact

In response to the US and Israeli attacks, Iran launched missiles and drones toward Israel and four Gulf Arab countries that host US military bases. The retaliatory strikes caused debris from intercepted Iranian drones and missiles to fall over airports in Dubai and Abu Dhabi, leading to the death of one person and injuries to 11 others, as well as widespread damage. This escalation increases tensions across the Gulf region and raises concerns about further conflict involving multiple countries.
