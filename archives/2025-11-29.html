Aviation Safety Concerns Lead to Grounding of Planes

Airbus has identified a potential safety issue affecting around 6,000 planes related to solar radiation interference with onboard systems. As a precaution, these aircraft have been grounded until they undergo an urgent software update or have their computer systems replaced. This move is aimed at preventing any possible failures during flight operations due to the detected risk. The update process is expected to take some time and could impact flight schedules in the near term.
