Conflict and Protests in Cyprus

Tensions in Cyprus have increased following a drone strike on the RAF Akrotiri airbase, a UK military installation on the island. The attack has sparked protests under the slogan "British Bases Out," with demonstrators calling for the removal of the UK's military presence. The situation highlights ongoing local discontent about foreign bases in Cyprus and raises questions about the security of such sites amid broader regional conflicts.

Investigation Underway After Oslo Embassy Explosion

Norwegian police are investigating an explosion at the US embassy in Oslo that caused minor damage to the building. Authorities have said terrorism is among the possible motives but are also exploring other reasons behind the blast. The investigation is ongoing, with officials providing limited details as they gather more information.

Deadly Tornadoes Strike US States

Tornadoes in Michigan and Oklahoma have caused significant damage and loss of life, with at least six people reported dead, including a 12-year-old boy. The storms uprooted trees, downed power lines, damaged buildings, and removed roofs from houses. Emergency efforts continue in the affected areas as communities respond to the destruction and work on recovery.

Nepal’s Election Results Surprise

In Nepal’s general election, rapper-politician Balendra Shah has unseated the country's former prime minister. This election came after violent youth-led protests in September led to the government's collapse. Shah’s victory signals a notable political shift, reflecting changing public sentiment and the rise of new voices in Nepalese politics.

Swiss Voters Maintain Public Broadcasting Fee

Swiss voters have rejected a right-wing initiative to reduce the licence fee for public broadcasting. Early projections show that over 60% of voters supported keeping the current fee levels. This decision maintains funding for public media services, which are an established part of Switzerland’s media landscape.

Ongoing Search for Missing TV Presenter’s Mother

The disappearance of Nancy Guthrie, mother of US TV presenter Savannah Guthrie, remains unresolved as the search enters a new month. Police have not made any arrests, and the case continues to draw public attention. Authorities have provided few updates, keeping the investigation active as they seek new leads.
