International Security and Conflict

Tensions involving Iran continue to draw global attention. Prediction markets have seen millions of dollars wagered on various outcomes related to the war in Iran, prompting calls for stricter regulation of these markets. Cultural Shifts in Chess

Women are playing a key role in changing the perception of chess, traditionally seen as an "old man's game." Through fast-paced "bullet" matches and engaging viral videos, female content creators are bringing chess into the digital age. Their efforts help make the game more accessible and appealing to a younger, more diverse audience.
