Severe Flooding in Kenya

Kenya is dealing with severe flooding that has claimed 62 lives amid continuing heavy rains. In Nairobi, rescue teams saved eleven people overnight when a minibus taxi became trapped in rising floodwaters. The flooding has caused widespread disruption and loss of life, highlighting the challenges posed by extreme weather events in the region. Efforts to provide aid and improve flood management may become priorities as the rains continue.
