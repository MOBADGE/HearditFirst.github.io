Deadly Plane Crash in Bolivia

At least 15 people died after a military plane carrying banknotes crashed in Bolivia. The incident caused chaos as crowds tried to seize the banknotes scattered near the crash site. Police responded by firing tear gas to control the situation. Authorities are investigating the circumstances of the crash and managing the aftermath to prevent further unrest.

The move highlights ongoing tensions over AI regulation and government contracts in this rapidly evolving sector.

Canada and India Work to Strengthen Relations

Increased Charges in Minnesota Anti-ICE Protest

The US Department of Justice has charged 30 additional people connected to a protest against the Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) agency at a Minnesota church. Among those initially arrested was former CNN anchor Don Lemon. The protest and subsequent legal actions reflect ongoing debates and tensions surrounding immigration policies and law enforcement tactics in the United States.
