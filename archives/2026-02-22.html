Venezuela Political Prisoners Seek Amnesty

More than 1,500 political prisoners in Venezuela have applied for amnesty, according to the head of the country’s National Assembly. This development follows the recent capture of former President Nicolás Maduro. The move comes amid increasing pressure from the United States on Venezuela’s government. The large number of applications highlights the ongoing political tensions in Venezuela and may influence future negotiations or reforms within the country.

Deadly Avalanche in Lake Tahoe

The final missing skier from a recent Lake Tahoe avalanche has been found dead, bringing the total confirmed deaths to nine. The victims include six women and three guides, marking this tragedy as the worst avalanche in the history of the state. Authorities have released the names of those who died. This event raises attention to avalanche safety and the risks faced by winter sports enthusiasts in mountainous areas.

Legendary Musician Willie Colón Passes Away

Willie Colón, a pioneering trombonist in salsa music, has died at the age of 75. His career spanned six decades and included dozens of albums. He was widely recognized as one of the most influential Latino artists. Colón’s passing is a significant loss to the music community, celebrating a legacy that shaped salsa and Latin music worldwide.

Football Empowers Girls Against Forced Marriage in India

In a village in India, two sisters have shared how playing football has transformed their lives and helped them resist child marriage pressures. Football has become more than a sport for these girls, serving as a tool for empowerment and social change. This story sheds light on how sports can contribute to fighting social issues such as forced marriage by giving young women confidence and a stronger voice in their communities.

Implementation of Project 2025 Policies Under Trump

Observers note that about half of the policies outlined in Project 2025 have been implemented during the first year of Donald Trump’s second term. The roadmap includes a range of measures, from immigration crackdowns to other domestic policies. The extent of these policies’ rollout demonstrates the administration’s commitment to the agenda set by Project 2025 and signals potential for further changes in the coming years.
