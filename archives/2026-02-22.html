This move comes amid increased pressure from the United States following the capture of ex-President Nicolás Maduro, signaling possible shifts in Venezuela’s political landscape and judicial processes.

Notable Deaths and Human Interest Stories

His six-decade career left a lasting impact on Latin music. In sports, the Minnesota Vikings have paid tribute to Rondale Moore, a 25-year-old wide receiver who recently died, marking a tragic loss for the football community. Social Change through Sports

In India, football is proving to be a powerful tool for social change. Two sisters from a village shared how playing football has helped them resist forced child marriage, demonstrating how sports can contribute to empowering girls and challenging traditional practices.
