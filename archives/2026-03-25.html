Politics: Danish Election Results and Philippine Impeachment Hearings

In Denmark, the Prime Minister's party secured the most votes in the recent election but failed to achieve a majority. Energy and Economy: Philippines Energy Emergency and Global Recession Warning

The Philippines has declared an energy emergency amid concerns about oil supplies. President Marcos announced the plan to procure one million barrels of oil to bolster national reserves, aiming to stabilize the country's energy situation.

On a global scale, Larry Fink, head of the financial firm BlackRock, issued a warning about the economic risks of sustained high oil prices. He cautioned that if oil prices reach $150 per barrel and remain elevated, it could trigger a worldwide recession with significant economic implications.
