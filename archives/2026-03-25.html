Global Politics

The Philippines’ vice president, Sara Duterte, is currently facing impeachment hearings that have intensified ahead of her expected 2028 presidential campaign. The proceedings add political pressure as she navigates this challenging phase. Reports noted she was under the influence of MDMA and alcohol during the incident. Human Rights and Tragedy

A disturbing discovery in Kenya has brought international attention, as a mass grave containing 32 bodies was uncovered. Most of the bodies were infants and came from local hospitals and mortuaries, raising serious questions about the circumstances and prompting calls for investigation.

Economy and Business

Larry Fink, head of financial services company BlackRock, issued a warning about the global economy, citing potential severe consequences if oil prices hit and remain at $150 per barrel. He highlighted the risk of a worldwide recession under sustained high oil prices, which would have significant implications for markets and economic stability.

Technology and Entertainment

OpenAI has ended its partnership with Disney by closing the AI-powered video-making app Sora, less than two years after its launch. The app had caused a stir in the media industry by demonstrating new capabilities in AI-generated video content. On a lighter note, the globally popular K-pop band BTS held a comeback concert streamed on Netflix, drawing 18.4 million viewers worldwide. This event marked the group’s first performance together since October 2022 and was celebrated by fans everywhere.

US Politics

In a notable political shift, Democrat Emily Gregory has won a Florida state legislative seat that includes former US president Donald Trump’s Mar-a-Lago estate. The district had favored a Republican candidate by a 19-point margin in 2024, making Gregory’s victory a significant change in voter sentiment in that area.
