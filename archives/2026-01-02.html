Tragic Fire in Swiss Bar Under Investigation

Authorities are investigating a deadly fire in a Swiss bar that killed 40 people. Officials suspect the blaze may have started due to sparklers placed on champagne bottles. They have not ruled out criminal charges as part of their inquiry. Meanwhile, families of teenagers believed to have been at the bar are urgently seeking news about their missing loved ones. Some of the missing are as young as 15, and relatives describe the situation as a nightmare as they wait for information.
