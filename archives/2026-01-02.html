Yemen Conflict Escalates with Rival Gulf Support

Clashes in Yemen have intensified as forces backed by Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates confront each other. The two Gulf allies support opposing sides in the ongoing conflict, raising concerns about further division within the country. The escalating violence adds to the complex and unstable situation in Yemen, where different factions compete for control.

Missing Teens After Ski Resort Fire

Families of teenagers missing after a fire at a ski resort are urgently seeking news of their loved ones. Some of the young people, believed to be as young as 15, were thought to be at a bar when the fire occurred. The incident has caused distress among family members who continue to await information about the missing youths.

US Signals Intervention If Iranian Protesters Are Harmed

The United States has issued a warning of potential intervention following protests and clashes in Iran. The US president stated that American forces would "come to the rescue" if protesters were killed. This statement comes amid ongoing demonstrations in Iran and signals a willingness to get involved if violence escalates against those protesting.

Ukraine Appoints Spy Chief to Lead Presidential Office

Kyrylo Budanov, who has led Ukraine’s military intelligence service known for effective strikes against Russia, has been named head of the presidential office. This appointment places a key figure with military intelligence experience into a central political role as Ukraine continues to face security challenges.

China’s BYD Surpasses Tesla in Electric Vehicle Sales

Chinese automaker BYD has overtaken Tesla to become the world’s top seller of electric vehicles for the first time. This shift highlights the growing strength of Chinese companies in the global EV market and marks a significant change in industry rankings that may influence future competition and innovation.

Migrant Boat Sinks Off Gambia, Dozens Missing

A boat carrying around 200 migrants sank off the coast of Gambia, leaving dozens of people missing. The migrants were believed to be traveling one of the world’s most dangerous routes from West Africa to Spain. This tragedy underscores the ongoing risks faced by migrants attempting perilous journeys in search of better opportunities.
