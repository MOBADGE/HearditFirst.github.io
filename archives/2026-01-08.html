US Incident Raises Questions

International Diplomacy

Conflict and Power Outages in Ukraine

Russian attacks have caused widespread power outages in two southeastern Ukrainian regions: Dnipropetrovsk and Zaporizhzhia. Officials report that these areas were nearly completely without electricity, highlighting the continued impact of the conflict on civilian infrastructure and daily life.

Environmental Concerns in Australia
